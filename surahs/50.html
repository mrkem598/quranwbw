qaf قٓ Qaf wal-qur'āni وَالْقُرْءَانِ By the Quran l-majīdi الْمَجِيدِ the Glorious

1

1.

Qaf. By the honored Qur'an.

1

bal بَلْ Nay ʿajibū عَجِبُوا they wonder an أَنْ that jāahum جَاءَهُمْ has come to them mundhirun مُنْذِرٌ a warner min'hum مِنْهُمْ from them faqāla فَقَالَ So say l-kāfirūna الْكَافِرُونَ the disbelievers hādhā هَذَا This shayon شَيْءٌ (is) a thing ʿajībun عَجِيبٌ amazing

2

2.

But they wonder that there has come to them a warner from among themselves, and the disbelievers say, "This is an amazing thing.

2

a-idhā أَءِذَا What! When mit'nā مِتْنَا we die wakunnā وَكُنَّا and have become turāban تُرَابًا dust dhālika ذَلِكَ That rajʿun رَجْعٌ (is) a return baʿīdun بَعِيدٌ far

3

3.

When we have died and have become dust, [we will return to life]? That is a distant return."

3

qad قَدْ Certainly ʿalim'nā عَلِمْنَا We know mā مَا what tanquṣu تَنْقُصُ diminishes l-arḍu الْأَرْضُ the earth min'hum مِنْهُمْ of them waʿindanā وَعِنْدَنَا and with Us kitābun كِتَابٌ (is) a Book ḥafīẓun حَفِيظٌ guarded

4

4.

We know what the earth diminishes of them, and with Us is a retaining record.

4

bal بَلْ Nay kadhabū كَذَّبُوا they denied bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ the truth lammā لَمَّا when jāahum جَاءَهُمْ it came (to) them fahum فَهُمْ so they fī فِي (are) in amrin أَمْرٍ a state marījin مَرِيجٍ confused

5

5.

But they denied the truth when it came to them, so they are in a confused condition.

5

afalam أَفَلَمْ Then do not yanẓurū يَنْظُرُوا they look ilā إِلَى at l-samāi السَّمَاءِ the sky fawqahum فَوْقَهُمْ above them kayfa كَيْفَ how banaynāhā بَنَيْنَاهَا We structured it wazayyannāhā وَزَيَّنَّاهَا and adorned it wamā وَمَا and not lahā لَهَا for it min مِنْ any furūjin فُرُوجٍ rifts

6

6.

Have they not looked at the heaven above them - how We structured it and adorned it and [how] it has no rifts?

6

wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ And the earth madadnāhā مَدَدْنَاهَا We have spread it out wa-alqaynā وَأَلْقَيْنَا and cast fīhā فِيهَا therein rawāsiya رَوَاسِيَ firmly set mountains wa-anbatnā وَأَنْبَتْنَا and We made to grow fīhā فِيهَا therein min مِنْ of kulli كُلِّ every zawjin زَوْجٍ kind bahījin بَهِيجٍ beautiful

7

7.

And the earth - We spread it out and cast therein firmly set mountains and made grow therein [something] of every beautiful kind,

7

tabṣiratan تَبْصِرَةً Giving insight wadhik'rā وَذِكْرَى and a reminder likulli لِكُلِّ for every ʿabdin عَبْدٍ slave munībin مُنِيبٍ who turns

8

8.

Giving insight and a reminder for every servant who turns [to Allah].

8

wanazzalnā وَنَزَّلْنَا And We have sent down mina مِنَ from l-samāi السَّمَاءِ the sky māan مَاءً water mubārakan مُبَارَكًا blessed fa-anbatnā فَأَنْبَتْنَا then We made to grow bihi بِهِ thereby jannātin جَنَّاتٍ gardens waḥabba وَحَبَّ and grain l-ḥaṣīdi الْحَصِيدِ (for) the harvest

9

9.

And We have sent down blessed rain from the sky and made grow thereby gardens and grain from the harvest

9

wal-nakhla وَالنَّخْلَ And the palms trees bāsiqātin بَاسِقَاتٍ tall lahā لَهَا for it ṭalʿun طَلْعٌ (are) layers naḍīdun نَضِيدٌ arranged

10

10.

And lofty palm trees having fruit arranged in layers -

10

riz'qan رِزْقًا A provision lil'ʿibādi لِلْعِبَادِ for the slaves wa-aḥyaynā وَأَحْيَيْنَا and We give life bihi بِهِ therewith baldatan بَلْدَةً (to) a land maytan مَيْتًا dead kadhālika كَذَلِكَ Thus l-khurūju الْخُرُوجُ (will be) the coming forth

11

11.

As provision for the servants, and We have given life thereby to a dead land. Thus is the resurrection.

11

kadhabat كَذَّبَتْ Denied qablahum قَبْلَهُمْ before them qawmu قَوْمُ (the) people nūḥin نُوحٍ (of) Nuh wa-aṣḥābu وَأَصْحَابُ and (the) companions l-rasi الرَّسِّ (of) Ar-Raas wathamūdu وَثَمُودُ and Thamud

12

12.

The people of Noah denied before them, and the companions of the well and Thamud

12

waʿādun وَعَادٌ And Aad wafir'ʿawnu وَفِرْعَوْنُ and Firaun wa-ikh'wānu وَإِخْوَانُ and (the) brothers lūṭin لُوطٍ (of) Lut

13

13.

And 'Aad and Pharaoh and the brothers of Lot

13

wa-aṣḥābu وَأَصْحَابُ And (the) companions l-aykati الْأَيْكَةِ (of) the wood waqawmu وَقَوْمُ and (the) people tubbaʿin تُبَّعٍ (of) Tubba kullun كُلٌّ All kadhaba كَذَّبَ denied l-rusula الرُّسُلَ the Messengers faḥaqqa فَحَقَّ so was fulfilled waʿīdi وَعِيدِ My Threat

14

14.

And the companions of the thicket and the people of Tubba'. All denied the messengers, so My threat was justly fulfilled.

14

afaʿayīnā أَفَعَيِينَا Were We then tired bil-khalqi بِالْخَلْقِ with the creation l-awali الْأَوَّلِ the first bal بَلْ Nay hum هُمْ they fī فِي (are) in labsin لَبْسٍ doubt min مِنْ about khalqin خَلْقٍ a creation jadīdin جَدِيدٍ new

15

15.

Did We fail in the first creation? But they are in confusion over a new creation.

15

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly khalaqnā خَلَقْنَا We created l-insāna الْإِنْسَانَ man wanaʿlamu وَنَعْلَمُ and We know mā مَا what tuwaswisu تُوَسْوِسُ whispers bihi بِهِ to him nafsuhu نَفْسُهُ his soul wanaḥnu وَنَحْنُ and We aqrabu أَقْرَبُ (are) nearer ilayhi إِلَيْهِ to him min مِنْ than ḥabli حَبْلِ (his) jugular vein l-warīdi الْوَرِيدِ (his) jugular vein

16

16.

And We have already created man and know what his soul whispers to him, and We are closer to him than [his] jugular vein

16

idh إِذْ When yatalaqqā يَتَلَقَّى receive l-mutalaqiyāni الْمُتَلَقِّيَانِ the two receivers ʿani عَنِ on l-yamīni الْيَمِينِ the right waʿani وَعَنِ and on l-shimāli الشِّمَالِ the left qaʿīdun قَعِيدٌ seated

17

17.

When the two receivers receive, seated on the right and on the left.

17

mā مَا Not yalfiẓu يَلْفِظُ he utters min مِنْ any qawlin قَوْلٍ word illā إِلَّا but ladayhi لَدَيْهِ with him raqībun رَقِيبٌ (is) an observer ʿatīdun عَتِيدٌ ready

18

18.

Man does not utter any word except that with him is an observer prepared [to record].

18

wajāat وَجَاءَتْ And will come sakratu سَكْرَةُ (the) stupor l-mawti الْمَوْتِ (of) death bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in truth dhālika ذَلِكَ That mā مَا (is) what kunta كُنْتَ you were min'hu مِنْهُ [from it] taḥīdu تَحِيدُ avoiding

19

19.

And the intoxication of death will bring the truth; that is what you were trying to avoid.

19

wanufikha وَنُفِخَ And will be blown fī فِي [in] l-ṣūri الصُّورِ the trumpet dhālika ذَلِكَ That yawmu يَوْمُ (is the) Day l-waʿīdi الْوَعِيدِ (of) the Warning

20

20.

And the Horn will be blown. That is the Day of [carrying out] the threat.

20

wajāat وَجَاءَتْ And will come kullu كُلُّ every nafsin نَفْسٍ soul maʿahā مَعَهَا with it sāiqun سَائِقٌ a driver washahīdun وَشَهِيدٌ and a witness

21

21.

And every soul will come, with it a driver and a witness.

21

laqad لَقَدْ Certainly kunta كُنْتَ you were fī فِي in ghaflatin غَفْلَةٍ heedlessness min مِنْ of hādhā هَذَا this fakashafnā فَكَشَفْنَا So We have removed ʿanka عَنْكَ from you ghiṭāaka غِطَاءَكَ your cover fabaṣaruka فَبَصَرُكَ so your sight l-yawma الْيَوْمَ today ḥadīdun حَدِيدٌ (is) sharp

22

22.

[It will be said], "You were certainly in unmindfulness of this, and We have removed from you your cover, so your sight, this Day, is sharp."

22

waqāla وَقَالَ And (will) say qarīnuhu قَرِينُهُ his companion hādhā هَذَا This mā مَا (is) what ladayya لَدَيَّ (is) with me ʿatīdun عَتِيدٌ ready

23

23.

And his companion, [the angel], will say, "This [record] is what is with me, prepared."

23

alqiyā أَلْقِيَا Throw fī فِي into jahannama جَهَنَّمَ Hell kulla كُلَّ every kaffārin كَفَّارٍ disbeliever ʿanīdin عَنِيدٍ stubborn

24

24.

[Allah will say], "Throw into Hell every obstinate disbeliever,

24

mannāʿin مَنَّاعٍ Forbidder lil'khayri لِلْخَيْرِ of good muʿ'tadin مُعْتَدٍ transgressor murībin مُرِيبٍ doubter

25

25.

Preventer of good, aggressor, and doubter,

25

alladhī الَّذِي Who jaʿala جَعَلَ made maʿa مَعَ with l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah ilāhan إِلَهًا a god ākhara ءَاخَرَ another fa-alqiyāhu فَأَلْقِيَاهُ so throw him fī فِي in(to) l-ʿadhābi الْعَذَابِ the punishment l-shadīdi الشَّدِيدِ the severe

26

26.

Who made [as equal] with Allah another deity; then throw him into the severe punishment."

26

qāla قَالَ Will say qarīnuhu قَرِينُهُ his companion rabbanā رَبَّنَا Our Lord mā مَا not aṭghaytuhu أَطْغَيْتُهُ I made him transgress walākin وَلَكِنْ but kāna كَانَ he was fī فِي in ḍalālin ضَلَالٍ error baʿīdin بَعِيدٍ far

27

27.

His [devil] companion will say, "Our Lord, I did not make him transgress, but he [himself] was in extreme error."

27

qāla قَالَ He will say lā لَا (Do) not takhtaṣimū تَخْتَصِمُوا dispute ladayya لَدَيَّ (in) My presence waqad وَقَدْ and indeed qaddamtu قَدَّمْتُ I sent forth ilaykum إِلَيْكُمْ to you bil-waʿīdi بِالْوَعِيدِ the Warning

28

28.

[Allah] will say, "Do not dispute before Me, while I had already presented to you the warning.

28

mā مَا Not yubaddalu يُبَدَّلُ will be changed l-qawlu الْقَوْلُ the word ladayya لَدَيَّ with Me wamā وَمَا and not anā أَنَا I Am biẓallāmin بِظَلَّامٍ unjust lil'ʿabīdi لِلْعَبِيدِ to My slaves

29

29.

The word will not be changed with Me, and never will I be unjust to the servants."

29

yawma يَوْمَ (The) Day naqūlu نَقُولُ We will say lijahannama لِجَهَنَّمَ to Hell hali هَلِ Are im'talati امْتَلَأْتِ you filled wataqūlu وَتَقُولُ And it will say hal هَلْ Are min مِنْ (there) any mazīdin مَزِيدٍ more

30

30.

On the Day We will say to Hell, "Have you been filled?" and it will say, "Are there some more,"

30

wa-uz'lifati وَأُزْلِفَتِ And will be brought near l-janatu الْجَنَّةُ the Paradise lil'muttaqīna لِلْمُتَّقِينَ to the righteous ghayra غَيْرَ not baʿīdin بَعِيدٍ far

31

31.

And Paradise will be brought near to the righteous, not far,

31

hādhā هَذَا This mā مَا (is) what tūʿadūna تُوعَدُونَ you were promised likulli لِكُلِّ for everyone awwābin أَوَّابٍ who turns ḥafīẓin حَفِيظٍ (and) who keeps

32

32.

[It will be said], "This is what you were promised - for every returner [to Allah] and keeper [of His covenant]

32

man مَنْ Who khashiya خَشِيَ feared l-raḥmāna الرَّحْمَنَ the Most Gracious bil-ghaybi بِالْغَيْبِ in the unseen wajāa وَجَاءَ and came biqalbin بِقَلْبٍ with a heart munībin مُنِيبٍ returning

33

33.

Who feared the Most Merciful unseen and came with a heart returning [in repentance].

33

ud'khulūhā ادْخُلُوهَا Enter it bisalāmin بِسَلَامٍ in peace dhālika ذَلِكَ That yawmu يَوْمُ (is) a Day l-khulūdi الْخُلُودِ (of) Eternity

34

34.

Enter it in peace. This is the Day of Eternity."

34

lahum لَهُمْ For them mā مَا whatever yashāūna يَشَاءُونَ they wish fīhā فِيهَا therein waladaynā وَلَدَيْنَا and with Us mazīdun مَزِيدٌ (is) more

35

35.

They will have whatever they wish therein, and with Us is more.

35

wakam وَكَمْ And how many ahlaknā أَهْلَكْنَا We destroyed qablahum قَبْلَهُمْ before them min مِنْ of qarnin قَرْنٍ a generation hum هُمْ they ashaddu أَشَدُّ (were) stronger min'hum مِنْهُمْ than them baṭshan بَطْشًا (in) power fanaqqabū فَنَقَّبُوا so they explored fī فِي throughout l-bilādi الْبِلَادِ the lands hal هَلْ Is (there) min مِنْ any maḥīṣin مَحِيصٍ place of escape

36

36.

And how many a generation before them did We destroy who were greater than them in [striking] power and had explored throughout the lands. Is there any place of escape?

36

inna إِنَّ Indeed fī فِي in dhālika ذَلِكَ that ladhik'rā لَذِكْرَى surely, is a reminder liman لِمَنْ for (one) who kāna كَانَ is lahu لَهُ for him qalbun قَلْبٌ a heart aw أَوْ or alqā أَلْقَى (who) gives ear l-samʿa السَّمْعَ (who) gives ear wahuwa وَهُوَ while he shahīdun شَهِيدٌ (is) a witness

37

37.

Indeed in that is a reminder for whoever has a heart or who listens while he is present [in mind].

37

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly khalaqnā خَلَقْنَا We created l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth wamā وَمَا and whatever baynahumā بَيْنَهُمَا (is) between both of them fī فِي in sittati سِتَّةِ six ayyāmin أَيَّامٍ periods wamā وَمَا and (did) not massanā مَسَّنَا touch Us min مِنْ any lughūbin لُغُوبٍ fatigue

38

38.

And We did certainly create the heavens and earth and what is between them in six days, and there touched Us no weariness.

38

fa-iṣ'bir فَاصْبِرْ So be patient ʿalā عَلَى over mā مَا what yaqūlūna يَقُولُونَ they say wasabbiḥ وَسَبِّحْ and glorify biḥamdi بِحَمْدِ (the) praise rabbika رَبِّكَ (of) your Lord qabla قَبْلَ before ṭulūʿi طُلُوعِ (the) rising l-shamsi الشَّمْسِ (of) the sun waqabla وَقَبْلَ and before l-ghurūbi الْغُرُوبِ the setting

39

39.
