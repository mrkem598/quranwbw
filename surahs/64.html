yusabbiḥu يُسَبِّحُ Glorifies lillahi لِلَّهِ [to] Allah mā مَا whatever fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wamā وَمَا and whatever fī فِي (is) in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth lahu لَهُ For Him l-mul'ku الْمُلْكُ (is the) dominion walahu وَلَهُ and for Him l-ḥamdu الْحَمْدُ (is) the praise wahuwa وَهُوَ And He ʿalā عَلَى (is) on kulli كُلِّ every shayin شَيْءٍ thing qadīrun قَدِيرٌ All-powerful

1

1.

Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth is exalting Allah. To Him belongs dominion, and to Him belongs [all] praise, and He is over all things competent.

1

huwa هُوَ He alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who khalaqakum خَلَقَكُمْ created you faminkum فَمِنْكُمْ and among you kāfirun كَافِرٌ (is) a disbeliever waminkum وَمِنْكُمْ and among you mu'minun مُؤْمِنٌ (is) a believer wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do baṣīrun بَصِيرٌ (is) All-Seer

2

2.

It is He who created you, and among you is the disbeliever, and among you is the believer. And Allah, of what you do, is Seeing.

2

khalaqa خَلَقَ He created l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ with truth waṣawwarakum وَصَوَّرَكُمْ and He formed you fa-aḥsana فَأَحْسَنَ and made good ṣuwarakum صُوَرَكُمْ your forms wa-ilayhi وَإِلَيْهِ and to Him l-maṣīru الْمَصِيرُ (is) the final return

3

3.

He created the heavens and earth in truth and formed you and perfected your forms; and to Him is the [final] destination.

3

yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ He knows mā مَا what fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth wayaʿlamu وَيَعْلَمُ and He knows mā مَا what tusirrūna تُسِرُّونَ you conceal wamā وَمَا and what tuʿ'linūna تُعْلِنُونَ you declare wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knowing bidhāti بِذَاتِ of what l-ṣudūri الصُّدُورِ (is in) the breasts

4

4.

He knows what is within the heavens and earth and knows what you conceal and what you declare. And Allah is Knowing of that within the breasts.

4

alam أَلَمْ Has not yatikum يَأْتِكُمْ come to you naba-u نَبَؤُا (the) news alladhīna الَّذِينَ (of) those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved min مِنْ from qablu قَبْلُ before fadhāqū فَذَاقُوا So they tasted wabāla وَبَالَ (the) bad consequence amrihim أَمْرِهِمْ (of) their affair walahum وَلَهُمْ and for them ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment alīmun أَلِيمٌ painful

5

5.

Has there not come to you the news of those who disbelieved before? So they tasted the bad consequence of their affair, and they will have a painful punishment.

5

dhālika ذَلِكَ That bi-annahu بِأَنَّهُ (is) because kānat كَانَتْ had tatīhim تَأْتِيهِمْ come to them rusuluhum رُسُلُهُمْ their Messengers bil-bayināti بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ with clear proofs faqālū فَقَالُوا but they said abasharun أَبَشَرٌ Shall human beings yahdūnanā يَهْدُونَنَا guide us fakafarū فَكَفَرُوا So they disbelieved watawallaw وَتَوَلَّوْا and turned away wa-is'taghnā وَاسْتَغْنَى And can do without them l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah ghaniyyun غَنِيٌّ (is) Self-sufficient ḥamīdun حَمِيدٌ Praiseworthy

6

6.

That is because their messengers used to come to them with clear evidences, but they said, "Shall human beings guide us?" and disbelieved and turned away. And Allah dispensed [with them]; and Allah is Free of need and Praiseworthy.

6

zaʿama زَعَمَ Claim alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve an أَنْ that lan لَنْ never yub'ʿathū يُبْعَثُوا will they be raised qul قُلْ Say balā بَلَى Yes warabbī وَرَبِّي by my Lord latub'ʿathunna لَتُبْعَثُنَّ surely you will be raised; thumma ثُمَّ then latunabba-unna لَتُنَبَّؤُنَّ surely you will be informed bimā بِمَا of what ʿamil'tum عَمِلْتُمْ you did wadhālika وَذَلِكَ And that ʿalā عَلَى for l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah yasīrun يَسِيرٌ (is) easy

7

7.

Those who disbelieve have claimed that they will never be resurrected. Say, "Yes, by my Lord, you will surely be resurrected; then you will surely be informed of what you did. And that, for Allah, is easy."

7

faāminū فَءَامِنُوا So believe bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah warasūlihi وَرَسُولِهِ and His Messenger wal-nūri وَالنُّورِ and the Light alladhī الَّذِي which anzalnā أَنْزَلْنَا We have sent down wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do khabīrun خَبِيرٌ (is) All-Aware

8

8.

So believe in Allah and His Messenger and the Qur'an which We have sent down. And Allah is Acquainted with what you do.

8

yawma يَوْمَ (The) Day yajmaʿukum يَجْمَعُكُمْ He will assemble you liyawmi لِيَوْمِ for (the) Day l-jamʿi الْجَمْعِ (of) the Assembly dhālika ذَلِكَ that yawmu يَوْمُ (will be the) Day l-taghābuni التَّغَابُنِ (of) mutual loss and gain waman وَمَنْ And whoever yu'min يُؤْمِنْ believes bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah wayaʿmal وَيَعْمَلْ and does ṣāliḥan صَالِحًا righteousness yukaffir يُكَفِّرْ He will remove ʿanhu عَنْهُ from him sayyiātihi سَيِّئَاتِهِ his misdeeds wayud'khil'hu وَيُدْخِلْهُ and He will admit him jannātin جَنَّاتٍ (to) Gardens tajrī تَجْرِي flow min مِنْ from taḥtihā تَحْتِهَا underneath it l-anhāru الْأَنْهَارُ the rivers khālidīna خَالِدِينَ abiding fīhā فِيهَا therein abadan أَبَدًا forever dhālika ذَلِكَ That l-fawzu الْفَوْزُ (is) the success l-ʿaẓīmu الْعَظِيمُ the great

9

9.

The Day He will assemble you for the Day of Assembly - that is the Day of Deprivation. And whoever believes in Allah and does righteousness - He will remove from him his misdeeds and admit him to gardens beneath which rivers flow, wherein they will abide forever. That is the great attainment.

9

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ But those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved wakadhabū وَكَذَّبُوا and denied biāyātinā بِءَايَاتِنَا [in] Our Verses ulāika أُولَئِكَ those aṣḥābu أَصْحَابُ (are the) companions l-nāri النَّارِ (of) the Fire khālidīna خَالِدِينَ abiding forever fīhā فِيهَا therein wabi'sa وَبِئْسَ And wretched is l-maṣīru الْمَصِيرُ the destination

10

10.

But the ones who disbelieved and denied Our verses - those are the companions of the Fire, abiding eternally therein; and wretched is the destination.

10

mā مَا Not aṣāba أَصَابَ strikes min مِنْ any muṣībatin مُصِيبَةٍ disaster illā إِلَّا except bi-idh'ni بِإِذْنِ by (the) permission l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah waman وَمَنْ And whoever yu'min يُؤْمِنْ believes bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah yahdi يَهْدِ He guides qalbahu قَلْبَهُ his heart wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bikulli بِكُلِّ of every shayin شَيْءٍ thing ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knowing

11

11.

No disaster strikes except by permission of Allah. And whoever believes in Allah - He will guide his heart. And Allah is Knowing of all things.

11

wa-aṭīʿū وَأَطِيعُوا So obey l-laha اللَّهَ Allah wa-aṭīʿū وَأَطِيعُوا and obey l-rasūla الرَّسُولَ the Messenger; fa-in فَإِنْ but if tawallaytum تَوَلَّيْتُمْ you turn away fa-innamā فَإِنَّمَا then only ʿalā عَلَى upon rasūlinā رَسُولِنَا Our Messenger l-balāghu الْبَلَاغُ (is) the conveyance l-mubīnu الْمُبِينُ clear

12

12.

And obey Allah and obey the Messenger; but if you turn away - then upon Our Messenger is only [the duty of] clear notification.

12

al-lahu اللَّهُ Allah lā لَا (there is) no ilāha إِلَهَ god illā إِلَّا except huwa هُوَ Him waʿalā وَعَلَى And upon l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah falyatawakkali فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ let put (their) trust l-mu'minūna الْمُؤْمِنُونَ the believers

13

13.

Allah - there is no deity except Him. And upon Allah let the believers rely.

13

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O alladhīna الَّذِينَ (you) who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe inna إِنَّ Indeed min مِنْ from azwājikum أَزْوَاجِكُمْ your spouses wa-awlādikum وَأَوْلَادِكُمْ and your children ʿaduwwan عَدُوًّا (are) enemies lakum لَكُمْ to you fa-iḥ'dharūhum فَاحْذَرُوهُمْ so beware of them wa-in وَإِنْ But if taʿfū تَعْفُوا you pardon wataṣfaḥū وَتَصْفَحُوا and overlook wataghfirū وَتَغْفِرُوا and forgive fa-inna فَإِنَّ then indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ghafūrun غَفُورٌ (is) Oft-Forgiving raḥīmun رَحِيمٌ Most Merciful

14

14.

O you who have believed, indeed, among your wives and your children are enemies to you, so beware of them. But if you pardon and overlook and forgive - then indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

14

innamā إِنَّمَا Only amwālukum أَمْوَالُكُمْ your wealth wa-awlādukum وَأَوْلَادُكُمْ and your children fit'natun فِتْنَةٌ (are) a trial wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ and Allah ʿindahu عِنْدَهُ with Him ajrun أَجْرٌ (is) a reward ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

15

15.

Your wealth and your children are but a trial, and Allah has with Him a great reward.

15

fa-ittaqū فَاتَّقُوا So fear l-laha اللَّهَ Allah mā مَا as much as is'taṭaʿtum اسْتَطَعْتُمْ you are able wa-is'maʿū وَاسْمَعُوا and listen wa-aṭīʿū وَأَطِيعُوا and obey wa-anfiqū وَأَنْفِقُوا and spend; khayran خَيْرًا (it is) better li-anfusikum لِأَنْفُسِكُمْ for your souls waman وَمَنْ And whoever yūqa يُوقَ is saved shuḥḥa شُحَّ (from the) greediness nafsihi نَفْسِهِ (of) his soul fa-ulāika فَأُولَئِكَ then those humu هُمُ [they] l-muf'liḥūna الْمُفْلِحُونَ (are) the successful ones

16

16.

So fear Allah as much as you are able and listen and obey and spend [in the way of Allah]; it is better for your selves. And whoever is protected from the stinginess of his soul - it is those who will be the successful.

16

in إِنْ If tuq'riḍū تُقْرِضُوا you loan l-laha اللَّهَ (to) Allah qarḍan قَرْضًا a loan ḥasanan حَسَنًا goodly yuḍāʿif'hu يُضَاعِفْهُ He will multiply it lakum لَكُمْ for you wayaghfir وَيَغْفِرْ and will forgive lakum لَكُمْ you wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah shakūrun شَكُورٌ (is) Most Appreciative ḥalīmun حَلِيمٌ Most Forbearing

17

17.

If you loan Allah a goodly loan, He will multiply it for you and forgive you. And Allah is Most Appreciative and Forbearing.

17

ʿālimu عَالِمُ (The) Knower l-ghaybi الْغَيْبِ (of) the unseen wal-shahādati وَالشَّهَادَةِ and the witnessed l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ the All-Mighty l-ḥakīmu الْحَكِيمُ the All-Wise

18

18.

Knower of the unseen and the witnessed, the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

18
