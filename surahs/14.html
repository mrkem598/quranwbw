alif-Lam-ra الٓر Alif Laam Ra kitābun كِتَابٌ A Book anzalnāhu أَنْزَلْنَاهُ which We have revealed ilayka إِلَيْكَ to you litukh'rija لِتُخْرِجَ so that you may bring out l-nāsa النَّاسَ the mankind mina مِنَ from l-ẓulumāti الظُّلُمَاتِ the darkness[es] ilā إِلَى to l-nūri النُّورِ the light bi-idh'ni بِإِذْنِ by the permission rabbihim رَبِّهِمْ (of) their Lord ilā إِلَى to ṣirāṭi صِرَاطِ the Path l-ʿazīzi الْعَزِيزِ (of) the All-Mighty l-ḥamīdi الْحَمِيدِ the Praiseworthy

1

1.

Alif, Lam, Ra. [This is] a Book which We have revealed to you, [O Muhammad], that you might bring mankind out of darknesses into the light by permission of their Lord - to the path of the Exalted in Might, the Praiseworthy -

1

al-lahi اللَّهِ Allah alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One lahu لَهُ to Him (belongs) mā مَا whatever fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wamā وَمَا and whatever fī فِي (is) in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth wawaylun وَوَيْلٌ And woe lil'kāfirīna لِلْكَافِرِينَ to the disbelievers min مِنْ from ʿadhābin عَذَابٍ the punishment shadīdin شَدِيدٍ severe

2

2.

Allah, to whom belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth. And woe to the disbelievers from a severe punishment

2

alladhīna الَّذِينَ Those who yastaḥibbūna يَسْتَحِبُّونَ love more l-ḥayata الْحَيَوةَ the life l-dun'yā الدُّنْيَا (of) the world ʿalā عَلَى than l-ākhirati الْءَاخِرَةِ the Hereafter wayaṣuddūna وَيَصُدُّونَ and hinder ʿan عَنْ from sabīli سَبِيلِ (the) Path l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah wayabghūnahā وَيَبْغُونَهَا and seek in it ʿiwajan عِوَجًا crookedness ulāika أُولَئِكَ those fī فِي [in] ḍalālin ضَلَالٍ (are) far astray baʿīdin بَعِيدٍ (are) far astray

3

3.

The ones who prefer the worldly life over the Hereafter and avert [people] from the way of Allah, seeking to make it (seem) deviant. Those are in extreme error.

3

wamā وَمَا And not arsalnā أَرْسَلْنَا We sent min مِنْ any rasūlin رَسُولٍ Messenger illā إِلَّا except bilisāni بِلِسَانِ with the language qawmihi قَوْمِهِ (of) his people liyubayyina لِيُبَيِّنَ so that he might make clear lahum لَهُمْ for them fayuḍillu فَيُضِلُّ Then Allah lets go astray l-lahu اللَّهُ Then Allah lets go astray man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills wayahdī وَيَهْدِي and guides man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills wahuwa وَهُوَ And He l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ (is) the All-Mighty l-ḥakīmu الْحَكِيمُ the All-Wise

4

4.

And We did not send any messenger except [speaking] in the language of his people to state clearly for them, and Allah sends astray [thereby] whom He wills and guides whom He wills. And He is the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

4

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And verily arsalnā أَرْسَلْنَا We sent mūsā مُوسَى Musa biāyātinā بِءَايَاتِنَا with Our Signs an أَنْ that akhrij أَخْرِجْ Bring out qawmaka قَوْمَكَ your people mina مِنَ from l-ẓulumāti الظُّلُمَاتِ the darkness[es] ilā إِلَى to l-nūri النُّورِ the light wadhakkir'hum وَذَكِّرْهُمْ And remind them bi-ayyāmi بِأَيَّامِ of the days l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah inna إِنَّ Indeed fī فِي in dhālika ذَلِكَ that laāyātin لَءَايَاتٍ surely (are) the signs likulli لِكُلِّ for everyone ṣabbārin صَبَّارٍ patient shakūrin شَكُورٍ and thankful

5

5.

And We certainly sent Moses with Our signs, [saying], "Bring out your people from darknesses into the light and remind them of the days of Allah." Indeed in that are signs for everyone patient and grateful.

5

wa-idh وَإِذْ And when qāla قَالَ said mūsā مُوسَى Musa liqawmihi لِقَوْمِهِ to his people udh'kurū اذْكُرُوا Remember niʿ'mata نِعْمَةَ (the) Favor of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (the) Favor of Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you idh إِذْ when anjākum أَنْجَاكُمْ He saved you min مِنْ from āli ءَالِ (the) people fir'ʿawna فِرْعَوْنَ (of) Firaun yasūmūnakum يَسُومُونَكُمْ they were afflicting you sūa سُوءَ (with) evil l-ʿadhābi الْعَذَابِ torment wayudhabbiḥūna وَيُذَبِّحُونَ and were slaughtering abnāakum أَبْنَاءَكُمْ your sons wayastaḥyūna وَيَسْتَحْيُونَ and letting live nisāakum نِسَاءَكُمْ your women wafī وَفِي And in dhālikum ذَلِكُمْ that balāon بَلَاءٌ (was) a trial min مِنْ from rabbikum رَبِّكُمْ your Lord ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

6

6.

And [recall, O Children of Israel], when Moses said to His people, "Remember the favor of Allah upon you when He saved you from the people of Pharaoh, who were afflicting you with the worst torment and were slaughtering your [newborn] sons and keeping your females alive. And in that was a great trial from your Lord.

6

wa-idh وَإِذْ And when ta-adhana تَأَذَّنَ proclaimed rabbukum رَبُّكُمْ your Lord la-in لَئِنْ If shakartum شَكَرْتُمْ you are thankful la-azīdannakum لَأَزِيدَنَّكُمْ surely I will increase you wala-in وَلَئِنْ but if kafartum كَفَرْتُمْ you are ungrateful inna إِنَّ indeed ʿadhābī عَذَابِي My punishment lashadīdun لَشَدِيدٌ (is) surely severe

7

7.

And [remember] when your Lord proclaimed, 'If you are grateful, I will surely increase you [in favor]; but if you deny, indeed, My punishment is severe.' "

7

waqāla وَقَالَ And said mūsā مُوسَى Musa in إِنْ If takfurū تَكْفُرُوا you disbelieve antum أَنْتُمْ you waman وَمَنْ and whoever fī فِي (is) in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth jamīʿan جَمِيعًا all fa-inna فَإِنَّ then indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah laghaniyyun لَغَنِيٌّ certainly (is) Free of need ḥamīdun حَمِيدٌ Praiseworthy

8

8.

And Moses said, "If you should disbelieve, you and whoever is on the earth entirely - indeed, Allah is Free of need and Praiseworthy."

8

alam أَلَمْ Has not yatikum يَأْتِكُمْ come to you naba-u نَبَؤُا (the) news alladhīna الَّذِينَ (of) those who min مِنْ (were) before you qablikum قَبْلِكُمْ (were) before you qawmi قَوْمِ the people nūḥin نُوحٍ of Nuh waʿādin وَعَادٍ and Aad wathamūda وَثَمُودَ and Thamud wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ and those who min مِنْ (were) after them baʿdihim بَعْدِهِمْ (were) after them lā لَا None yaʿlamuhum يَعْلَمُهُمْ knows them illā إِلَّا except l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah jāathum جَاءَتْهُمْ Came to them rusuluhum رُسُلُهُمْ their Messengers bil-bayināti بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ with clear proofs faraddū فَرَدُّوا but they returned aydiyahum أَيْدِيَهُمْ their hands fī فِي in afwāhihim أَفْوَاهِهِمْ their mouths waqālū وَقَالُوا and they said innā إِنَّا indeed, we kafarnā كَفَرْنَا [we] disbelieve bimā بِمَا in what ur'sil'tum أُرْسِلْتُمْ you have been sent bihi بِهِ with [it] wa-innā وَإِنَّا and indeed, we lafī لَفِي (are) surely in shakkin شَكٍّ doubt mimmā مِمَّا about what tadʿūnanā تَدْعُونَنَا you invite us ilayhi إِلَيْهِ to it murībin مُرِيبٍ suspicious

9

9.

Has there not reached you the news of those before you - the people of Noah and 'Aad and Thamud and those after them? No one knows them but Allah. Their messengers brought them clear proofs, but they returned their hands to their mouths and said, "Indeed, we disbelieve in that with which you have been sent, and indeed we are, about that to which you invite us, in disquieting doubt."

9

qālat قَالَتْ Said rusuluhum رُسُلُهُمْ their Messengers afī أَفِي Can (there) be about l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah shakkun شَكٌّ any doubt fāṭiri فَاطِرِ (the) Creator l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth yadʿūkum يَدْعُوكُمْ He invites you liyaghfira لِيَغْفِرَ so that He may forgive lakum لَكُمْ for you min مِنْ [of] dhunūbikum ذُنُوبِكُمْ your sins wayu-akhirakum وَيُؤَخِّرَكُمْ and give you respite ilā إِلَى for ajalin أَجَلٍ a term musamman مُسَمًّى appointed qālū قَالُوا They said in إِنْ Not antum أَنْتُمْ you illā إِلَّا (are) but basharun بَشَرٌ a human mith'lunā مِثْلُنَا like us turīdūna تُرِيدُونَ you wish an أَنْ to taṣuddūnā تَصُدُّونَا hinder us ʿammā عَمَّا from what kāna كَانَ used to yaʿbudu يَعْبُدُ worship ābāunā ءَابَاؤُنَا our forefathers fatūnā فَأْتُونَا So bring us bisul'ṭānin بِسُلْطَانٍ an authority mubīnin مُبِينٍ clear

10

10.

Their messengers said, "Can there be doubt about Allah, Creator of the heavens and earth? He invites you that He may forgive you of your sins, and He delays your death for a specified term." They said, "You are not but men like us who wish to avert us from what our fathers were worshipping. So bring us a clear authority."

10

qālat قَالَتْ Said lahum لَهُمْ to them rusuluhum رُسُلُهُمْ their Messengers in إِنْ Not naḥnu نَحْنُ we (are) illā إِلَّا but basharun بَشَرٌ a human mith'lukum مِثْلُكُمْ like you walākinna وَلَكِنَّ but l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yamunnu يَمُنُّ bestows His Grace ʿalā عَلَى on man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills min مِنْ of ʿibādihi عِبَادِهِ His slaves wamā وَمَا And not kāna كَانَ is lanā لَنَا for us an أَنْ that natiyakum نَأْتِيَكُمْ we bring you bisul'ṭānin بِسُلْطَانٍ an authority illā إِلَّا except bi-idh'ni بِإِذْنِ by the permission of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ by the permission of Allah waʿalā وَعَلَى And upon l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah falyatawakkali فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ so let put (their) trust l-mu'minūna الْمُؤْمِنُونَ the believers

11

11.

Their messengers said to them, "We are only men like you, but Allah confers favor upon whom He wills of His servants. It has never been for us to bring you evidence except by permission of Allah. And upon Allah let the believers rely.

11

wamā وَمَا And what lanā لَنَا (is) for us allā أَلَّا that not natawakkala نَتَوَكَّلَ we put our trust ʿalā عَلَى upon l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah waqad وَقَدْ while certainly hadānā هَدَانَا He has guided us subulanā سُبُلَنَا to our ways walanaṣbiranna وَلَنَصْبِرَنَّ And surely we will bear with patience ʿalā عَلَى on mā مَا what ādhaytumūnā ءَاذَيْتُمُونَا harm you may cause us waʿalā وَعَلَى And upon l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah falyatawakkali فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ so let put (their) trust l-mutawakilūna الْمُتَوَكِّلُونَ the ones who put (their) trust

12

12.

And why should we not rely upon Allah while He has guided us to our [good] ways. And we will surely be patient against whatever harm you should cause us. And upon Allah let those who would rely [indeed] rely."

12

waqāla وَقَالَ And said alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved lirusulihim لِرُسُلِهِمْ to their Messengers lanukh'rijannakum لَنُخْرِجَنَّكُمْ Surely we will drive you out min مِنْ of arḍinā أَرْضِنَا our land aw أَوْ or lataʿūdunna لَتَعُودُنَّ surely you should return fī فِي to millatinā مِلَّتِنَا our religion fa-awḥā فَأَوْحَى So inspired ilayhim إِلَيْهِمْ to them rabbuhum رَبُّهُمْ their Lord lanuh'likanna لَنُهْلِكَنَّ We will surely destroy l-ẓālimīna الظَّالِمِينَ the wrongdoers

13

13.

And those who disbelieved said to their messengers, "We will surely drive you out of our land, or you must return to our religion." So their Lord inspired to them, "We will surely destroy the wrongdoers.

13

walanus'kinannakumu وَلَنُسْكِنَنَّكُمُ And surely We will make you dwell l-arḍa الْأَرْضَ (in) the land min مِنْ after them baʿdihim بَعْدِهِمْ after them dhālika ذَلِكَ That liman لِمَنْ (is) for whoever khāfa خَافَ fears maqāmī مَقَامِي standing before Me wakhāfa وَخَافَ and fears waʿīdi وَعِيدِ My Threat

14

14.

And We will surely cause you to dwell in the land after them. That is for he who fears My position and fears My threat."

14

wa-is'taftaḥū وَاسْتَفْتَحُوا And they sought victory wakhāba وَخَابَ and disappointed kullu كُلُّ every jabbārin جَبَّارٍ tyrant ʿanīdin عَنِيدٍ obstinate

15

15.

And they requested victory from Allah, and disappointed, [therefore], was every obstinate tyrant.

15

min مِنْ Ahead of him warāihi وَرَائِهِ Ahead of him jahannamu جَهَنَّمُ (is) Hell wayus'qā وَيُسْقَى and he will be made to drink min مِنْ of māin مَاءٍ water ṣadīdin صَدِيدٍ purulent

16

16.

Before him is Hell, and he will be given a drink of purulent water.

16

yatajarraʿuhu يَتَجَرَّعُهُ He will sip it walā وَلَا but not yakādu يَكَادُ he will be near yusīghuhu يُسِيغُهُ (to) swallowing it wayatīhi وَيَأْتِيهِ And will come to him l-mawtu الْمَوْتُ the death min مِنْ from kulli كُلِّ every makānin مَكَانٍ side wamā وَمَا but not huwa هُوَ he bimayyitin بِمَيِّتٍ will die wamin وَمِنْ And ahead of him warāihi وَرَائِهِ And ahead of him ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment ghalīẓun غَلِيظٌ harsh

17

17.

He will gulp it but will hardly [be able to] swallow it. And death will come to him from everywhere, but he is not to die. And before him is a massive punishment.

17

mathalu مَثَلُ (The) example alladhīna الَّذِينَ (of) those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve birabbihim بِرَبِّهِمْ in their Lord aʿmāluhum أَعْمَالُهُمْ their deeds karamādin كَرَمَادٍ (are) like ashes ish'taddat اشْتَدَّتْ blows furiously bihi بِهِ on it l-rīḥu الرِّيحُ the wind fī فِي in yawmin يَوْمٍ a day ʿāṣifin عَاصِفٍ stormy lā لَا No yaqdirūna يَقْدِرُونَ control (they have) mimmā مِمَّا of what kasabū كَسَبُوا they have earned ʿalā عَلَى on shayin شَيْءٍ anything dhālika ذَلِكَ That huwa هُوَ [it] l-ḍalālu الضَّلَالُ (is) the straying l-baʿīdu الْبَعِيدُ far
