ha-meem حمٓ Ha Meem

1

1.

Ha, Meem.

1

wal-kitābi وَالْكِتَابِ By the Book l-mubīni الْمُبِينِ the clear

2

2.

By the clear Book,

2

innā إِنَّا Indeed We anzalnāhu أَنْزَلْنَاهُ revealed it fī فِي in laylatin لَيْلَةٍ a Night mubārakatin مُبَارَكَةٍ Blessed innā إِنَّا Indeed We kunnā كُنَّا [We] are mundhirīna مُنْذِرِينَ (ever) warning

3

3.

Indeed, We sent it down during a blessed night. Indeed, We were to warn [mankind].

3

fīhā فِيهَا Therein yuf'raqu يُفْرَقُ is made distinct kullu كُلُّ every amrin أَمْرٍ affair ḥakīmin حَكِيمٍ wise

4

4.

On that night is made distinct every precise matter -

4

amran أَمْرًا A command min مِنْ from ʿindinā عِنْدِنَا Us innā إِنَّا Indeed We kunnā كُنَّا [We] are mur'silīna مُرْسِلِينَ (ever) sending

5

5.

[Every] matter [proceeding] from Us. Indeed, We were to send [a messenger]

5

raḥmatan رَحْمَةً As Mercy min مِنْ from rabbika رَبِّكَ your Lord innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed He huwa هُوَ [He] l-samīʿu السَّمِيعُ (is) the All-Hearer l-ʿalīmu الْعَلِيمُ the All-Knower

6

6.

As mercy from your Lord. Indeed, He is the Hearing, the Knowing.

6

rabbi رَبِّ Lord l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth wamā وَمَا and whatever baynahumā بَيْنَهُمَا (is) between both of them in إِنْ if kuntum كُنْتُمْ you mūqinīna مُوقِنِينَ would be certain

7

7.

Lord of the heavens and the earth and that between them, if you would be certain.

7

lā لَا (There is) no ilāha إِلَهَ god illā إِلَّا except huwa هُوَ Him yuḥ'yī يُحْيِي He gives life wayumītu وَيُمِيتُ and causes death rabbukum رَبُّكُمْ your Lord warabbu وَرَبُّ and (the) Lord ābāikumu ءَابَائِكُمُ (of) your fathers l-awalīna الْأَوَّلِينَ the former

8

8.

There is no deity except Him; He gives life and causes death. [He is] your Lord and the Lord of your first forefathers.

8

bal بَلْ Nay hum هُمْ they fī فِي (are) in shakkin شَكٍّ doubt yalʿabūna يَلْعَبُونَ playing

9

9.

But they are in doubt, amusing themselves.

9

fa-ir'taqib فَارْتَقِبْ Then watch yawma يَوْمَ (for the) Day tatī تَأْتِي (when) will bring l-samāu السَّمَاءُ the sky bidukhānin بِدُخَانٍ smoke mubīnin مُبِينٍ visible

10

10.

Then watch for the Day when the sky will bring a visible smoke.

10

yaghshā يَغْشَى Enveloping l-nāsa النَّاسَ the people hādhā هَذَا This ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (will be) a punishment alīmun أَلِيمٌ painful

11

11.

Covering the people; this is a painful torment.

11

rabbanā رَبَّنَا Our Lord! ik'shif اكْشِفْ Remove ʿannā عَنَّا from us l-ʿadhāba الْعَذَابَ the punishment innā إِنَّا indeed we mu'minūna مُؤْمِنُونَ (are) believers

12

12.

[They will say], "Our Lord, remove from us the torment; indeed, we are believers."

12

annā أَنَّى How can lahumu لَهُمُ (there be) for them l-dhik'rā الذِّكْرَى the reminder waqad وَقَدْ when verily jāahum جَاءَهُمْ had come to them rasūlun رَسُولٌ a Messenger mubīnun مُبِينٌ clear

13

13.

How will there be for them a reminder [at that time]? And there had come to them a clear Messenger.

13

thumma ثُمَّ Then tawallaw تَوَلَّوْا they turned away ʿanhu عَنْهُ from him waqālū وَقَالُوا and said muʿallamun مُعَلَّمٌ One taught majnūnun مَجْنُونٌ a mad man

14

14.

Then they turned away from him and said, "[He was] taught [and is] a madman."

14

innā إِنَّا Indeed We kāshifū كَاشِفُوا (will) remove l-ʿadhābi الْعَذَابِ the punishment qalīlan قَلِيلًا a little innakum إِنَّكُمْ indeed you ʿāidūna عَائِدُونَ (will) return

15

15.

Indeed, We will remove the torment for a little. Indeed, you [disbelievers] will return [to disbelief].

15

yawma يَوْمَ (The) Day nabṭishu نَبْطِشُ We will seize l-baṭshata الْبَطْشَةَ (with) the seizure l-kub'rā الْكُبْرَى the greatest innā إِنَّا indeed We muntaqimūna مُنْتَقِمُونَ (will) take retribution

16

16.

The Day We will strike with the greatest assault, indeed, We will take retribution.

16

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly fatannā فَتَنَّا We tried qablahum قَبْلَهُمْ before them qawma قَوْمَ (the) people fir'ʿawna فِرْعَوْنَ (of) Firaun wajāahum وَجَاءَهُمْ and came to them rasūlun رَسُولٌ a Messenger karīmun كَرِيمٌ noble

17

17.

And We had already tried before them the people of Pharaoh, and there came to them a noble messenger,

17

an أَنْ That addū أَدُّوا Deliver ilayya إِلَيَّ to me ʿibāda عِبَادَ (the) servants l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah innī إِنِّي Indeed I am lakum لَكُمْ to you rasūlun رَسُولٌ a Messenger amīnun أَمِينٌ trustworthy

18

18.

[Saying], "Render to me the servants of Allah. Indeed, I am to you a trustworthy messenger,"

18

wa-an وَأَنْ And that lā لَا (do) not taʿlū تَعْلُوا exalt yourselves ʿalā عَلَى against l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah innī إِنِّي Indeed I ātīkum ءَاتِيكُمْ [I] have come to you bisul'ṭānin بِسُلْطَانٍ with an authority mubīnin مُبِينٍ clear

19

19.

And [saying], "Be not haughty with Allah. Indeed, I have come to you with clear authority.

19

wa-innī وَإِنِّي And indeed I ʿudh'tu عُذْتُ [I] seek refuge birabbī بِرَبِّي with my Lord warabbikum وَرَبِّكُمْ and your Lord an أَنْ lest tarjumūni تَرْجُمُونِ you stone me

20

20.

And indeed, I have sought refuge in my Lord and your Lord, lest you stone me.

20

wa-in وَإِنْ And if lam لَمْ not tu'minū تُؤْمِنُوا you believe lī لِي me fa-iʿ'tazilūni فَاعْتَزِلُونِ then leave me alone

21

21.

But if you do not believe me, then leave me alone."

21

fadaʿā فَدَعَا So he called rabbahu رَبَّهُ his Lord anna أَنَّ That hāulāi هَؤُلَاءِ these qawmun قَوْمٌ (are) a people muj'rimūna مُجْرِمُونَ criminals

22

22.

And [finally] he called to his Lord that these were a criminal people.

22

fa-asri فَأَسْرِ Then Set out biʿibādī بِعِبَادِي with My slaves laylan لَيْلًا (by) night innakum إِنَّكُمْ Indeed you muttabaʿūna مُتَّبَعُونَ (will be) followed

23

23.

[Allah said], "Then set out with My servants by night. Indeed, you are to be pursued.

23

wa-ut'ruki وَاتْرُكِ And leave l-baḥra الْبَحْرَ the sea rahwan رَهْوًا at rest innahum إِنَّهُمْ Indeed they jundun جُنْدٌ (are) an army mugh'raqūna مُغْرَقُونَ (to be) drowned

24

24.

And leave the sea in stillness. Indeed, they are an army to be drowned."

24

kam كَمْ How many tarakū تَرَكُوا (did) they leave min مِنْ of jannātin جَنَّاتٍ gardens waʿuyūnin وَعُيُونٍ and springs

25

25.

How much they left behind of gardens and springs

25

wazurūʿin وَزُرُوعٍ And cornfields wamaqāmin وَمَقَامٍ and places karīmin كَرِيمٍ noble

26

26.

And crops and noble sites

26

wanaʿmatin وَنَعْمَةٍ And pleasant things kānū كَانُوا they used (to) fīhā فِيهَا therein fākihīna فَاكِهِينَ take delight!

27

27.

And comfort wherein they were amused.

27

kadhālika كَذَلِكَ Thus wa-awrathnāhā وَأَوْرَثْنَاهَا And We made it (an) inherit(ance) qawman قَوْمًا (for) a people ākharīna ءَاخَرِينَ another

28

28.

Thus. And We caused to inherit it another people.

28

famā فَمَا And not bakat بَكَتْ wept ʿalayhimu عَلَيْهِمُ for them l-samāu السَّمَاءُ the heaven wal-arḍu وَالْأَرْضُ and the earth wamā وَمَا and not kānū كَانُوا they were munẓarīna مُنْظَرِينَ given respite

29

29.

And the heaven and earth wept not for them, nor were they reprieved.

29

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly najjaynā نَجَّيْنَا We saved banī بَنِي (the) Children of Israel is'rāīla إِسْرَاءِيلَ (the) Children of Israel mina مِنَ from l-ʿadhābi الْعَذَابِ the punishment l-muhīni الْمُهِينِ the humiliating

30

30.

And We certainly saved the Children of Israel from the humiliating torment -

30

min مِنْ From fir'ʿawna فِرْعَوْنَ Firaun innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed he kāna كَانَ was ʿāliyan عَالِيًا arrogant mina مِنَ among l-mus'rifīna الْمُسْرِفِينَ the transgressors

31

31.

From Pharaoh. Indeed, he was a haughty one among the transgressors.

31

walaqadi وَلَقَدِ And certainly ikh'tarnāhum اخْتَرْنَاهُمْ We chose them ʿalā عَلَى by ʿil'min عِلْمٍ knowledge ʿalā عَلَى over l-ʿālamīna الْعَالَمِينَ the worlds

32

32.

And We certainly chose them by knowledge over [all] the worlds.

32

waātaynāhum وَءَاتَيْنَاهُمْ And We gave them mina مِنَ of l-āyāti الْءَايَاتِ the Signs mā مَا that fīhi فِيهِ in it balāon بَلَؤٌا (was) a trial mubīnun مُبِينٌ clear

33

33.

And We gave them of signs that in which there was a clear trial.

33

inna إِنَّ Indeed hāulāi هَؤُلَاءِ these layaqūlūna لَيَقُولُونَ surely they say

34

34.

Indeed, these [disbelievers] are saying,

34

in إِنْ Not hiya هِيَ it illā إِلَّا (is) but mawtatunā مَوْتَتُنَا our death l-ūlā الْأُولَى the first wamā وَمَا and not naḥnu نَحْنُ we bimunsharīna بِمُنْشَرِينَ (will be) raised again

35

35.

"There is not but our first death, and we will not be resurrected.

35

fatū فَأْتُوا Then bring biābāinā بِءَابَائِنَا our forefathers in إِنْ if kuntum كُنْتُمْ you are ṣādiqīna صَادِقِينَ truthful

36

36.

Then bring [back] our forefathers, if you should be truthful."

36

ahum أَهُمْ Are they khayrun خَيْرٌ better am أَمْ or qawmu قَوْمُ (the) people tubbaʿin تُبَّعٍ (of) Tubba wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ and those min مِنْ before them qablihim قَبْلِهِمْ before them ahlaknāhum أَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ We destroyed them innahum إِنَّهُمْ indeed, they kānū كَانُوا were muj'rimīna مُجْرِمِينَ criminals

37

37.

Are they better or the people of Tubba' and those before them? We destroyed them, [for] indeed, they were criminals.

37

wamā وَمَا And not khalaqnā خَلَقْنَا We created l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth wamā وَمَا and whatever baynahumā بَيْنَهُمَا (is) between them lāʿibīna لَاعِبِينَ (in) play

38

38.

And We did not create the heavens and earth and that between them in play.

38

mā مَا Not khalaqnāhumā خَلَقْنَاهُمَا We created both of them illā إِلَّا but bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in [the] truth walākinna وَلَكِنَّ but aktharahum أَكْثَرَهُمْ most of them lā لَا (do) not yaʿlamūna يَعْلَمُونَ know

39

39.

We did not create them except in truth, but most of them do not know.

39

inna إِنَّ Indeed yawma يَوْمَ (the) Day of Judgment l-faṣli الْفَصْلِ (the) Day of Judgment mīqātuhum مِيقَاتُهُمْ (is) an appointed term for them ajmaʿīna أَجْمَعِينَ all

40

40.

Indeed, the Day of Judgement is the appointed time for them all -

40

yawma يَوْمَ (The) Day lā لَا not yugh'nī يُغْنِي will avail mawlan مَوْلًى a relation ʿan عَنْ for mawlan مَوْلًى a relation shayan شَيْئًا anything walā وَلَا and not hum هُمْ they yunṣarūna يُنْصَرُونَ will be helped

41

41.

The Day when no relation will avail a relation at all, nor will they be helped -

41

illā إِلَّا Except man مَنْ (on) whom raḥima رَحِمَ Allah has mercy l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah has mercy innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed, He huwa هُوَ [He] l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ (is) the All-Mighty l-raḥīmu الرَّحِيمُ the Most Merciful

42

42.

Except those [believers] on whom Allah has mercy. Indeed, He is the Exalted in Might, the Merciful.

42

inna إِنَّ Indeed shajarata شَجَرَتَ (the) tree l-zaqūmi الزَّقُّومِ (of) Zaqqum

43

43.

Indeed, the tree of zaqqum

43

ṭaʿāmu طَعَامُ (Will be) food l-athīmi الْأَثِيمِ (of) the sinner(s)

44

44.

Is food for the sinful.

44

kal-muh'li كَالْمُهْلِ Like the murky oil yaghlī يَغْلِي it will boil fī فِي in l-buṭūni الْبُطُونِ the bellies

45

45.

Like murky oil, it boils within bellies

45

kaghalyi كَغَلْيِ Like boiling l-ḥamīmi الْحَمِيمِ (of) scalding water

46

46.

Like the boiling of scalding water.

46

khudhūhu خُذُوهُ Seize him fa-iʿ'tilūhu فَاعْتِلُوهُ and drag him ilā إِلَى into sawāi سَوَاءِ (the) midst l-jaḥīmi الْجَحِيمِ (of) the Hellfire

47

47.

[It will be commanded], "Seize him and drag him into the midst of the Hellfire,

47

thumma ثُمَّ Then ṣubbū صُبُّوا pour fawqa فَوْقَ over rasihi رَأْسِهِ his head min مِنْ of ʿadhābi عَذَابِ (the) punishment l-ḥamīmi الْحَمِيمِ (of) the scalding water

48

48.

Then pour over his head from the torment of scalding water."

48

dhuq ذُقْ Taste! innaka إِنَّكَ Indeed you anta أَنْتَ [you] (were) l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ the mighty l-karīmu الْكَرِيمُ the noble

49

49.

[It will be said], "Taste! Indeed, you are the honored, the noble!

49

inna إِنَّ Indeed hādhā هَذَا this mā مَا (is) what kuntum كُنْتُمْ you used (to) bihi بِهِ [about it] tamtarūna تَمْتَرُونَ doubt

50

50.

Indeed, this is what you used to dispute."

50

inna إِنَّ Indeed l-mutaqīna الْمُتَّقِينَ the righteous fī فِي (will be) in maqāmin مَقَامٍ a place amīnin أَمِينٍ secure

51

51.

Indeed, the righteous will be in a secure place;

51
