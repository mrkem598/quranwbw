lā لَا Nay! uq'simu أُقْسِمُ I swear bihādhā بِهَذَا by this l-baladi الْبَلَدِ city

1

1.

I swear by this city, Makkah -

1

wa-anta وَأَنْتَ And you ḥillun حِلٌّ (are) free (to dwell) bihādhā بِهَذَا in this l-baladi الْبَلَدِ city

2

2.

And you, [O Muhammad], are free of restriction in this city -

2

wawālidin وَوَالِدٍ And the begetter wamā وَمَا and what walada وَلَدَ he begot

3

3.

And [by] the father and that which was born [of him],

3

laqad لَقَدْ Certainly khalaqnā خَلَقْنَا We have created l-insāna الْإِنْسَانَ man fī فِي (to be) in kabadin كَبَدٍ hardship

4

4.

We have certainly created man into hardship.

4

ayaḥsabu أَيَحْسَبُ Does he think an أَنْ that lan لَنْ not yaqdira يَقْدِرَ has power ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ over him aḥadun أَحَدٌ anyone

5

5.

Does he think that never will anyone overcome him?

5

yaqūlu يَقُولُ He will say ahlaktu أَهْلَكْتُ I have squandered mālan مَالًا wealth lubadan لُبَدًا abundant

6

6.

He says, "I have spent wealth in abundance."

6

ayaḥsabu أَيَحْسَبُ Does he think an أَنْ that lam لَمْ not yarahu يَرَهُ sees him aḥadun أَحَدٌ anyone

7

7.

Does he think that no one has seen him?

7

alam أَلَمْ Have not najʿal نَجْعَلْ We made lahu لَهُ for him ʿaynayni عَيْنَيْنِ two eyes

8

8.

Have We not made for him two eyes?

8

walisānan وَلِسَانًا And a tongue washafatayni وَشَفَتَيْنِ and two lips

9

9.

And a tongue and two lips?

9

wahadaynāhu وَهَدَيْنَاهُ And shown him l-najdayni النَّجْدَيْنِ the two ways

10

10.

And have shown him the two ways?

10

falā فَلَا But not iq'taḥama اقْتَحَمَ he has attempted l-ʿaqabata الْعَقَبَةَ the steep path

11

11.

But he has not broken through the difficult pass.

11

wamā وَمَا And what adrāka أَدْرَاكَ can make you know mā مَا what l-ʿaqabatu الْعَقَبَةُ the steep path is

12

12.

And what can make you know what is [breaking through] the difficult pass?

12

fakku فَكُّ (It is) freeing raqabatin رَقَبَةٍ a neck

13

13.

It is the freeing of a slave

13

aw أَوْ Or iṭ'ʿāmun إِطْعَامٌ feeding fī فِي in yawmin يَوْمٍ a day dhī ذِي of masghabatin مَسْغَبَةٍ severe hunger

14

14.

Or feeding on a day of severe hunger

14

yatīman يَتِيمًا An orphan dhā ذَا of maqrabatin مَقْرَبَةٍ near relationship

15

15.

An orphan of near relationship

15

aw أَوْ Or mis'kīnan مِسْكِينًا a needy person dhā ذَا in matrabatin مَتْرَبَةٍ misery

16

16.

Or a needy person in misery

16

thumma ثُمَّ Then kāna كَانَ he is mina مِنَ of alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe watawāṣaw وَتَوَاصَوْا and enjoin each other bil-ṣabri بِالصَّبْرِ to patience watawāṣaw وَتَوَاصَوْا and enjoin each other bil-marḥamati بِالْمَرْحَمَةِ to compassion

17

17.

And then being among those who believed and advised one another to patience and advised one another to compassion.

17

ulāika أُولَئِكَ Those aṣḥābu أَصْحَابُ (are the) companions l-maymanati الْمَيْمَنَةِ (of) the right hand

18

18.

Those are the companions of the right.

18

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ But those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve biāyātinā بِءَايَاتِنَا in Our Verses hum هُمْ they aṣḥābu أَصْحَابُ (are the) companions l-mashamati الْمَشْءَمَةِ (of) the left hand

19

19.

But they who disbelieved in Our signs - those are the companions of the left.

19

ʿalayhim عَلَيْهِمْ Over them nārun نَارٌ (will be the) Fire mu'ṣadatun مُؤْصَدَةٌ closed in

20

20.

Over them will be fire closed in.

20
