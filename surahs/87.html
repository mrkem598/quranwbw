sabbiḥi سَبِّحِ Glorify is'ma اسْمَ (the) name rabbika رَبِّكَ (of) your Lord l-aʿlā الْأَعْلَى the Most High

1

1.

Exalt the name of your Lord, the Most High,

1

alladhī الَّذِي The One Who khalaqa خَلَقَ created fasawwā فَسَوَّى then proportioned

2

2.

Who created and proportioned

2

wa-alladhī وَالَّذِي And the One Who qaddara قَدَّرَ measured fahadā فَهَدَى then guided

3

3.

And who destined and [then] guided

3

wa-alladhī وَالَّذِي And the One Who akhraja أَخْرَجَ brings forth l-marʿā الْمَرْعَى the pasture

4

4.

And who brings out the pasture

4

fajaʿalahu فَجَعَلَهُ And then made it ghuthāan غُثَاءً stubble aḥwā أَحْوَى dark

5

5.

And [then] makes it black stubble.

sanuq'ri-uka سَنُقْرِئُكَ We will make you recite falā فَلَا so not tansā تَنْسَى you will forget

6

6.

We will make you recite, [O Muhammad], and you will not forget,

6

illā إِلَّا Except mā مَا what shāa شَاءَ wills l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed, He yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ knows l-jahra الْجَهْرَ the manifest wamā وَمَا and what yakhfā يَخْفَى is hidden

7

7.

Except what Allah should will. Indeed, He knows what is declared and what is hidden.

7

wanuyassiruka وَنُيَسِّرُكَ And We will ease you lil'yus'rā لِلْيُسْرَى to the ease

8

8.

And We will ease you toward ease.

8

fadhakkir فَذَكِّرْ So remind in إِنْ if nafaʿati نَفَعَتِ benefits l-dhik'rā الذِّكْرَى the reminder

9

9.

So remind, if the reminder should benefit;

9

sayadhakkaru سَيَذَّكَّرُ He will pay heed man مَنْ (one) who yakhshā يَخْشَى fears (Allah)

10

10.

He who fears [Allah] will be reminded.

10

wayatajannabuhā وَيَتَجَنَّبُهَا And will avoid it l-ashqā الْأَشْقَى the wretched one

11

11.

But the wretched one will avoid it -

11

alladhī الَّذِي The one who yaṣlā يَصْلَى will burn l-nāra النَّارَ (in) the Fire l-kub'rā الْكُبْرَى [the] great

12

12.

[He] who will [enter and] burn in the greatest Fire,

thumma ثُمَّ Then lā لَا not yamūtu يَمُوتُ he will die fīhā فِيهَا therein walā وَلَا and not yaḥyā يَحْيَى will live

13

13.

Neither dying therein nor living.

14.

15.

And mentions the name of his Lord and prays.

17.

While the Hereafter is better and more enduring.
