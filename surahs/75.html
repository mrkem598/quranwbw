lā لَا Nay! uq'simu أُقْسِمُ I swear biyawmi بِيَوْمِ by (the) Day l-qiyāmati الْقِيَامَةِ (of) the Resurrection

1

1.

I swear by the Day of Resurrection

1

walā وَلَا And nay! uq'simu أُقْسِمُ I swear bil-nafsi بِالنَّفْسِ by the soul l-lawāmati اللَّوَّامَةِ self-accusing

2

2.

And I swear by the reproaching soul [to the certainty of resurrection].

2

ayaḥsabu أَيَحْسَبُ Does think l-insānu الْإِنْسَانُ [the] man allan أَلَّنْ that not najmaʿa نَجْمَعَ We will assemble ʿiẓāmahu عِظَامَهُ his bones

3

3.

Does man think that We will not assemble his bones?

3

balā بَلَى Nay! qādirīna قَادِرِينَ [We are] able ʿalā عَلَى on an أَنْ that nusawwiya نُسَوِّيَ We can restore banānahu بَنَانَهُ his fingertips

4

4.

Yes. [We are] Able [even] to proportion his fingertips.

4

bal بَلْ Nay! yurīdu يُرِيدُ Desires l-insānu الْإِنْسَانُ [the] man liyafjura لِيَفْجُرَ to give (the) lie amāmahu أَمَامَهُ (to) what is before him

5

5.

But man desires to continue in sin.

5

yasalu يَسْءَلُ He asks ayyāna أَيَّانَ When yawmu يَوْمُ (is the) Day l-qiyāmati الْقِيَامَةِ (of) the Resurrection

6

6.

He asks, "When is the Day of Resurrection?"

And the sun and the moon are joined,

yaqūlu يَقُولُ Will say l-insānu الْإِنْسَانُ [the] man yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ that Day ayna أَيْنَ Where l-mafaru الْمَفَرُّ (is) the escape

10

10.

Man will say on that Day, "Where is the [place of] escape?"

10

11

11.

No! There is no refuge.

11

12

12.

To your Lord, that Day, is the [place of] permanence.

13

13.

Man will be informed that Day of what he sent ahead and kept back.

14

14.

Rather, man, against himself, will be a witness,

17.

Does man think that he will be left neglected?
