idhā إِذَا When zul'zilati زُلْزِلَتِ is shaken l-arḍu الْأَرْضُ the earth zil'zālahā زِلْزَالَهَا with its earthquake

1

1.

When the earth is shaken with its [final] earthquake

1

wa-akhrajati وَأَخْرَجَتِ And brings forth l-arḍu الْأَرْضُ the earth athqālahā أَثْقَالَهَا its burdens

2

2.

And the earth discharges its burdens

2

waqāla وَقَالَ And says l-insānu الْإِنْسَانُ man mā مَا What lahā لَهَا (is) with it

3

3.

And man says, "What is [wrong] with it?" -

3

yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ That Day tuḥaddithu تُحَدِّثُ it will report akhbārahā أَخْبَارَهَا its news

4

4.

That Day, it will report its news

4

bi-anna بِأَنَّ Because rabbaka رَبَّكَ your Lord awḥā أَوْحَى inspired lahā لَهَا [to] it

5

5.

Because your Lord has commanded it.

5

yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ That Day yaṣduru يَصْدُرُ will proceed l-nāsu النَّاسُ the mankind ashtātan أَشْتَاتًا (in) scattered groups liyuraw لِيُرَوْا to be shown aʿmālahum أَعْمَالَهُمْ their deeds

6

6.

That Day, the people will depart separated [into categories] to be shown [the result of] their deeds.

6

faman فَمَنْ So whoever yaʿmal يَعْمَلْ does mith'qāla مِثْقَالَ (equal to the) weight dharratin ذَرَّةٍ (of) an atom khayran خَيْرًا good yarahu يَرَهُ will see it

7

7.

So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see it,

7

waman وَمَنْ And whoever yaʿmal يَعْمَلْ does mith'qāla مِثْقَالَ (equal to the) weight dharratin ذَرَّةٍ (of) an atom sharran شَرًّا evil yarahu يَرَهُ will see it

8

8.

And whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see it.
