alif-Lam-meem-sad الٓمٓصٓ Alif Laam Meem Saad

1

1.

Alif, Lam, Meem, Sad.

1

kitābun كِتَابٌ A Book unzila أُنْزِلَ revealed ilayka إِلَيْكَ to you falā فَلَا so (let) not yakun يَكُنْ be fī فِي in ṣadrika صَدْرِكَ your breast ḥarajun حَرَجٌ any uneasiness min'hu مِنْهُ from it litundhira لِتُنْذِرَ that you warn bihi بِهِ with it wadhik'rā وَذِكْرَى and a reminder lil'mu'minīna لِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ for the believers

2

2.

[This is] a Book revealed to you, [O Muhammad] - so let there not be in your breast distress therefrom - that you may warn thereby and as a reminder to the believers.

2

ittabiʿū اتَّبِعُوا Follow mā مَا what unzila أُنْزِلَ has been revealed ilaykum إِلَيْكُمْ to you min مِنْ from rabbikum رَبِّكُمْ your Lord walā وَلَا and (do) not tattabiʿū تَتَّبِعُوا follow min مِنْ from dūnihi دُونِهِ beside Him awliyāa أَوْلِيَاءَ any allies qalīlan قَلِيلًا Little mā مَا (is) what tadhakkarūna تَذَكَّرُونَ you remember

3

3.

Follow, [O mankind], what has been revealed to you from your Lord and do not follow other than Him any allies. Little do you remember.

3

wakam وَكَمْ And how many min مِنْ of qaryatin قَرْيَةٍ a city ahlaknāhā أَهْلَكْنَاهَا We destroyed it fajāahā فَجَاءَهَا and came to it basunā بَأْسُنَا Our punishment bayātan بَيَاتًا (at) night aw أَوْ or hum هُمْ (while) they qāilūna قَائِلُونَ were sleeping at noon

4

4.

And how many cities have We destroyed, and Our punishment came to them at night or while they were sleeping at noon.

4

famā فَمَا Then not kāna كَانَ was daʿwāhum دَعْوَاهُمْ their plea idh إِذْ when jāahum جَاءَهُمْ came to them basunā بَأْسُنَا Our punishment illā إِلَّا except an أَنْ that qālū قَالُوا they said innā إِنَّا Indeed we kunnā كُنَّا were ẓālimīna ظَالِمِينَ wrongdoers

5

5.

And their declaration when Our punishment came to them was only that they said, "Indeed, we were wrongdoers!"

5

falanasalanna فَلَنَسْءَلَنَّ Then surely We will question alladhīna الَّذِينَ those (to) whom ur'sila أُرْسِلَ were sent ilayhim إِلَيْهِمْ to them (Messengers) walanasalanna وَلَنَسْءَلَنَّ and surely We will question l-mur'salīna الْمُرْسَلِينَ the Messengers

6

6.

Then We will surely question those to whom [a message] was sent, and We will surely question the messengers.

6

falanaquṣṣanna فَلَنَقُصَّنَّ Then surely We will narrate ʿalayhim عَلَيْهِمْ to them biʿil'min بِعِلْمٍ with knowledge wamā وَمَا and not kunnā كُنَّا We were ghāibīna غَائِبِينَ absent

7

7.

Then We will surely relate [their deeds] to them with knowledge, and We were not [at all] absent.

7

wal-waznu وَالْوَزْنُ And the weighing yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ that day l-ḥaqu الْحَقُّ (will be) the truth faman فَمَنْ So whose thaqulat ثَقُلَتْ (will be) heavy mawāzīnuhu مَوَازِينُهُ his scales fa-ulāika فَأُولَئِكَ then those humu هُمُ [they] l-muf'liḥūna الْمُفْلِحُونَ (will be) the successful ones

8

8.

And the weighing [of deeds] that Day will be the truth. So those whose scales are heavy - it is they who will be the successful.

8

waman وَمَنْ And (for) those khaffat خَفَّتْ (will be) light mawāzīnuhu مَوَازِينُهُ his scales fa-ulāika فَأُولَئِكَ so those alladhīna الَّذِينَ (will be) the ones who khasirū خَسِرُوا lost anfusahum أَنْفُسَهُمْ themselves bimā بِمَا because kānū كَانُوا they were biāyātinā بِءَايَاتِنَا to Our Verses yaẓlimūna يَظْلِمُونَ (doing) injustice

9

9.

And those whose scales are light - they are the ones who will lose themselves for what injustice they were doing toward Our verses.

9

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly makkannākum مَكَّنَّاكُمْ We established you fī فِي in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth wajaʿalnā وَجَعَلْنَا and We made lakum لَكُمْ for you fīhā فِيهَا in it maʿāyisha مَعَايِشَ livelihood qalīlan قَلِيلًا Little mā مَا (is) what tashkurūna تَشْكُرُونَ you (are) grateful

10

10.

And We have certainly established you upon the earth and made for you therein ways of livelihood. Little are you grateful.

10

walaqad وَلَقَدْ And certainly khalaqnākum خَلَقْنَاكُمْ We created you thumma ثُمَّ then ṣawwarnākum صَوَّرْنَاكُمْ We fashioned you thumma ثُمَّ Then qul'nā قُلْنَا We said lil'malāikati لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ to the Angels us'judū اسْجُدُوا Prostrate liādama لِءَادَمَ to Adam fasajadū فَسَجَدُوا So they prostrated illā إِلَّا except ib'līsa إِبْلِيسَ Iblees lam لَمْ Not yakun يَكُنْ he was mina مِنَ of l-sājidīna السَّاجِدِينَ those who prostrated

11

11.

And We have certainly created you, [O Mankind], and given you [human] form. Then We said to the angels, "Prostrate to Adam"; so they prostrated, except for Iblees. He was not of those who prostrated.

11

qāla قَالَ (Allah) said mā مَا What manaʿaka مَنَعَكَ prevented you allā أَلَّا that not tasjuda تَسْجُدَ you prostrate idh إِذْ when amartuka أَمَرْتُكَ I commanded you qāla قَالَ (Shaitaan) said anā أَنَا I am khayrun خَيْرٌ better min'hu مِنْهُ than him khalaqtanī خَلَقْتَنِي You created me min مِنْ from nārin نَارٍ fire wakhalaqtahu وَخَلَقْتَهُ and You created him min مِنْ from ṭīnin طِينٍ clay

12

12.

[Allah] said, "What prevented you from prostrating when I commanded you?" [Satan] said, "I am better than him. You created me from fire and created him from clay."

12

qāla قَالَ (Allah) said fa-ih'biṭ فَاهْبِطْ Then go down min'hā مِنْهَا from it famā فَمَا for not yakūnu يَكُونُ it is laka لَكَ for you an أَنْ that tatakabbara تَتَكَبَّرَ you be arrogant fīhā فِيهَا in it fa-ukh'ruj فَاخْرُجْ So get out innaka إِنَّكَ indeed you mina مِنَ (are) of l-ṣāghirīna الصَّاغِرِينَ the disgraced ones

13

13.

[Allah] said, "Descend from Paradise, for it is not for you to be arrogant therein. So get out; indeed, you are of the debased.

13

qāla قَالَ (Shaitaan) said anẓir'nī أَنْظِرْنِي Give me respite ilā إِلَى till yawmi يَوْمِ (the) Day yub'ʿathūna يُبْعَثُونَ they are raised up

14

14.

[Satan] said, "Reprieve me until the Day they are resurrected."

14

qāla قَالَ (Allah) said innaka إِنَّكَ Indeed you mina مِنَ (are) of l-munẓarīna الْمُنْظَرِينَ the ones given respite

15

15.

[Allah] said, "Indeed, you are of those reprieved."

15

qāla قَالَ (Shaitaan) said fabimā فَبِمَا Because aghwaytanī أَغْوَيْتَنِي You have sent me astray la-aqʿudanna لَأَقْعُدَنَّ surely I will sit lahum لَهُمْ for them ṣirāṭaka صِرَاطَكَ (on) Your path l-mus'taqīma الْمُسْتَقِيمَ the straight

16

16.

[Satan] said, "Because You have put me in error, I will surely sit in wait for them on Your straight path.

16

thumma ثُمَّ Then laātiyannahum لَءَاتِيَنَّهُمْ surely, I will come to them min مِنْ from bayni بَيْنِ before aydīhim أَيْدِيهِمْ them wamin وَمِنْ and from khalfihim خَلْفِهِمْ behind them waʿan وَعَنْ and from aymānihim أَيْمَانِهِمْ their right waʿan وَعَنْ and from shamāilihim شَمَائِلِهِمْ their left walā وَلَا and not tajidu تَجِدُ You (will) find aktharahum أَكْثَرَهُمْ most of them shākirīna شَاكِرِينَ grateful

17

17.

Then I will come to them from before them and from behind them and on their right and on their left, and You will not find most of them grateful [to You]."

17

qāla قَالَ (Allah) said ukh'ruj اخْرُجْ Get out min'hā مِنْهَا of it madhūman مَذْءُومًا disgraced madḥūran مَدْحُورًا and expelled laman لَمَنْ Certainly whoever tabiʿaka تَبِعَكَ follows you min'hum مِنْهُمْ among them la-amla-anna لَأَمْلَأَنَّ surely I will fill jahannama جَهَنَّمَ Hell minkum مِنْكُمْ with you ajmaʿīna أَجْمَعِينَ all

18

18.

[Allah] said, "Get out of Paradise, reproached and expelled. Whoever follows you among them - I will surely fill Hell with you, all together."

18

wayāādamu وَيَاءَادَمُ And O Adam! us'kun اسْكُنْ Dwell anta أَنْتَ you wazawjuka وَزَوْجُكَ and your wife l-janata الْجَنَّةَ (in) the Garden fakulā فَكُلَا and you both eat min مِنْ from ḥaythu حَيْثُ wherever shi'tumā شِئْتُمَا you both wish walā وَلَا but (do) not taqrabā تَقْرَبَا approach [you both] hādhihi هَذِهِ this l-shajarata الشَّجَرَةَ [the] tree fatakūnā فَتَكُونَا lest you both be mina مِنَ among l-ẓālimīna الظَّالِمِينَ the wrongdoers

19

19.

And "O Adam, dwell, you and your wife, in Paradise and eat from wherever you will but do not approach this tree, lest you be among the wrongdoers."

19

fawaswasa فَوَسْوَسَ Then whispered lahumā لَهُمَا to both of them l-shayṭānu الشَّيْطَانُ the Shaitaan liyub'diya لِيُبْدِيَ to make apparent lahumā لَهُمَا to both of them mā مَا what wūriya وُورِيَ was concealed ʿanhumā عَنْهُمَا from both of them min مِنْ of sawātihimā سَوْءَاتِهِمَا their shame waqāla وَقَالَ And he said mā مَا (Did) not nahākumā نَهَاكُمَا forbid you both rabbukumā رَبُّكُمَا your Lord ʿan عَنْ from hādhihi هَذِهِ this l-shajarati الشَّجَرَةِ [the] tree illā إِلَّا except an أَنْ that takūnā تَكُونَا you two become malakayni مَلَكَيْنِ Angels aw أَوْ or takūnā تَكُونَا you two become mina مِنَ of l-khālidīna الْخَالِدِينَ the immortals

20

20.

But Satan whispered to them to make apparent to them that which was concealed from them of their private parts. He said, "Your Lord did not forbid you this tree except that you become angels or become of the immortal."

20

waqāsamahumā وَقَاسَمَهُمَا And he swore (to) both of them innī إِنِّي Indeed I am lakumā لَكُمَا to both of you lamina لَمِنَ among l-nāṣiḥīna النَّاصِحِينَ the sincere advisers

21

21.

And he swore [by Allah] to them, "Indeed, I am to you from among the sincere advisors."

21

fadallāhumā فَدَلَّاهُمَا So he made both of them fall bighurūrin بِغُرُورٍ by deception falammā فَلَمَّا Then when dhāqā ذَاقَا they both tasted l-shajarata الشَّجَرَةَ the tree badat بَدَتْ became apparent lahumā لَهُمَا to both of them sawātuhumā سَوْءَاتُهُمَا their shame waṭafiqā وَطَفِقَا and they began yakhṣifāni يَخْصِفَانِ (to) fasten ʿalayhimā عَلَيْهِمَا over themselves min مِنْ from waraqi وَرَقِ (the) leaves l-janati الْجَنَّةِ (of) the Garden wanādāhumā وَنَادَاهُمَا And called them both rabbuhumā رَبُّهُمَا their Lord alam أَلَمْ Did not anhakumā أَنْهَكُمَا I forbid you both ʿan عَنْ from til'kumā تِلْكُمَا this l-shajarati الشَّجَرَةِ [the] tree wa-aqul وَأَقُلْ and [I] say lakumā لَكُمَا to both of you inna إِنَّ that l-shayṭāna الشَّيْطَانَ [the] Shaitaan lakumā لَكُمَا to both of you ʿaduwwun عَدُوٌّ (is) an enemy mubīnun مُبِينٌ open

22

22.

So he made them fall, through deception. And when they tasted of the tree, their private parts became apparent to them, and they began to fasten together over themselves from the leaves of Paradise. And their Lord called to them, "Did I not forbid you from that tree and tell you that Satan is to you a clear enemy?"

22

qālā قَالَا Both of them said rabbanā رَبَّنَا Our Lord ẓalamnā ظَلَمْنَا we have wronged anfusanā أَنْفُسَنَا ourselves wa-in وَإِنْ and if lam لَمْ not taghfir تَغْفِرْ You forgive lanā لَنَا [for] us watarḥamnā وَتَرْحَمْنَا and have mercy (on) us lanakūnanna لَنَكُونَنَّ surely we will be mina مِنَ among l-khāsirīna الْخَاسِرِينَ the losers

23

23.

They said, "Our Lord, we have wronged ourselves, and if You do not forgive us and have mercy upon us, we will surely be among the losers."

23

qāla قَالَ (Allah) said ih'biṭū اهْبِطُوا Get down baʿḍukum بَعْضُكُمْ some of you libaʿḍin لِبَعْضٍ to some others ʿaduwwun عَدُوٌّ (as) enemy walakum وَلَكُمْ And for you fī فِي in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth mus'taqarrun مُسْتَقَرٌّ (is) a dwelling place wamatāʿun وَمَتَاعٌ and livelihood ilā إِلَى for ḥīnin حِينٍ a time

24

24.

[Allah] said, "Descend, being to one another enemies. And for you on the earth is a place of settlement and enjoyment for a time."

24

qāla قَالَ He said fīhā فِيهَا In it taḥyawna تَحْيَوْنَ you will live wafīhā وَفِيهَا and in it tamūtūna تَمُوتُونَ you will die wamin'hā وَمِنْهَا and from it tukh'rajūna تُخْرَجُونَ you will be brought forth

25

25.

He said, "Therein you will live, and therein you will die, and from it you will be brought forth."

25

yābanī يَابَنِي O Children ādama ءَادَمَ (of) Adam qad قَدْ Verily anzalnā أَنْزَلْنَا We have sent down ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ to you libāsan لِبَاسًا clothing yuwārī يُوَارِي it covers sawātikum سَوْءَاتِكُمْ your shame warīshan وَرِيشًا and (as) an adornment walibāsu وَلِبَاسُ But the clothing l-taqwā التَّقْوَى (of) [the] righteousness dhālika ذَلِكَ that khayrun خَيْرٌ (is) best dhālika ذَلِكَ That min مِنْ (is) from āyāti ءَايَاتِ (the) Signs l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah laʿallahum لَعَلَّهُمْ so that they may yadhakkarūna يَذَّكَّرُونَ remember

26

26.
