qad قَدْ Indeed samiʿa سَمِعَ has heard l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah qawla قَوْلَ (the) speech allatī الَّتِي (of) one who tujādiluka تُجَادِلُكَ disputes with you fī فِي concerning zawjihā زَوْجِهَا her husband watashtakī وَتَشْتَكِي and she directs her complaint ilā إِلَى to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah yasmaʿu يَسْمَعُ hears taḥāwurakumā تَحَاوُرَكُمَا (the) dialogue of both of you inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah samīʿun سَمِيعٌ (is) All-Hearer baṣīrun بَصِيرٌ All-Seer

1

1.

Certainly has Allah heard the speech of the one who argues with you, [O Muhammad], concerning her husband and directs her complaint to Allah. And Allah hears your dialogue; indeed, Allah is Hearing and Seeing.

1

alladhīna الَّذِينَ Those who yuẓāhirūna يُظَاهِرُونَ pronounce zihar minkum مِنْكُمْ among you min مِنْ [from] nisāihim نِسَائِهِمْ (to) their wives mā مَا not hunna هُنَّ they ummahātihim أُمَّهَاتِهِمْ (are) their mothers in إِنْ Not ummahātuhum أُمَّهَاتُهُمْ (are) their mothers illā إِلَّا except allāī اللَّائِي those who waladnahum وَلَدْنَهُمْ gave them birth wa-innahum وَإِنَّهُمْ And indeed they layaqūlūna لَيَقُولُونَ surely say munkaran مُنْكَرًا an evil mina مِنَ [of] l-qawli الْقَوْلِ [the] word wazūran وَزُورًا and a lie wa-inna وَإِنَّ But indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah laʿafuwwun لَعَفُوٌّ (is) surely Oft-Pardoning ghafūrun غَفُورٌ Oft-Forgiving

2

2.

Those who pronounce thihar among you [to separate] from their wives - they are not [consequently] their mothers. Their mothers are none but those who gave birth to them. And indeed, they are saying an objectionable statement and a falsehood. But indeed, Allah is Pardoning and Forgiving.

2

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those who yuẓāhirūna يُظَاهِرُونَ pronounce zihar min مِنْ [from] nisāihim نِسَائِهِمْ (to) their wives thumma ثُمَّ then yaʿūdūna يَعُودُونَ go back limā لِمَا on what qālū قَالُوا they said fataḥrīru فَتَحْرِيرُ then freeing raqabatin رَقَبَةٍ (of) a slave min مِنْ before qabli قَبْلِ before an أَنْ [that] yatamāssā يَتَمَاسَّا they touch each other dhālikum ذَلِكُمْ That tūʿaẓūna تُوعَظُونَ you are admonished bihi بِهِ to it wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do khabīrun خَبِيرٌ (is) All-Aware

3

3.

And those who pronounce thihar from their wives and then [wish to] go back on what they said - then [there must be] the freeing of a slave before they touch one another. That is what you are admonished thereby; and Allah is Acquainted with what you do.

3

faman فَمَنْ Then whoever lam لَمْ (does) not yajid يَجِدْ find faṣiyāmu فَصِيَامُ then fasting shahrayni شَهْرَيْنِ (for) two months mutatābiʿayni مُتَتَابِعَيْنِ consecutively min مِنْ before qabli قَبْلِ before an أَنْ [that] yatamāssā يَتَمَاسَّا they both touch each other faman فَمَنْ But (he) who lam لَمْ not yastaṭiʿ يَسْتَطِعْ is able fa-iṭ'ʿāmu فَإِطْعَامُ then (the) feeding sittīna سِتِّينَ (of) sixty mis'kīnan مِسْكِينًا needy one(s) dhālika ذَلِكَ That litu'minū لِتُؤْمِنُوا so that you may believe bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah warasūlihi وَرَسُولِهِ and His Messenger watil'ka وَتِلْكَ and these ḥudūdu حُدُودُ (are the) limits l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah walil'kāfirīna وَلِلْكَافِرِينَ and for the disbelievers ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment alīmun أَلِيمٌ painful

4

4.

And he who does not find [a slave] - then a fast for two months consecutively before they touch one another; and he who is unable - then the feeding of sixty poor persons. That is for you to believe [completely] in Allah and His Messenger; and those are the limits [set by] Allah. And for the disbelievers is a painful punishment.

4

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who yuḥāddūna يُحَادُّونَ oppose l-laha اللَّهَ Allah warasūlahu وَرَسُولَهُ and His Messenger kubitū كُبِتُوا (will) be disgraced kamā كَمَا as kubita كُبِتَ were disgraced alladhīna الَّذِينَ those min مِنْ before them qablihim قَبْلِهِمْ before them waqad وَقَدْ And certainly anzalnā أَنْزَلْنَا We have sent down āyātin ءَايَاتٍ Verses bayyinātin بَيِّنَاتٍ clear walil'kāfirīna وَلِلْكَافِرِينَ And for the disbelievers ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment muhīnun مُهِينٌ humiliating

5

5.

Indeed, those who oppose Allah and His Messenger are abased as those before them were abased. And We have certainly sent down verses of clear evidence. And for the disbelievers is a humiliating punishment.

5

yawma يَوْمَ (On the) Day yabʿathuhumu يَبْعَثُهُمُ (when) Allah will raise them l-lahu اللَّهُ (when) Allah will raise them jamīʿan جَمِيعًا all fayunabbi-uhum فَيُنَبِّئُهُمْ and inform them bimā بِمَا of what ʿamilū عَمِلُوا they did aḥṣāhu أَحْصَاهُ Allah has recorded it l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah has recorded it wanasūhu وَنَسُوهُ while they forgot it wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah ʿalā عَلَى (is) over kulli كُلِّ all shayin شَيْءٍ things shahīdun شَهِيدٌ a Witness

6

6.

On the Day when Allah will resurrect them all and inform them of what they did. Allah had enumerated it, while they forgot it; and Allah is, over all things, Witness.

6

alam أَلَمْ Do not tara تَرَ you see anna أَنَّ that l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ knows mā مَا whatever fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wamā وَمَا and whatever fī فِي (is) in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth mā مَا Not yakūnu يَكُونُ there is min مِنْ any najwā نَجْوَى secret counsel thalāthatin ثَلَاثَةٍ (of) three illā إِلَّا but huwa هُوَ He (is) rābiʿuhum رَابِعُهُمْ (the) fourth of them walā وَلَا and not khamsatin خَمْسَةٍ five illā إِلَّا but huwa هُوَ He (is) sādisuhum سَادِسُهُمْ (the) sixth of them walā وَلَا and not adnā أَدْنَى less min مِنْ than dhālika ذَلِكَ that walā وَلَا and not akthara أَكْثَرَ more illā إِلَّا but huwa هُوَ He maʿahum مَعَهُمْ (is) with them ayna أَيْنَ wherever mā مَا wherever kānū كَانُوا they are thumma ثُمَّ Then yunabbi-uhum يُنَبِّئُهُمْ He will inform them bimā بِمَا of what ʿamilū عَمِلُوا they did yawma يَوْمَ (on the) Day l-qiyāmati الْقِيَامَةِ (of) the Resurrection inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah bikulli بِكُلِّ of every shayin شَيْءٍ thing ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knower

7

7.

Have you not considered that Allah knows what is in the heavens and what is on the earth? There is in no private conversation three but that He is the fourth of them, nor are there five but that He is the sixth of them - and no less than that and no more except that He is with them [in knowledge] wherever they are. Then He will inform them of what they did, on the Day of Resurrection. Indeed Allah is, of all things, Knowing.

7

alam أَلَمْ Do not tara تَرَ you see ilā إِلَى [to] alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who nuhū نُهُوا were forbidden ʿani عَنِ from l-najwā النَّجْوَى secret counsels thumma ثُمَّ then yaʿūdūna يَعُودُونَ they return limā لِمَا to what nuhū نُهُوا they were forbidden ʿanhu عَنْهُ from [it] wayatanājawna وَيَتَنَاجَوْنَ and they hold secret counsels bil-ith'mi بِالْإِثْمِ for sin wal-ʿud'wāni وَالْعُدْوَانِ and aggression wamaʿṣiyati وَمَعْصِيَتِ and disobedience l-rasūli الرَّسُولِ (to) the Messenger wa-idhā وَإِذَا And when jāūka جَاءُوكَ they come to you ḥayyawka حَيَّوْكَ they greet you bimā بِمَا with what lam لَمْ not yuḥayyika يُحَيِّكَ greets you bihi بِهِ therewith l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah wayaqūlūna وَيَقُولُونَ and they say fī فِي among anfusihim أَنْفُسِهِمْ themselves lawlā لَوْلَا Why (does) not yuʿadhibunā يُعَذِّبُنَا Allah punish us l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah punish us bimā بِمَا for what naqūlu نَقُولُ we say ḥasbuhum حَسْبُهُمْ Sufficient (for) them jahannamu جَهَنَّمُ (is) Hell yaṣlawnahā يَصْلَوْنَهَا they will burn in it fabi'sa فَبِئْسَ and worst is l-maṣīru الْمَصِيرُ the destination

8

8.

Have you not considered those who were forbidden from private conversation, then they return to that which they were forbidden and converse among themselves about sin and aggression and disobedience to the Messenger? And when they come to you, they greet you with that [word] by which Allah does not greet you and say among themselves, "Why does Allah not punish us for what we say?" Sufficient for them is Hell, which they will [enter to] burn, and wretched is the destination.

8

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O you who believe alladhīna الَّذِينَ O you who believe āmanū ءَامَنُوا O you who believe idhā إِذَا When tanājaytum تَنَاجَيْتُمْ you hold secret counsel falā فَلَا then (do) not tatanājaw تَتَنَاجَوْا hold secret counsel bil-ith'mi بِالْإِثْمِ for sin wal-ʿud'wāni وَالْعُدْوَانِ and aggression wamaʿṣiyati وَمَعْصِيَتِ and disobedience l-rasūli الرَّسُولِ (to) the Messenger watanājaw وَتَنَاجَوْا but hold secret counsel bil-biri بِالْبِرِّ for righteousness wal-taqwā وَالتَّقْوَى and piety wa-ittaqū وَاتَّقُوا And fear l-laha اللَّهَ Allah alladhī الَّذِي the One Who ilayhi إِلَيْهِ to Him tuḥ'sharūna تُحْشَرُونَ you will be gathered

9

9.

O you who have believed, when you converse privately, do not converse about sin and aggression and disobedience to the Messenger but converse about righteousness and piety. And fear Allah, to whom you will be gathered.

9

innamā إِنَّمَا Only l-najwā النَّجْوَى the secret counsels mina مِنَ (are) from l-shayṭāni الشَّيْطَانِ the Shaitaan liyaḥzuna لِيَحْزُنَ that he may grieve alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe walaysa وَلَيْسَ but not biḍārrihim بِضَارِّهِمْ he (can) harm them shayan شَيْئًا (in) anything illā إِلَّا except bi-idh'ni بِإِذْنِ by (Allah's) permission l-lahi اللَّهِ (by) Allah's (permission) waʿalā وَعَلَى And upon l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah falyatawakkali فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ let put (their) trust l-mu'minūna الْمُؤْمِنُونَ the believers

10

10.

Private conversation is only from Satan that he may grieve those who have believed, but he will not harm them at all except by permission of Allah. And upon Allah let the believers rely.

10

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O you who believe alladhīna الَّذِينَ O you who believe āmanū ءَامَنُوا O you who believe idhā إِذَا When qīla قِيلَ it is said lakum لَكُمْ to you tafassaḥū تَفَسَّحُوا Make room fī فِي in l-majālisi الْمَجَالِسِ the assemblies fa-if'saḥū فَافْسَحُوا then make room yafsaḥi يَفْسَحِ Allah will make room l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah will make room lakum لَكُمْ for you wa-idhā وَإِذَا And when qīla قِيلَ it is said unshuzū انْشُزُوا Rise up fa-unshuzū فَانْشُزُوا then rise up yarfaʿi يَرْفَعِ Allah will raise l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah will raise alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe minkum مِنْكُمْ among you wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ and those who ūtū أُوتُوا were given l-ʿil'ma الْعِلْمَ the knowledge darajātin دَرَجَاتٍ (in) degrees wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do khabīrun خَبِيرٌ (is) All-Aware

11

11.

O you who have believed, when you are told, "Space yourselves" in assemblies, then make space; Allah will make space for you. And when you are told, "Arise," then arise; Allah will raise those who have believed among you and those who were given knowledge, by degrees. And Allah is Acquainted with what you do.

11

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O you who believe alladhīna الَّذِينَ O you who believe āmanū ءَامَنُوا O you who believe idhā إِذَا When nājaytumu نَاجَيْتُمُ you privately consult l-rasūla الرَّسُولَ the Messenger faqaddimū فَقَدِّمُوا then offer bayna بَيْنَ before yaday يَدَيْ before najwākum نَجْوَاكُمْ your private consultation ṣadaqatan صَدَقَةً charity dhālika ذَلِكَ That khayrun خَيْرٌ (is) better lakum لَكُمْ for you wa-aṭharu وَأَطْهَرُ and purer fa-in فَإِنْ But if lam لَمْ not tajidū تَجِدُوا you find fa-inna فَإِنَّ then indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ghafūrun غَفُورٌ (is) Oft-Forgiving raḥīmun رَحِيمٌ Most Merciful

12

12.

O you who have believed, when you [wish to] privately consult the Messenger, present before your consultation a charity. That is better for you and purer. But if you find not [the means] - then indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

12

a-ashfaqtum ءَأَشْفَقْتُمْ Are you afraid an أَنْ to tuqaddimū تُقَدِّمُوا offer bayna بَيْنَ before yaday يَدَيْ before najwākum نَجْوَاكُمْ your private consultation ṣadaqātin صَدَقَاتٍ charities fa-idh فَإِذْ Then when lam لَمْ you do not tafʿalū تَفْعَلُوا you do not watāba وَتَابَ and Allah has forgiven l-lahu اللَّهُ and Allah has forgiven ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ you fa-aqīmū فَأَقِيمُوا then establish l-ṣalata الصَّلَوةَ the prayer waātū وَءَاتُوا and give l-zakata الزَّكَوةَ the zakah wa-aṭīʿū وَأَطِيعُوا and obey l-laha اللَّهَ Allah warasūlahu وَرَسُولَهُ and His Messenger wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah khabīrun خَبِيرٌ (is) All-Aware bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do

13

13.

Have you feared to present before your consultation charities? Then when you do not and Allah has forgiven you, then [at least] establish prayer and give zakah and obey Allah and His Messenger. And Allah is Acquainted with what you do.

13

You will not find a people who believe in Allah and the Last Day having affection for those who oppose Allah and His Messenger, even if they were their fathers or their sons or their brothers or their kindred.
