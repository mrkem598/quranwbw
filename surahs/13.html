alif-Lam-meem-ra الٓمٓر Alif Laam Mim Ra til'ka تِلْكَ These āyātu ءَايَاتُ (are) the Verses l-kitābi الْكِتَابِ (of) the Book wa-alladhī وَالَّذِي And that which unzila أُنْزِلَ has been revealed ilayka إِلَيْكَ to you min مِنْ from rabbika رَبِّكَ your Lord l-ḥaqu الْحَقُّ (is) the truth walākinna وَلَكِنَّ but akthara أَكْثَرَ most l-nāsi النَّاسِ (of) the mankind lā لَا (do) not yu'minūna يُؤْمِنُونَ believe

1

1.

Alif, Lam, Meem, Ra. These are the verses of the Book; and what has been revealed to you from your Lord is the truth, but most of the people do not believe.

1

al-lahu اللَّهُ Allah alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who rafaʿa رَفَعَ raised l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens bighayri بِغَيْرِ without ʿamadin عَمَدٍ pillars tarawnahā تَرَوْنَهَا that you see thumma ثُمَّ then is'tawā اسْتَوَى He established ʿalā عَلَى on l-ʿarshi الْعَرْشِ the Throne wasakhara وَسَخَّرَ and subjected l-shamsa الشَّمْسَ the sun wal-qamara وَالْقَمَرَ and the moon kullun كُلٌّ each yajrī يَجْرِي running li-ajalin لِأَجَلٍ for a term musamman مُسَمًّى appointed yudabbiru يُدَبِّرُ He arranges l-amra الْأَمْرَ the matter yufaṣṣilu يُفَصِّلُ He details l-āyāti الْءَايَاتِ the Signs laʿallakum لَعَلَّكُمْ so that you may biliqāi بِلِقَاءِ in the meeting rabbikum رَبِّكُمْ (with) your Lord tūqinūna تُوقِنُونَ believe with certainty

2

2.

It is Allah who erected the heavens without pillars that you [can] see; then He established Himself above the Throne and made subject the sun and the moon, each running [its course] for a specified term. He arranges [each] matter; He details the signs that you may, of the meeting with your Lord, be certain.

2

wahuwa وَهُوَ And He alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who madda مَدَّ spread l-arḍa الْأَرْضَ the earth wajaʿala وَجَعَلَ and placed fīhā فِيهَا in it rawāsiya رَوَاسِيَ firm mountains wa-anhāran وَأَنْهَارًا and rivers wamin وَمِنْ and from kulli كُلِّ all l-thamarāti الثَّمَرَاتِ (of) the fruits jaʿala جَعَلَ He made fīhā فِيهَا in it zawjayni زَوْجَيْنِ pairs ith'nayni اثْنَيْنِ two yugh'shī يُغْشِي He covers al-layla اللَّيْلَ the night l-nahāra النَّهَارَ (with) the day inna إِنَّ Indeed fī فِي in dhālika ذَلِكَ that laāyātin لَءَايَاتٍ surely (are) Signs liqawmin لِقَوْمٍ for a people yatafakkarūna يَتَفَكَّرُونَ who ponder

3

3.

And it is He who spread the earth and placed therein firmly set mountains and rivers; and from all of the fruits He made therein two mates; He causes the night to cover the day. Indeed in that are signs for a people who give thought.

3

wafī وَفِي And in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth qiṭaʿun قِطَعٌ are tracks mutajāwirātun مُتَجَاوِرَاتٌ neighboring wajannātun وَجَنَّاتٌ and gardens min مِنْ of aʿnābin أَعْنَابٍ grapevines wazarʿun وَزَرْعٌ and crops wanakhīlun وَنَخِيلٌ and date-palms ṣin'wānun صِنْوَانٌ trees (growing) from a single root waghayru وَغَيْرُ and not ṣin'wānin صِنْوَانٍ trees (growing) from a single root yus'qā يُسْقَى watered bimāin بِمَاءٍ with water wāḥidin وَاحِدٍ one wanufaḍḍilu وَنُفَضِّلُ but We cause to exceed baʿḍahā بَعْضَهَا some of them ʿalā عَلَى over baʿḍin بَعْضٍ others fī فِي in l-ukuli الْأُكُلِ the fruit inna إِنَّ Indeed fī فِي in dhālika ذَلِكَ that laāyātin لَءَايَاتٍ surely (are) Signs liqawmin لِقَوْمٍ for a people yaʿqilūna يَعْقِلُونَ who use reason

4

4.

And within the land are neighboring plots and gardens of grapevines and crops and palm trees, [growing] several from a root or otherwise, watered with one water; but We make some of them exceed others in [quality of] fruit. Indeed in that are signs for a people who reason.

4

wa-in وَإِنْ And if taʿjab تَعْجَبْ you (are) astonished faʿajabun فَعَجَبٌ then astonishing qawluhum قَوْلُهُمْ (is) their saying a-idhā أَءِذَا When kunnā كُنَّا we are turāban تُرَابًا dust a-innā أَءِنَّا will we lafī لَفِي (be) indeed in khalqin خَلْقٍ a creation jadīdin جَدِيدٍ new ulāika أُولَئِكَ Those alladhīna الَّذِينَ (are) the ones who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved birabbihim بِرَبِّهِمْ in their Lord wa-ulāika وَأُولَئِكَ and those l-aghlālu الْأَغْلَالُ the iron chains fī فِي (will be) in aʿnāqihim أَعْنَاقِهِمْ their necks wa-ulāika وَأُولَئِكَ those aṣḥābu أَصْحَابُ (are the) companions l-nāri النَّارِ (of) the Fire hum هُمْ they fīhā فِيهَا in it khālidūna خَالِدُونَ (will) abide forever

5

5.

And if you are astonished, [O Muhammad] - then astonishing is their saying, "When we are dust, will we indeed be [brought] into a new creation?" Those are the ones who have disbelieved in their Lord, and those will have shackles upon their necks, and those are the companions of the Fire; they will abide therein eternally.

5

wayastaʿjilūnaka وَيَسْتَعْجِلُونَكَ And they ask you to hasten bil-sayi-ati بِالسَّيِّئَةِ the evil qabla قَبْلَ before l-ḥasanati الْحَسَنَةِ the good waqad وَقَدْ and verily khalat خَلَتْ has occurred min مِنْ from qablihimu قَبْلِهِمُ before them l-mathulātu الْمَثُلَاتُ [the] similar punishments wa-inna وَإِنَّ And indeed rabbaka رَبَّكَ your Lord ladhū لَذُو (is) full maghfiratin مَغْفِرَةٍ (of) forgiveness lilnnāsi لِلنَّاسِ for mankind ʿalā عَلَى for ẓul'mihim ظُلْمِهِمْ their wrongdoing wa-inna وَإِنَّ and indeed rabbaka رَبَّكَ your Lord lashadīdu لَشَدِيدُ (is) severe l-ʿiqābi الْعِقَابِ (in) the penalty

6

6.

They impatiently urge you to bring about evil before good, while there has already occurred before them similar punishments [to what they demand]. And indeed, your Lord is full of forgiveness for the people despite their wrongdoing, and indeed, your Lord is severe in penalty.

6

wayaqūlu وَيَقُولُ And say alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved lawlā لَوْلَا Why not unzila أُنْزِلَ has been sent down ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ to him āyatun ءَايَةٌ a sign min مِنْ from rabbihi رَبِّهِ his Lord innamā إِنَّمَا Only anta أَنْتَ you mundhirun مُنْذِرٌ (are) a warner walikulli وَلِكُلِّ and for every qawmin قَوْمٍ people hādin هَادٍ (is) a guide

7

7.

And those who disbelieved say, "Why has a sign not been sent down to him from his Lord?" You are only a warner, and for every people is a guide.

7

al-lahu اللَّهُ Allah yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ knows mā مَا what taḥmilu تَحْمِلُ carries kullu كُلُّ every unthā أُنْثَى female wamā وَمَا and what taghīḍu تَغِيضُ fall short l-arḥāmu الْأَرْحَامُ the womb wamā وَمَا and what tazdādu تَزْدَادُ they exceed wakullu وَكُلُّ And every shayin شَيْءٍ thing ʿindahu عِنْدَهُ with Him bimiq'dārin بِمِقْدَارٍ (is) in due proportion

8

8.

Allah knows what every female carries and what the wombs lose [prematurely] or exceed. And everything with Him is by due measure.

8

ʿālimu عَالِمُ Knower l-ghaybi الْغَيْبِ (of) the unseen wal-shahādati وَالشَّهَادَةِ and the witnessed l-kabīru الْكَبِيرُ the Most Great l-mutaʿāli الْمُتَعَالِ the Most High

9

9.

[He is] Knower of the unseen and the witnessed, the Grand, the Exalted.

9

sawāon سَوَاءٌ (It is) same (to Him) minkum مِنْكُمْ [of you] man مَنْ (one) who asarra أَسَرَّ conceals l-qawla الْقَوْلَ the speech waman وَمَنْ or (one) who jahara جَهَرَ publicizes bihi بِهِ it waman وَمَنْ and (one) who huwa هُوَ [he] mus'takhfin مُسْتَخْفٍ (is) hidden bi-al-layli بِاللَّيْلِ by night wasāribun وَسَارِبٌ or goes freely bil-nahāri بِالنَّهَارِ by day

10

10.

It is the same [to Him] concerning you whether one conceals [his] speech or one publicizes it and whether one is hidden by night or conspicuous [among others] by day.

10

lahu لَهُ For him muʿaqqibātun مُعَقِّبَاتٌ (are) successive (Angels) min مِنْ from bayni بَيْنِ (before) yadayhi يَدَيْهِ (him) wamin وَمِنْ and from khalfihi خَلْفِهِ and behind him yaḥfaẓūnahu يَحْفَظُونَهُ who guard him min مِنْ by amri أَمْرِ (the) command l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah lā لَا (does) not yughayyiru يُغَيِّرُ change mā مَا (the condition) biqawmin بِقَوْمٍ of a people ḥattā حَتَّى until yughayyirū يُغَيِّرُوا they change mā مَا what bi-anfusihim بِأَنْفُسِهِمْ (is) in themselves wa-idhā وَإِذَا And when arāda أَرَادَ wills l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah biqawmin بِقَوْمٍ for a people sūan سُوءًا misfortune falā فَلَا then (there is) no maradda مَرَدَّ turning away lahu لَهُ of it wamā وَمَا and not lahum لَهُمْ for them min مِنْ from dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him min مِنْ any wālin وَالٍ protector

11

11.

For each one are successive [angels] before and behind him who protect him by the decree of Allah. Indeed, Allah will not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves. And when Allah intends for a people ill, there is no repelling it. And there is not for them besides Him any patron.

11

huwa هُوَ He alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who yurīkumu يُرِيكُمُ shows you l-barqa الْبَرْقَ the lightning khawfan خَوْفًا a fear waṭamaʿan وَطَمَعًا and a hope wayunshi-u وَيُنْشِئُ and brings up l-saḥāba السَّحَابَ the clouds l-thiqāla الثِّقَالَ the heavy

12

12.

It is He who shows you lightening, [causing] fear and aspiration, and generates the heavy clouds.

12

wayusabbiḥu وَيُسَبِّحُ And glorifies l-raʿdu الرَّعْدُ the thunder biḥamdihi بِحَمْدِهِ [with] His praise wal-malāikatu وَالْمَلَائِكَةُ and the Angels min مِنْ for khīfatihi خِيفَتِهِ fear of Him wayur'silu وَيُرْسِلُ And He sends l-ṣawāʿiqa الصَّوَاعِقَ the thunderbolts fayuṣību فَيُصِيبُ and strikes bihā بِهَا with it man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills wahum وَهُمْ yet they yujādilūna يُجَادِلُونَ dispute fī فِي about l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah wahuwa وَهُوَ And He shadīdu شَدِيدُ (is) Mighty l-miḥāli الْمِحَالِ (in) Strength

13

13.

And the thunder exalts [Allah] with praise of Him - and the angels [as well] from fear of Him - and He sends thunderbolts and strikes therewith whom He wills while they dispute about Allah; and He is severe in assault.

13

lahu لَهُ To Him daʿwatu دَعْوَةُ (is) supplication l-ḥaqi الْحَقِّ (of) the truth wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those whom yadʿūna يَدْعُونَ they invoke min مِنْ besides Him dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him lā لَا not yastajībūna يَسْتَجِيبُونَ they respond lahum لَهُمْ to them bishayin بِشَيْءٍ with a thing illā إِلَّا except kabāsiṭi كَبَاسِطِ like one who stretches kaffayhi كَفَّيْهِ his hands ilā إِلَى towards l-māi الْمَاءِ water liyablugha لِيَبْلُغَ to reach fāhu فَاهُ his mouth wamā وَمَا but not huwa هُوَ it bibālighihi بِبَالِغِهِ reaches it wamā وَمَا And not duʿāu دُعَاءُ (is) the supplication l-kāfirīna الْكَافِرِينَ (of) the disbelievers illā إِلَّا but fī فِي in ḍalālin ضَلَالٍ error

14

14.

To Him [alone] is the supplication of truth. And those they call upon besides Him do not respond to them with a thing, except as one who stretches his hands toward water [from afar, calling it] to reach his mouth, but it will not reach it [thus]. And the supplication of the disbelievers is not but in error [i.e. futility].

14

walillahi وَلِلَّهِ And to Allah yasjudu يَسْجُدُ prostrates man مَنْ whoever fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth ṭawʿan طَوْعًا willingly wakarhan وَكَرْهًا or unwillingly waẓilāluhum وَظِلَالُهُمْ and (so do) their shadows bil-ghuduwi بِالْغُدُوِّ in the mornings wal-āṣāli وَالْءَاصَالِ and in the afternoons

15

15.

And to Allah prostrates whoever is within the heavens and the earth, willingly or by compulsion, and their shadows [as well] in the mornings and the afternoons.

15

qul قُلْ Say man مَنْ Who rabbu رَبُّ (is) the Lord l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth quli قُلِ Say l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah qul قُلْ Say afa-ittakhadhtum أَفَاتَّخَذْتُمْ Have you then taken min مِنْ from dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him awliyāa أَوْلِيَاءَ protectors lā لَا not yamlikūna يَمْلِكُونَ they have power li-anfusihim لِأَنْفُسِهِمْ for themselves nafʿan نَفْعًا (to) benefit walā وَلَا and not ḍarran ضَرًّا (to) harm qul قُلْ Say hal هَلْ Is yastawī يَسْتَوِي equal l-aʿmā الْأَعْمَى the blind wal-baṣīru وَالْبَصِيرُ and the seeing am أَمْ Or hal هَلْ is tastawī تَسْتَوِي equal l-ẓulumātu الظُّلُمَاتُ the darkness[es] wal-nūru وَالنُّورُ and the light am أَمْ Or jaʿalū جَعَلُوا they attribute lillahi لِلَّهِ to Allah shurakāa شُرَكَاءَ partners khalaqū خَلَقُوا who created kakhalqihi كَخَلْقِهِ like His creation fatashābaha فَتَشَابَهَ so that seemed alike l-khalqu الْخَلْقُ the creation ʿalayhim عَلَيْهِمْ to them quli قُلِ Say l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah khāliqu خَالِقُ (is) the Creator kulli كُلِّ of all shayin شَيْءٍ things wahuwa وَهُوَ and He l-wāḥidu الْوَاحِدُ (is) the One l-qahāru الْقَهَّارُ the Irresistible

16

16.

Say, "Who is Lord of the heavens and earth?" Say, "Allah." Say, "Have you then taken besides Him allies not possessing [even] for themselves any benefit or any harm?" Say, "Is the blind equivalent to the seeing? Or is darkness equivalent to light? Or have they attributed to Allah partners who created like His creation so that the creation [of each] seemed similar to them?" Say, "Allah is the Creator of all things, and He is the One, the Prevailing."
