sūratun سُورَةٌ A Surah anzalnāhā أَنْزَلْنَاهَا We (have) sent it down wafaraḍnāhā وَفَرَضْنَاهَا and We (have) made it obligatory wa-anzalnā وَأَنْزَلْنَا and We (have) revealed fīhā فِيهَا therein āyātin ءَايَاتٍ Verses bayyinātin بَيِّنَاتٍ clear laʿallakum لَعَلَّكُمْ so that you may tadhakkarūna تَذَكَّرُونَ take heed

1

1.

[This is] a surah which We have sent down and made [that within it] obligatory and revealed therein verses of clear evidence that you might remember.

1

al-zāniyatu الزَّانِيَةُ The fornicatress wal-zānī وَالزَّانِي and the fornicator fa-ij'lidū فَاجْلِدُوا [then] flog kulla كُلَّ each wāḥidin وَاحِدٍ one min'humā مِنْهُمَا of them mi-ata مِائَةَ (with) hundred jaldatin جَلْدَةٍ lash(es) walā وَلَا And (let) not takhudh'kum تَأْخُذْكُمْ withhold you bihimā بِهِمَا pity for them rafatun رَأْفَةٌ pity for them fī فِي concerning dīni دِينِ (the) religion l-lahi اللَّهِ of Allah in إِنْ if kuntum كُنْتُمْ you tu'minūna تُؤْمِنُونَ believe bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah wal-yawmi وَالْيَوْمِ and the Day l-ākhiri الْءَاخِرِ the Last walyashhad وَلْيَشْهَدْ And let witness ʿadhābahumā عَذَابَهُمَا their punishment ṭāifatun طَائِفَةٌ a group mina مِنَ of l-mu'minīna الْمُؤْمِنِينَ the believers

2

2.

The [unmarried] woman or [unmarried] man found guilty of sexual intercourse - lash each one of them with a hundred lashes, and do not be taken by pity for them in the religion of Allah, if you should believe in Allah and the Last Day. And let a group of the believers witness their punishment.

2

al-zānī الزَّانِي The fornicator lā لَا (will) not yankiḥu يَنْكِحُ marry illā إِلَّا except zāniyatan زَانِيَةً a fornicatress aw أَوْ or mush'rikatan مُشْرِكَةً a polytheist woman wal-zāniyatu وَالزَّانِيَةُ and the fornicatress lā لَا (will) not yankiḥuhā يَنْكِحُهَا marry her illā إِلَّا except zānin زَانٍ a fornicator aw أَوْ or mush'rikun مُشْرِكٌ a polytheist man waḥurrima وَحُرِّمَ And is forbidden dhālika ذَلِكَ that ʿalā عَلَى to l-mu'minīna الْمُؤْمِنِينَ the believers

3

3.

The fornicator does not marry except a [female] fornicator or polytheist, and none marries her except a fornicator or a polytheist, and that has been made unlawful to the believers.

3

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those who yarmūna يَرْمُونَ accuse l-muḥ'ṣanāti الْمُحْصَنَاتِ the chaste women thumma ثُمَّ then lam لَمْ not yatū يَأْتُوا they bring bi-arbaʿati بِأَرْبَعَةِ four shuhadāa شُهَدَاءَ witnesses fa-ij'lidūhum فَاجْلِدُوهُمْ then flog them thamānīna ثَمَانِينَ (with) eighty jaldatan جَلْدَةً lashe(s) walā وَلَا and (do) not taqbalū تَقْبَلُوا accept lahum لَهُمْ their shahādatan شَهَادَةً testimony abadan أَبَدًا ever wa-ulāika وَأُولَئِكَ And those humu هُمُ they l-fāsiqūna الْفَاسِقُونَ (are) the defiantly disobedient

4

4.

And those who accuse chaste women and then do not produce four witnesses - lash them with eighty lashes and do not accept from them testimony ever after. And those are the defiantly disobedient,

4

illā إِلَّا Except alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who tābū تَابُوا repent min مِنْ after baʿdi بَعْدِ after dhālika ذَلِكَ that wa-aṣlaḥū وَأَصْلَحُوا and reform fa-inna فَإِنَّ Then indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ghafūrun غَفُورٌ (is) Oft-Forgiving raḥīmun رَحِيمٌ Most Merciful

5

5.

Except for those who repent thereafter and reform, for indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

5

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those who yarmūna يَرْمُونَ accuse azwājahum أَزْوَاجَهُمْ their spouses walam وَلَمْ and not yakun يَكُنْ have lahum لَهُمْ for them shuhadāu شُهَدَاءُ witnesses illā إِلَّا except anfusuhum أَنْفُسُهُمْ themselves fashahādatu فَشَهَادَةُ then (the) testimony aḥadihim أَحَدِهِمْ (of) one of them arbaʿu أَرْبَعُ (is) four shahādātin شَهَادَاتٍ testimonies bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ by Allah innahu إِنَّهُ that he lamina لَمِنَ (is) surely of l-ṣādiqīna الصَّادِقِينَ the truthful

6

6.

And those who accuse their wives [of adultery] and have no witnesses except themselves - then the witness of one of them [shall be] four testimonies [swearing] by Allah that indeed, he is of the truthful.

6

wal-khāmisatu وَالْخَامِسَةُ And the fifth anna أَنَّ that laʿnata لَعْنَةَ (the) curse of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (the) curse of Allah ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ (be) upon him in إِنْ if kāna كَانَ he is mina مِنَ of l-kādhibīna الْكَاذِبِينَ the liars

7

7.

And the fifth [oath will be] that the curse of Allah be upon him if he should be among the liars.

7

wayadra-u وَيَدْرَؤُا But it would prevent ʿanhā عَنْهَا from her l-ʿadhāba الْعَذَابَ the punishment an أَنْ that tashhada تَشْهَدَ she bears witness arbaʿa أَرْبَعَ four shahādātin شَهَادَاتٍ testimonies bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ by Allah innahu إِنَّهُ that he lamina لَمِنَ (is) surely of l-kādhibīna الْكَاذِبِينَ the liars

8

8.

But it will prevent punishment from her if she gives four testimonies [swearing] by Allah that indeed, he is of the liars.

8

wal-khāmisata وَالْخَامِسَةَ And the fifth anna أَنَّ that ghaḍaba غَضَبَ the wrath of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ the wrath of Allah ʿalayhā عَلَيْهَا (be) upon her in إِنْ if kāna كَانَ he is mina مِنَ of l-ṣādiqīna الصَّادِقِينَ the truthful

9

9.

And the fifth [oath will be] that the wrath of Allah be upon her if he was of the truthful.

9

walawlā وَلَوْلَا And if not faḍlu فَضْلُ (for) the Grace of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (for) the Grace of Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you waraḥmatuhu وَرَحْمَتُهُ and His Mercy wa-anna وَأَنَّ and that l-laha اللَّهَ Allah tawwābun تَوَّابٌ (is) Oft-Returning (to Mercy) ḥakīmun حَكِيمٌ All-Wise

10

10.

And if not for the favor of Allah upon you and His mercy... and because Allah is Accepting of repentance and Wise.

10

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who jāū جَاءُو brought bil-if'ki بِالْإِفْكِ the lie ʿuṣ'batun عُصْبَةٌ (are) a group minkum مِنْكُمْ among you lā لَا (Do) not taḥsabūhu تَحْسَبُوهُ think it sharran شَرًّا bad lakum لَكُمْ for you bal بَلْ nay huwa هُوَ it khayrun خَيْرٌ (is) good lakum لَكُمْ for you likulli لِكُلِّ For every im'ri-in امْرِئٍ person min'hum مِنْهُمْ among them mā مَا (is) what ik'tasaba اكْتَسَبَ he earned mina مِنَ of l-ith'mi الْإِثْمِ the sin wa-alladhī وَالَّذِي and the one who tawallā تَوَلَّى took upon himself a greater share of it kib'rahu كِبْرَهُ took upon himself a greater share of it min'hum مِنْهُمْ among them lahu لَهُ for him ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

11

11.

Indeed, those who came with falsehood are a group among you. Do not think it bad for you; rather it is good for you. For every person among them is what [punishment] he has earned from the sin, and he who took upon himself the greater portion thereof - for him is a great punishment.

11

lawlā لَوْلَا Why not idh إِذْ when samiʿ'tumūhu سَمِعْتُمُوهُ you heard it ẓanna ظَنَّ think l-mu'minūna الْمُؤْمِنُونَ the believing men wal-mu'minātu وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتُ and the believing women bi-anfusihim بِأَنْفُسِهِمْ good of themselves khayran خَيْرًا good of themselves waqālū وَقَالُوا and say hādhā هَذَا This if'kun إِفْكٌ (is) a lie mubīnun مُبِينٌ clear

12

12.

Why, when you heard it, did not the believing men and believing women think good of one another and say, "This is an obvious falsehood"?

12

lawlā لَوْلَا Why (did) not jāū جَاءُو they bring ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ for it bi-arbaʿati بِأَرْبَعَةِ four shuhadāa شُهَدَاءَ witnesses fa-idh فَإِذْ Then when lam لَمْ not yatū يَأْتُوا they brought bil-shuhadāi بِالشُّهَدَاءِ the witnesses fa-ulāika فَأُولَئِكَ then those ʿinda عِنْدَ near Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ near Allah humu هُمُ they l-kādhibūna الْكَاذِبُونَ (are) the liars

13

13.

Why did they [who slandered] not produce for it four witnesses? And when they do not produce the witnesses, then it is they, in the sight of Allah, who are the liars.

13

walawlā وَلَوْلَا And if not faḍlu فَضْلُ (for the) Grace l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you waraḥmatuhu وَرَحْمَتُهُ and His Mercy fī فِي in l-dun'yā الدُّنْيَا the world wal-ākhirati وَالْءَاخِرَةِ and the Hereafter lamassakum لَمَسَّكُمْ surely would have touched you fī فِي in mā مَا what afaḍtum أَفَضْتُمْ you had rushed glibly fīhi فِيهِ concerning it ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ a punishment ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

14

14.

And if it had not been for the favor of Allah upon you and His mercy in this world and the Hereafter, you would have been touched for that [lie] in which you were involved by a great punishment

14

idh إِذْ When talaqqawnahu تَلَقَّوْنَهُ you received it bi-alsinatikum بِأَلْسِنَتِكُمْ with your tongues wataqūlūna وَتَقُولُونَ and you said bi-afwāhikum بِأَفْوَاهِكُمْ with your mouths mā مَا what laysa لَيْسَ not lakum لَكُمْ for you bihi بِهِ of it ʿil'mun عِلْمٌ any knowledge wataḥsabūnahu وَتَحْسَبُونَهُ and you thought it hayyinan هَيِّنًا (was) insignificant wahuwa وَهُوَ while it ʿinda عِنْدَ (was) near Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (was) near Allah ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

15

15.

When you received it with your tongues and said with your mouths that of which you had no knowledge and thought it was insignificant while it was, in the sight of Allah, tremendous.

15

walawlā وَلَوْلَا And why not idh إِذْ when samiʿ'tumūhu سَمِعْتُمُوهُ you heard it qul'tum قُلْتُمْ you said mā مَا Not yakūnu يَكُونُ it is lanā لَنَا for us an أَنْ that natakallama نَتَكَلَّمَ we speak bihādhā بِهَذَا of this sub'ḥānaka سُبْحَانَكَ Glory be to You! hādhā هَذَا This buh'tānun بُهْتَانٌ (is) a slander ʿaẓīmun عَظِيمٌ great

16

16.

And why, when you heard it, did you not say, "It is not for us to speak of this. Exalted are You, [O Allah]; this is a great slander"?

16

yaʿiẓukumu يَعِظُكُمَ Allah warns you l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah warns you an أَنْ that taʿūdū تَعُودُوا you return limith'lihi لِمِثْلِهِ (to the) like of it abadan أَبَدًا ever in إِنْ if kuntum كُنْتُمْ you are mu'minīna مُؤْمِنِينَ believers

17

17.

Allah warns you against returning to the likes of this [conduct], ever, if you should be believers.

17

wayubayyinu وَيُبَيِّنُ And Allah makes clear l-lahu اللَّهُ And Allah makes clear lakumu لَكُمُ to you l-āyāti الْءَايَاتِ the Verses wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knower ḥakīmun حَكِيمٌ All-Wise

18

18.

And Allah makes clear to you the verses, and Allah is Knowing and Wise.

18

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who yuḥibbūna يُحِبُّونَ like an أَنْ that tashīʿa تَشِيعَ (should) spread l-fāḥishatu الْفَاحِشَةُ the immorality fī فِي among alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe lahum لَهُمْ for them ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment alīmun أَلِيمٌ painful fī فِي in l-dun'yā الدُّنْيَا the world wal-ākhirati وَالْءَاخِرَةِ and the Hereafter wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ knows wa-antum وَأَنْتُمْ while you lā لَا (do) not taʿlamūna تَعْلَمُونَ know

19

19.

Indeed, those who like that immorality should be spread [or publicized] among those who have believed will have a painful punishment in this world and the Hereafter. And Allah knows and you do not know.

19

walawlā وَلَوْلَا And if not faḍlu فَضْلُ (for the) Grace of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (for the) Grace of Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you waraḥmatuhu وَرَحْمَتُهُ and His Mercy wa-anna وَأَنَّ And that l-laha اللَّهَ Allah raūfun رَءُوفٌ (is) Full of Kindness raḥīmun رَحِيمٌ Most Merciful

20

20.

And if it had not been for the favor of Allah upon you and His mercy... and because Allah is Kind and Merciful.

20

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O (you who) alladhīna الَّذِينَ (O) you who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe lā لَا (Do) not tattabiʿū تَتَّبِعُوا follow khuṭuwāti خُطُوَاتِ (the) footsteps l-shayṭāni الشَّيْطَانِ (of) the Shaitaan waman وَمَنْ and whoever yattabiʿ يَتَّبِعْ follows khuṭuwāti خُطُوَاتِ (the) footsteps l-shayṭāni الشَّيْطَانِ (of) the Shaitaan fa-innahu فَإِنَّهُ then indeed, he yamuru يَأْمُرُ commands bil-faḥshāi بِالْفَحْشَاءِ the immorality wal-munkari وَالْمُنْكَرِ and the evil walawlā وَلَوْلَا And if not faḍlu فَضْلُ (for the) Grace of Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ (for the) Grace of Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you waraḥmatuhu وَرَحْمَتُهُ and His Mercy mā مَا not zakā زَكَى (would) have been pure minkum مِنْكُمْ among you min مِنْ anyone aḥadin أَحَدٍ anyone abadan أَبَدًا ever walākinna وَلَكِنَّ but l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yuzakkī يُزَكِّي purifies man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah samīʿun سَمِيعٌ (is) All-Hearer ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ All-Knower

21

21.

O you who have believed, do not follow the footsteps of Satan. And whoever follows the footsteps of Satan - indeed, he enjoins immorality and wrongdoing.
