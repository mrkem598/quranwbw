lam لَمْ Not yakuni يَكُنِ were alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved min مِنْ from ahli أَهْلِ (the) People l-kitābi الْكِتَابِ of the Book wal-mush'rikīna وَالْمُشْرِكِينَ and the polytheists munfakkīna مُنْفَكِّينَ to be abandoned ḥattā حَتَّى until tatiyahumu تَأْتِيَهُمُ (there) comes to them l-bayinatu الْبَيِّنَةُ the clear evidence

1

1.

Those who disbelieved among the People of the Scripture and the polytheists were not to be parted [from misbelief] until there came to them clear evidence -

1

rasūlun رَسُولٌ A Messenger mina مِنَ from l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah yatlū يَتْلُوا reciting ṣuḥufan صُحُفًا pages muṭahharatan مُطَهَّرَةً purified

2

2.

A Messenger from Allah, reciting purified scriptures

2

fīhā فِيهَا Wherein kutubun كُتُبٌ (are) writings qayyimatun قَيِّمَةٌ correct

3

3.

Within which are correct writings.

3

wamā وَمَا And not tafarraqa تَفَرَّقَ became divided alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who ūtū أُوتُوا were given l-kitāba الْكِتَابَ the Book illā إِلَّا until min مِنْ from baʿdi بَعْدِ after mā مَا what jāathumu جَاءَتْهُمُ came (to) them l-bayinatu الْبَيِّنَةُ (of) the clear evidence

4

4.

Nor did those who were given the Scripture become divided until after there had come to them clear evidence.

4

wamā وَمَا And not umirū أُمِرُوا they were commanded illā إِلَّا except liyaʿbudū لِيَعْبُدُوا to worship l-laha اللَّهَ Allah mukh'liṣīna مُخْلِصِينَ (being) sincere lahu لَهُ to Him l-dīna الدِّينَ (in) the religion ḥunafāa حُنَفَاءَ upright wayuqīmū وَيُقِيمُوا and to establish l-ṣalata الصَّلَوةَ the prayer wayu'tū وَيُؤْتُوا and to give l-zakata الزَّكَوةَ the Zakah wadhālika وَذَلِكَ And that dīnu دِينُ (is the) religion l-qayimati الْقَيِّمَةِ the correct

5

5.

And they were not commanded except to worship Allah, [being] sincere to Him in religion, inclining to truth, and to establish prayer and to give zakah. And that is the correct religion.

5

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve min مِنْ from ahli أَهْلِ (the) People l-kitābi الْكِتَابِ (of) the Book wal-mush'rikīna وَالْمُشْرِكِينَ and the polytheists fī فِي (will be) in nāri نَارِ (the) Fire jahannama جَهَنَّمَ (of) Hell khālidīna خَالِدِينَ abiding eternally fīhā فِيهَا therein ulāika أُولَئِكَ Those hum هُمْ they sharru شَرُّ (are the) worst l-bariyati الْبَرِيَّةِ (of) the creatures

6

6.

Indeed, they who disbelieved among the People of the Scripture and the polytheists will be in the fire of Hell, abiding eternally therein. Those are the worst of creatures.

6

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe waʿamilū وَعَمِلُوا and do l-ṣāliḥāti الصَّالِحَاتِ righteous deeds ulāika أُولَئِكَ those hum هُمْ they khayru خَيْرُ (are the) best l-bariyati الْبَرِيَّةِ (of) the creatures

7

7.

Indeed, they who have believed and done righteous deeds - those are the best of creatures.

7

jazāuhum جَزَاؤُهُمْ Their reward ʿinda عِنْدَ (is) with rabbihim رَبِّهِمْ their Lord jannātu جَنَّاتُ Gardens ʿadnin عَدْنٍ (of) Eternity tajrī تَجْرِي flow min مِنْ from taḥtihā تَحْتِهَا underneath them l-anhāru الْأَنْهَارُ the rivers khālidīna خَالِدِينَ will abide fīhā فِيهَا therein abadan أَبَدًا forever raḍiya رَضِيَ (will be) pleased l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah ʿanhum عَنْهُمْ with them waraḍū وَرَضُوا and they (will be) pleased ʿanhu عَنْهُ with Him dhālika ذَلِكَ That liman لِمَنْ (is) for whoever khashiya خَشِيَ feared rabbahu رَبَّهُ his Lord

8

8.

Their reward with Allah will be gardens of perpetual residence beneath which rivers flow, wherein they will abide forever, Allah being pleased with them and they with Him. That is for whoever has feared his Lord.

8
