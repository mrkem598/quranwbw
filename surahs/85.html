waL-samāi وَالسَّمَاءِ By the sky dhāti ذَاتِ containing l-burūji الْبُرُوجِ the constellations

1

1.

By the sky containing great stars

1

wal-yawmi وَالْيَوْمِ And the Day l-mawʿūdi الْمَوْعُودِ Promised

2

2.

And [by] the promised Day

2

washāhidin وَشَاهِدٍ And (the) witness wamashhūdin وَمَشْهُودٍ and what is witnessed

3

3.

And [by] the witness and what is witnessed,

3

qutila قُتِلَ Destroyed were aṣḥābu أَصْحَابُ (the) companions l-ukh'dūdi الْأُخْدُودِ (of) the pit

4

4.

Cursed were the companions of the trench

4

al-nāri النَّارِ (Of) the fire dhāti ذَاتِ full l-waqūdi الْوَقُودِ (of) the fuel

5

5.

[Containing] the fire full of fuel,

5

idh إِذْ When hum هُمْ they ʿalayhā عَلَيْهَا by it quʿūdun قُعُودٌ (were) sitting

6

6.

When they were sitting near it

6

wahum وَهُمْ And they ʿalā عَلَى over mā مَا what yafʿalūna يَفْعَلُونَ they were doing bil-mu'minīna بِالْمُؤْمِنِينَ to the believers shuhūdun شُهُودٌ witnesses

7

7.

And they, to what they were doing against the believers, were witnesses.

7

wamā وَمَا And not naqamū نَقَمُوا they resented min'hum مِنْهُمْ [of] them illā إِلَّا except an أَنْ that yu'minū يُؤْمِنُوا they believed bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah l-ʿazīzi الْعَزِيزِ the All-Mighty l-ḥamīdi الْحَمِيدِ the Praiseworthy

8

8.

And they resented them not except because they believed in Allah, the Exalted in Might, the Praiseworthy,

8

alladhī الَّذِي The One Who lahu لَهُ for Him mul'ku مُلْكُ (is) the dominion l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth; wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ and Allah ʿalā عَلَى on kulli كُلِّ every shayin شَيْءٍ thing shahīdun شَهِيدٌ (is) a Witness

9

9.

To whom belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth. And Allah, over all things, is Witness.

9

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who fatanū فَتَنُوا persecuted l-mu'minīna الْمُؤْمِنِينَ the believing men wal-mu'mināti وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ and the believing women thumma ثُمَّ then lam لَمْ not yatūbū يَتُوبُوا they repented falahum فَلَهُمْ then for them ʿadhābu عَذَابُ (is) the punishment jahannama جَهَنَّمَ (of) Hell walahum وَلَهُمْ and for them ʿadhābu عَذَابُ (is the) punishment l-ḥarīqi الْحَرِيقِ (of) the Burning Fire

10

10.

Indeed, those who have tortured the believing men and believing women and then have not repented will have the punishment of Hell, and they will have the punishment of the Burning Fire.

10

inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe waʿamilū وَعَمِلُوا and do l-ṣāliḥāti الصَّالِحَاتِ the righteous deeds lahum لَهُمْ for them jannātun جَنَّاتٌ (will be) Gardens tajrī تَجْرِي flow min مِنْ from taḥtihā تَحْتِهَا underneath it l-anhāru الْأَنْهَارُ the rivers dhālika ذَلِكَ That l-fawzu الْفَوْزُ (is) the success l-kabīru الْكَبِيرُ the great

11

11.

Indeed, those who have believed and done righteous deeds will have gardens beneath which rivers flow. That is the great attainment.

11

inna إِنَّ Indeed baṭsha بَطْشَ (the) Grip rabbika رَبِّكَ (of) your Lord lashadīdun لَشَدِيدٌ (is) surely strong

12

12.

Indeed, the vengeance of your Lord is severe.

12

innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed He huwa هُوَ He yub'di-u يُبْدِئُ originates wayuʿīdu وَيُعِيدُ and repeats

13

13.

Indeed, it is He who originates [creation] and repeats.

13

wahuwa وَهُوَ And He l-ghafūru الْغَفُورُ (is) the Oft-Forgiving l-wadūdu الْوَدُودُ the Most Loving

14

14.

And He is the Forgiving, the Affectionate,

14

dhū ذُو Owner (of) l-ʿarshi الْعَرْشِ the Throne l-majīdu الْمَجِيدُ the Glorious

15

15.

Honorable Owner of the Throne,

15

faʿʿālun فَعَّالٌ Doer limā لِمَا of what yurīdu يُرِيدُ He intends

16

16.

Effecter of what He intends.

16

hal هَلْ Has atāka أَتَىكَ come to you ḥadīthu حَدِيثُ (the) story l-junūdi الْجُنُودِ (of) the hosts

17

17.

Has there reached you the story of the soldiers -

17

fir'ʿawna فِرْعَوْنَ Firaun wathamūda وَثَمُودَ and Thamud

18

18.

[Those of] Pharaoh and Thamud?

18

bali بَلِ Nay! alladhīna الَّذِينَ Those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve fī فِي (are) in takdhībin تَكْذِيبٍ denial

19

19.

But they who disbelieve are in [persistent] denial,

19

wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ But Allah min مِنْ from warāihim وَرَائِهِمْ behind them muḥīṭun مُحِيطٌ encompasses

20

20.

While Allah encompasses them from behind.

20

bal بَلْ Nay! huwa هُوَ It qur'ānun قُرْءَانٌ (is) a Quran majīdun مَجِيدٌ Glorious

21

21.

But this is an honored Qur'an

21

fī فِي In lawḥin لَوْحٍ a Tablet maḥfūẓin مَحْفُوظٍ Guarded

[Inscribed] in a Preserved Slate.
