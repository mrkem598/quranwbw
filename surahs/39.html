tanzīlu تَنْزِيلُ (The) revelation l-kitābi الْكِتَابِ (of) the Book mina مِنَ (is) from l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah l-ʿazīzi الْعَزِيزِ the All-Mighty l-ḥakīmi الْحَكِيمِ the All-Wise

1

1.

The revelation of the Qur'an is from Allah, the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

1

innā إِنَّا Indeed We anzalnā أَنْزَلْنَا [We] have revealed ilayka إِلَيْكَ to you l-kitāba الْكِتَابَ the Book bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in truth fa-uʿ'budi فَاعْبُدِ so worship l-laha اللَّهَ Allah mukh'liṣan مُخْلِصًا (being) sincere lahu لَهُ to Him l-dīna الدِّينَ (in) the religion

2

2.

Indeed, We have sent down to you the Book, [O Muhammad], in truth. So worship Allah, [being] sincere to Him in religion.

2

alā أَلَا Unquestionably lillahi لِلَّهِ for Allah l-dīnu الدِّينُ (is) the religion l-khāliṣu الْخَالِصُ the pure wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those who ittakhadhū اتَّخَذُوا take min مِنْ besides Him dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him awliyāa أَوْلِيَاءَ protectors mā مَا Not naʿbuduhum نَعْبُدُهُمْ we worship them illā إِلَّا except liyuqarribūnā لِيُقَرِّبُونَا that they may bring us near ilā إِلَى to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah zul'fā زُلْفَى (in) position inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yaḥkumu يَحْكُمُ will judge baynahum بَيْنَهُمْ between them fī فِي in mā مَا what hum هُمْ they fīhi فِيهِ [in it] yakhtalifūna يَخْتَلِفُونَ differ inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah lā لَا (does) not yahdī يَهْدِي guide man مَنْ (one) who huwa هُوَ [he] kādhibun كَاذِبٌ (is) a liar kaffārun كَفَّارٌ and a disbeliever

3

3.

Unquestionably, for Allah is the pure religion. And those who take protectors besides Him [say], "We only worship them that they may bring us nearer to Allah in position." Indeed, Allah will judge between them concerning that over which they differ. Indeed, Allah does not guide he who is a liar and [confirmed] disbeliever.

3

law لَوْ If arāda أَرَادَ Allah (had) intended l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah (had) intended an أَنْ to yattakhidha يَتَّخِذَ take waladan وَلَدًا a son la-iṣ'ṭafā لَاصْطَفَى surely, He (could) have chosen mimmā مِمَّا from what yakhluqu يَخْلُقُ He creates mā مَا whatever yashāu يَشَاءُ He willed sub'ḥānahu سُبْحَانَهُ Glory be to Him huwa هُوَ He l-lahu اللَّهُ (is) Allah l-wāḥidu الْوَاحِدُ the One l-qahāru الْقَهَّارُ the Irresistible

4

4.

If Allah had intended to take a son, He could have chosen from what He creates whatever He willed. Exalted is He; He is Allah, the One, the Prevailing.

4

khalaqa خَلَقَ He created l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in [the] truth yukawwiru يُكَوِّرُ He wraps al-layla اللَّيْلَ the night ʿalā عَلَى over l-nahāri النَّهَارِ the day wayukawwiru وَيُكَوِّرُ and wraps l-nahāra النَّهَارَ the day ʿalā عَلَى over al-layli اللَّيْلِ the night wasakhara وَسَخَّرَ And He subjected l-shamsa الشَّمْسَ the sun wal-qamara وَالْقَمَرَ and the moon kullun كُلٌّ each yajrī يَجْرِي running li-ajalin لِأَجَلٍ for a term musamman مُسَمًّى specified alā أَلَا Unquestionably huwa هُوَ He l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ (is) the All-Mighty l-ghafāru الْغَفَّارُ the Oft-Forgiving

5

5.

He created the heavens and earth in truth. He wraps the night over the day and wraps the day over the night and has subjected the sun and the moon, each running [its course] for a specified term. Unquestionably, He is the Exalted in Might, the Perpetual Forgiver.

5

khalaqakum خَلَقَكُمْ He created you min مِنْ from nafsin نَفْسٍ a soul wāḥidatin وَاحِدَةٍ single thumma ثُمَّ Then jaʿala جَعَلَ He made min'hā مِنْهَا from it zawjahā زَوْجَهَا its mate wa-anzala وَأَنْزَلَ And He sent down lakum لَكُمْ for you mina مِنَ of l-anʿāmi الْأَنْعَامِ the cattle thamāniyata ثَمَانِيَةَ eight azwājin أَزْوَاجٍ kinds yakhluqukum يَخْلُقُكُمْ He creates you fī فِي in buṭūni بُطُونِ (the) wombs ummahātikum أُمَّهَاتِكُمْ (of) your mothers khalqan خَلْقًا creation min مِنْ after baʿdi بَعْدِ after khalqin خَلْقٍ creation fī فِي in ẓulumātin ظُلُمَاتٍ darkness[es] thalāthin ثَلَاثٍ three dhālikumu ذَلِكُمُ That l-lahu اللَّهُ (is) Allah rabbukum رَبُّكُمْ your Lord lahu لَهُ for Him l-mul'ku الْمُلْكُ (is) the dominion lā لَا (There is) no ilāha إِلَهَ god illā إِلَّا except huwa هُوَ He fa-annā فَأَنَّى Then how tuṣ'rafūna تُصْرَفُونَ are you turning away

6

6.

He created you from one soul. Then He made from it its mate, and He produced for you from the grazing livestock eight mates. He creates you in the wombs of your mothers, creation after creation, within three darknesses. That is Allah, your Lord; to Him belongs dominion. There is no deity except Him, so how are you averted?

6

in إِنْ If takfurū تَكْفُرُوا you disbelieve fa-inna فَإِنَّ then indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ghaniyyun غَنِيٌّ (is) free from need ʿankum عَنْكُمْ of you walā وَلَا And not yarḍā يَرْضَى He likes liʿibādihi لِعِبَادِهِ in His slaves l-kuf'ra الْكُفْرَ ungratefulness wa-in وَإِنْ And if tashkurū تَشْكُرُوا you are grateful yarḍahu يَرْضَهُ He likes it lakum لَكُمْ in you walā وَلَا And not taziru تَزِرُ will bear wāziratun وَازِرَةٌ bearer of burdens wiz'ra وِزْرَ (the) burden ukh'rā أُخْرَى (of) another thumma ثُمَّ Then ilā إِلَى to rabbikum رَبِّكُمْ your Lord marjiʿukum مَرْجِعُكُمْ (is) your return fayunabbi-ukum فَيُنَبِّئُكُمْ then He will inform you bimā بِمَا about what kuntum كُنْتُمْ you used (to) taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ do innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed, He ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) the All-Knower bidhāti بِذَاتِ of what (is) in the breasts l-ṣudūri الصُّدُورِ of what (is) in the breasts

7

7.

If you disbelieve - indeed, Allah is Free from need of you. And He does not approve for His servants disbelief. And if you are grateful, He approves it for you; and no bearer of burdens will bear the burden of another. Then to your Lord is your return, and He will inform you about what you used to do. Indeed, He is Knowing of that within the breasts.

7

wa-idhā وَإِذَا And when massa مَسَّ touches l-insāna الْإِنْسَانَ [the] man ḍurrun ضُرٌّ adversity daʿā دَعَا he calls rabbahu رَبَّهُ (to) his Lord munīban مُنِيبًا turning ilayhi إِلَيْهِ to Him thumma ثُمَّ then idhā إِذَا when khawwalahu خَوَّلَهُ He bestows on him niʿ'matan نِعْمَةً a favor min'hu مِنْهُ from Himself nasiya نَسِيَ he forgets mā مَا (for) what kāna كَانَ he used to call yadʿū يَدْعُوا he used to call ilayhi إِلَيْهِ [to] Him min مِنْ before qablu قَبْلُ before wajaʿala وَجَعَلَ and he sets up lillahi لِلَّهِ to Allah andādan أَنْدَادًا rivals liyuḍilla لِيُضِلَّ to mislead ʿan عَنْ from sabīlihi سَبِيلِهِ His Path qul قُلْ Say tamattaʿ تَمَتَّعْ Enjoy bikuf'rika بِكُفْرِكَ in your disbelief qalīlan قَلِيلًا (for) a little innaka إِنَّكَ Indeed, you min مِنْ (are) of aṣḥābi أَصْحَابِ (the) companions l-nāri النَّارِ (of) the Fire

8

8.

And when adversity touches man, he calls upon his Lord, turning to Him [alone]; then when He bestows on him a favor from Himself, he forgets Him whom he called upon before, and he attributes to Allah equals to mislead [people] from His way. Say, "Enjoy your disbelief for a little; indeed, you are of the companions of the Fire."

8

amman أَمَّنْ Is (one) who huwa هُوَ [he] qānitun قَانِتٌ (is) devoutly obedient ānāa ءَانَاءَ (during) hours al-layli اللَّيْلِ (of) the night sājidan سَاجِدًا prostrating waqāiman وَقَائِمًا and standing yaḥdharu يَحْذَرُ fearing l-ākhirata الْءَاخِرَةَ the Hereafter wayarjū وَيَرْجُوا and hoping raḥmata رَحْمَةَ (for the) Mercy rabbihi رَبِّهِ (of) his Lord qul قُلْ Say hal هَلْ Are yastawī يَسْتَوِي equal alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who yaʿlamūna يَعْلَمُونَ know wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ and those who lā لَا (do) not yaʿlamūna يَعْلَمُونَ know innamā إِنَّمَا Only yatadhakkaru يَتَذَكَّرُ will take heed ulū أُولُوا those of understanding l-albābi الْأَلْبَابِ those of understanding

9

9.

Is one who is devoutly obedient during periods of the night, prostrating and standing [in prayer], fearing the Hereafter and hoping for the mercy of his Lord, [like one who does not]? Say, "Are those who know equal to those who do not know?" Only they will remember [who are] people of understanding.

9

qul قُلْ Say yāʿibādi يَاعِبَادِ O My slaves alladhīna الَّذِينَ [those] who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe! ittaqū اتَّقُوا Fear rabbakum رَبَّكُمْ your Lord lilladhīna لِلَّذِينَ For those who aḥsanū أَحْسَنُوا do good fī فِي in hādhihi هَذِهِ this l-dun'yā الدُّنْيَا world ḥasanatun حَسَنَةٌ (is) good wa-arḍu وَأَرْضُ and the earth l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah wāsiʿatun وَاسِعَةٌ (is) spacious innamā إِنَّمَا Only yuwaffā يُوَفَّى will be paid back in full l-ṣābirūna الصَّابِرُونَ the patient ajrahum أَجْرَهُمْ their reward bighayri بِغَيْرِ without ḥisābin حِسَابٍ account

10

10.

Say, "O My servants who have believed, fear your Lord. For those who do good in this world is good, and the earth of Allah is spacious. Indeed, the patient will be given their reward without account."

10

qul قُلْ Say innī إِنِّي Indeed I umir'tu أُمِرْتُ [I] am commanded an أَنْ that aʿbuda أَعْبُدَ I worship l-laha اللَّهَ Allah mukh'liṣan مُخْلِصًا (being) sincere lahu لَهُ to Him l-dīna الدِّينَ (in) the religion

11

11.

Say, [O Muhammad], "Indeed, I have been commanded to worship Allah, [being] sincere to Him in religion.

11

wa-umir'tu وَأُمِرْتُ And I am commanded li-an لِأَنْ that akūna أَكُونَ I be awwala أَوَّلَ (the) first l-mus'limīna الْمُسْلِمِينَ (of) those who submit

12

12.

And I have been commanded to be the first [among you] of the Muslims."

12

qul قُلْ Say innī إِنِّي Indeed I akhāfu أَخَافُ [I] fear in إِنْ if ʿaṣaytu عَصَيْتُ I disobey rabbī رَبِّي my Lord ʿadhāba عَذَابَ (the) punishment yawmin يَوْمٍ (of) a Day ʿaẓīmin عَظِيمٍ great

13

13.

Say, "Indeed I fear, if I should disobey my Lord, the punishment of a tremendous Day."

13

14

14.

Say, "Allah [alone] do I worship, sincere to Him in my religion,

14

15

15.

So worship what you will besides Him." Say, "Indeed, the losers are the ones who will lose themselves and their families on the Day of Resurrection. Unquestionably, that is the manifest loss."

15

lahum لَهُمْ For them min مِنْ from fawqihim فَوْقِهِمْ above them ẓulalun ظُلَلٌ coverings mina مِنَ of l-nāri النَّارِ the Fire wamin وَمِنْ and from taḥtihim تَحْتِهِمْ below them ẓulalun ظُلَلٌ coverings dhālika ذَلِكَ (With) that yukhawwifu يُخَوِّفُ threatens l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah bihi بِهِ [with it] ʿibādahu عِبَادَهُ His slaves yāʿibādi يَاعِبَادِ O My slaves! fa-ittaqūni فَاتَّقُونِ So fear Me

16

16.

They will have canopies of fire above them and below them, canopies. By that Allah threatens His servants. O My servants, then fear Me.

16

wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those who ij'tanabū اجْتَنَبُوا avoid l-ṭāghūta الطَّاغُوتَ the false gods an أَنْ lest yaʿbudūhā يَعْبُدُوهَا they worship them wa-anābū وَأَنَابُوا and turn ilā إِلَى to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah lahumu لَهُمُ for them l-bush'rā الْبُشْرَى (is) the glad tiding fabashir فَبَشِّرْ So give glad tidings ʿibādi عِبَادِ (to) My slaves

17

17.

But those who have avoided Taghut, lest they worship it, and turned back to Allah - for them are good tidings. So give good tidings to My servants

17

18

18.

Who listen to speech and follow the best of it. Those are the ones Allah has guided, and those are people of understanding.

18

afaman أَفَمَنْ Then is (one) who ḥaqqa حَقَّ became due ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ on him kalimatu كَلِمَةُ the word l-ʿadhābi الْعَذَابِ (of) the punishment afa-anta أَفَأَنْتَ Then can you tunqidhu تُنْقِذُ save man مَنْ (one) who fī فِي (is) in l-nāri النَّارِ the Fire

19

19.
