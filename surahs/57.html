sabbaḥa سَبَّحَ Glorifies lillahi لِلَّهِ [to] Allah mā مَا whatever fī فِي (is) in l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth wahuwa وَهُوَ and He l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ (is) the All-Mighty l-ḥakīmu الْحَكِيمُ the All-Wise

1

1.

Whatever is in the heavens and earth exalts Allah, and He is the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

1

lahu لَهُ For Him mul'ku مُلْكُ (is the) dominion l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth yuḥ'yī يُحْيِي He gives life wayumītu وَيُمِيتُ and causes death wahuwa وَهُوَ and He ʿalā عَلَى (is) over kulli كُلِّ all shayin شَيْءٍ things qadīrun قَدِيرٌ All-Powerful

2

2.

His is the dominion of the heavens and earth. He gives life and causes death, and He is over all things competent.

2

huwa هُوَ He l-awalu الْأَوَّلُ (is) the First wal-ākhiru وَالْءَاخِرُ and the Last wal-ẓāhiru وَالظَّاهِرُ and the Apparent wal-bāṭinu وَالْبَاطِنُ and the Unapparent wahuwa وَهُوَ and He bikulli بِكُلِّ (is) of every shayin شَيْءٍ thing ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ All-Knower

3

3.

He is the First and the Last, the Ascendant and the Intimate, and He is, of all things, Knowing.

3

huwa هُوَ He alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who khalaqa خَلَقَ created l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ the heavens wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth fī فِي in sittati سِتَّةِ six ayyāmin أَيَّامٍ periods thumma ثُمَّ then is'tawā اسْتَوَى He rose ʿalā عَلَى over l-ʿarshi الْعَرْشِ the Throne yaʿlamu يَعْلَمُ He knows mā مَا what yaliju يَلِجُ penetrates fī فِي in(to) l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth wamā وَمَا and what yakhruju يَخْرُجُ comes forth min'hā مِنْهَا from it wamā وَمَا and what yanzilu يَنْزِلُ descends mina مِنَ from l-samāi السَّمَاءِ the heaven wamā وَمَا and what yaʿruju يَعْرُجُ ascends fīhā فِيهَا therein wahuwa وَهُوَ and He maʿakum مَعَكُمْ (is) with you ayna أَيْنَ wherever mā مَا wherever kuntum كُنْتُمْ you are wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do baṣīrun بَصِيرٌ (is) All-seer

4

4.

It is He who created the heavens and earth in six days and then established Himself above the Throne. He knows what penetrates into the earth and what emerges from it and what descends from the heaven and what ascends therein; and He is with you wherever you are. And Allah, of what you do, is Seeing.

4

lahu لَهُ For Him mul'ku مُلْكُ (is the) dominion l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth wa-ilā وَإِلَى and to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah tur'jaʿu تُرْجَعُ will be returned l-umūru الْأُمُورُ the matters

5

5.

His is the dominion of the heavens and earth. And to Allah are returned [all] matters.

5

yūliju يُولِجُ He merges al-layla اللَّيْلَ the night fī فِي into l-nahāri النَّهَارِ the day wayūliju وَيُولِجُ and He merges l-nahāra النَّهَارَ the day fī فِي into al-layli اللَّيْلِ the night wahuwa وَهُوَ and He ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knower bidhāti بِذَاتِ of what is in the breasts l-ṣudūri الصُّدُورِ of what is in the breasts

6

6.

He causes the night to enter the day and causes the day to enter the night, and he is Knowing of that within the breasts.

6

āminū ءَامِنُوا Believe bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah warasūlihi وَرَسُولِهِ and His Messenger wa-anfiqū وَأَنْفِقُوا and spend mimmā مِمَّا of what jaʿalakum جَعَلَكُمْ He has made you mus'takhlafīna مُسْتَخْلَفِينَ trustees fīhi فِيهِ therein fa-alladhīna فَالَّذِينَ And those āmanū ءَامَنُوا who believe minkum مِنْكُمْ among you wa-anfaqū وَأَنْفَقُوا and spend lahum لَهُمْ for them ajrun أَجْرٌ (is) a reward kabīrun كَبِيرٌ great

7

7.

Believe in Allah and His Messenger and spend out of that in which He has made you successors. For those who have believed among you and spent, there will be a great reward.

7

wamā وَمَا And what lakum لَكُمْ (is) for you lā لَا (that) not tu'minūna تُؤْمِنُونَ you believe bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ in Allah wal-rasūlu وَالرَّسُولُ while the Messenger yadʿūkum يَدْعُوكُمْ calls you litu'minū لِتُؤْمِنُوا that you believe birabbikum بِرَبِّكُمْ in your Lord waqad وَقَدْ and indeed akhadha أَخَذَ He has taken mīthāqakum مِيثَاقَكُمْ your covenant in إِنْ if kuntum كُنْتُمْ you are mu'minīna مُؤْمِنِينَ believers

8

8.

And why do you not believe in Allah while the Messenger invites you to believe in your Lord and He has taken your covenant, if you should [truly] be believers?

8

huwa هُوَ He alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who yunazzilu يُنَزِّلُ sends down ʿalā عَلَى upon ʿabdihi عَبْدِهِ His slave āyātin ءَايَاتٍ Verses bayyinātin بَيِّنَاتٍ clear liyukh'rijakum لِيُخْرِجَكُمْ that He may bring you out mina مِنَ from l-ẓulumāti الظُّلُمَاتِ the darkness[es] ilā إِلَى into l-nūri النُّورِ the light wa-inna وَإِنَّ And indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah bikum بِكُمْ to you laraūfun لَرَءُوفٌ (is the) Most Kind raḥīmun رَحِيمٌ (the) Most Merciful

9

9.

It is He who sends down upon His Servant [Muhammad] verses of clear evidence that He may bring you out from darknesses into the light. And indeed, Allah is to you Kind and Merciful.

9

wamā وَمَا And what lakum لَكُمْ (is) for you allā أَلَّا that not tunfiqū تُنْفِقُوا you spend fī فِي in sabīli سَبِيلِ (the) way l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah walillahi وَلِلَّهِ while for Allah mīrāthu مِيرَاثُ (is the) heritage l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth lā لَا Not yastawī يَسْتَوِي are equal minkum مِنْكُمْ among you man مَنْ (those) who anfaqa أَنْفَقَ spent min مِنْ before qabli قَبْلِ before l-fatḥi الْفَتْحِ the victory waqātala وَقَاتَلَ and fought ulāika أُولَئِكَ Those aʿẓamu أَعْظَمُ (are) greater darajatan دَرَجَةً (in) degree mina مِنَ than alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who anfaqū أَنْفَقُوا spent min مِنْ afterwards baʿdu بَعْدُ afterwards waqātalū وَقَاتَلُوا and fought wakullan وَكُلًّا But to all waʿada وَعَدَ Allah has promised l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah has promised l-ḥus'nā الْحُسْنَى the best wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah bimā بِمَا of what taʿmalūna تَعْمَلُونَ you do khabīrun خَبِيرٌ (is) All-Aware

10

10.

And why do you not spend in the cause of Allah while to Allah belongs the heritage of the heavens and the earth? Not equal among you are those who spent before the conquest [of Makkah] and fought [and those who did so after it]. Those are greater in degree than they who spent afterwards and fought. But to all Allah has promised the best [reward]. And Allah, with what you do, is Acquainted.

10

man مَنْ Who (is) dhā ذَا the one who alladhī الَّذِي the one who yuq'riḍu يُقْرِضُ will loan l-laha اللَّهَ (to) Allah qarḍan قَرْضًا a loan ḥasanan حَسَنًا goodly fayuḍāʿifahu فَيُضَاعِفَهُ so He will multiply it lahu لَهُ for him walahu وَلَهُ and for him ajrun أَجْرٌ (is) a reward karīmun كَرِيمٌ noble

11

11.

Who is it that would loan Allah a goodly loan so He will multiply it for him and he will have a noble reward?

11

yawma يَوْمَ (On the) Day tarā تَرَى you will see l-mu'minīna الْمُؤْمِنِينَ the believing men wal-mu'mināti وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ and the believing women yasʿā يَسْعَى running nūruhum نُورُهُمْ their light bayna بَيْنَ before them aydīhim أَيْدِيهِمْ before them wabi-aymānihim وَبِأَيْمَانِهِمْ and on their right bush'rākumu بُشْرَاكُمُ Glad tidings for you l-yawma الْيَوْمَ this Day jannātun جَنَّاتٌ gardens tajrī تَجْرِي flowing min مِنْ from taḥtihā تَحْتِهَا underneath it l-anhāru الْأَنْهَارُ the rivers khālidīna خَالِدِينَ abiding forever fīhā فِيهَا therein dhālika ذَلِكَ That huwa هُوَ [it] (is) l-fawzu الْفَوْزُ the success l-ʿaẓīmu الْعَظِيمُ the great

12

12.

On the Day you see the believing men and believing women, their light proceeding before them and on their right, [it will be said], "Your good tidings today are [of] gardens beneath which rivers flow, wherein you will abide eternally." That is what is the great attainment.

12

yawma يَوْمَ (On the) Day yaqūlu يَقُولُ will say l-munāfiqūna الْمُنَافِقُونَ the hypocrite men wal-munāfiqātu وَالْمُنَافِقَاتُ and the hypocrite women lilladhīna لِلَّذِينَ to those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believed unẓurūnā انْظُرُونَا Wait for us naqtabis نَقْتَبِسْ we may acquire min مِنْ of nūrikum نُورِكُمْ your light qīla قِيلَ It will be said ir'jiʿū ارْجِعُوا Go back warāakum وَرَاءَكُمْ behind you fal-tamisū فَالْتَمِسُوا and seek nūran نُورًا light faḍuriba فَضُرِبَ Then will be put up baynahum بَيْنَهُمْ between them bisūrin بِسُورٍ a wall lahu لَهُ for it bābun بَابٌ a gate bāṭinuhu بَاطِنُهُ its interior fīhi فِيهِ in it l-raḥmatu الرَّحْمَةُ (is) mercy waẓāhiruhu وَظَاهِرُهُ but its exterior min مِنْ facing towards [it] qibalihi قِبَلِهِ facing towards [it] l-ʿadhābu الْعَذَابُ the punishment

13

13.

On the [same] Day the hypocrite men and hypocrite women will say to those who believed, "Wait for us that we may acquire some of your light." It will be said, "Go back behind you and seek light." And a wall will be placed between them with a door, its interior containing mercy, but on the outside of it is torment.

13

yunādūnahum يُنَادُونَهُمْ They will call them alam أَلَمْ Were not nakun نَكُنْ we maʿakum مَعَكُمْ with you qālū قَالُوا They will say balā بَلَى Yes walākinnakum وَلَكِنَّكُمْ but you fatantum فَتَنْتُمْ led to temptation anfusakum أَنْفُسَكُمْ yourselves watarabbaṣtum وَتَرَبَّصْتُمْ and you awaited wa-ir'tabtum وَارْتَبْتُمْ and you doubted wagharratkumu وَغَرَّتْكُمُ and deceived you l-amāniyu الْأَمَانِيُّ the wishful thinking ḥattā حَتَّى until jāa جَاءَ came amru أَمْرُ (the) Command l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah wagharrakum وَغَرَّكُمْ And deceived you bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ about Allah l-gharūru الْغَرُورُ the deceiver

14

14.

The hypocrites will call to the believers, "Were we not with you?" They will say, "Yes, but you afflicted yourselves and awaited [misfortune for us] and doubted, and wishful thinking deluded you until there came the command of Allah. And the Deceiver deceived you concerning Allah.

14

fal-yawma فَالْيَوْمَ So today lā لَا not yu'khadhu يُؤْخَذُ will be accepted minkum مِنْكُمْ from you fid'yatun فِدْيَةٌ any ransom walā وَلَا and not mina مِنَ from alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieved mawākumu مَأْوَاكُمُ Your abode l-nāru النَّارُ (is) the Fire hiya هِيَ it (is) mawlākum مَوْلَاكُمْ your protector wabi'sa وَبِئْسَ and wretched is l-maṣīru الْمَصِيرُ the destination

15

15.

So today no ransom will be taken from you or from those who disbelieved. Your refuge is the Fire. It is most worthy of you, and wretched is the destination.

15

alam أَلَمْ Has not yani يَأْنِ come (the) time lilladhīna لِلَّذِينَ for those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believed an أَنْ that takhshaʿa تَخْشَعَ become humble qulūbuhum قُلُوبُهُمْ their hearts lidhik'ri لِذِكْرِ at (the) remembrance (of) Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ at (the) remembrance (of) Allah wamā وَمَا and what nazala نَزَلَ has come down mina مِنَ of l-ḥaqi الْحَقِّ the truth walā وَلَا And not yakūnū يَكُونُوا they become ka-alladhīna كَالَّذِينَ like those who ūtū أُوتُوا were given l-kitāba الْكِتَابَ the Book min مِنْ before qablu قَبْلُ before faṭāla فَطَالَ (and) was prolonged ʿalayhimu عَلَيْهِمُ for them l-amadu الْأَمَدُ the term faqasat فَقَسَتْ so hardened qulūbuhum قُلُوبُهُمْ their hearts wakathīrun وَكَثِيرٌ and many min'hum مِنْهُمْ of them fāsiqūna فَاسِقُونَ (are) defiantly disobedient

16

16.

Has the time not come for those who have believed that their hearts should become humbly submissive at the remembrance of Allah and what has come down of the truth? And let them not be like those who were given the Scripture before, and a long period passed over them, so their hearts hardened; and many of them are defiantly disobedient.

16

iʿ'lamū اعْلَمُوا Know anna أَنَّ that l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yuḥ'yī يُحْيِي gives life l-arḍa الْأَرْضَ (to) the earth baʿda بَعْدَ after mawtihā مَوْتِهَا its death qad قَدْ Indeed bayyannā بَيَّنَّا We have made clear lakumu لَكُمُ to you l-āyāti الْءَايَاتِ the Signs laʿallakum لَعَلَّكُمْ so that you may taʿqilūna تَعْقِلُونَ understand

17

17.

Know that Allah gives life to the earth after its lifelessness. We have made clear to you the signs; perhaps you will understand.

17

inna إِنَّ Indeed l-muṣadiqīna الْمُصَّدِّقِينَ the men who give charity wal-muṣadiqāti وَالْمُصَّدِّقَاتِ and the women who give charity wa-aqraḍū وَأَقْرَضُوا and who lend l-laha اللَّهَ (to) Allah qarḍan قَرْضًا a loan ḥasanan حَسَنًا goodly yuḍāʿafu يُضَاعَفُ it will be multiplied lahum لَهُمْ for them walahum وَلَهُمْ and for them ajrun أَجْرٌ (is) a reward karīmun كَرِيمٌ noble

18

18.

Indeed, the men who practice charity and the women who practice charity and [they who] have loaned Allah a goodly loan - it will be multiplied for them, and they will have a noble reward.

18

19.

20.

Know that the life of this world is but amusement and diversion and adornment and boasting to one another and competition in increase of wealth and children - like the example of a rain whose [resulting] plant growth pleases the tillers; then it dries and you see it turned yellow; then it becomes [scattered] debris. And in the Hereafter is severe punishment and forgiveness from Allah and approval. And what is the worldly life except the enjoyment of delusion.
