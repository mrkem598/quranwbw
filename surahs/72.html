qul قُلْ Say ūḥiya أُوحِيَ It has been revealed ilayya إِلَيَّ to me annahu أَنَّهُ that is'tamaʿa اسْتَمَعَ listened nafarun نَفَرٌ a group mina مِنَ of l-jini الْجِنِّ the jinn faqālū فَقَالُوا and they said innā إِنَّا Indeed we samiʿ'nā سَمِعْنَا heard qur'ānan قُرْءَانًا a Quran ʿajaban عَجَبًا amazing

1

1.

Say, [O Muhammad], "It has been revealed to me that a group of the jinn listened and said, 'Indeed, we have heard an amazing Qur'an.

1

yahdī يَهْدِي It guides ilā إِلَى to l-rush'di الرُّشْدِ the right way faāmannā فَءَامَنَّا so we believe bihi بِهِ in it walan وَلَنْ and never nush'rika نُشْرِكَ we will associate birabbinā بِرَبِّنَا with our Lord aḥadan أَحَدًا anyone

2

2.

It guides to the right course, and we have believed in it. And we will never associate with our Lord anyone.

2

wa-annahu وَأَنَّهُ And that He taʿālā تَعَالَى Exalted is jaddu جَدُّ (the) Majesty rabbinā رَبِّنَا (of) our Lord mā مَا not ittakhadha اتَّخَذَ He has taken ṣāḥibatan صَاحِبَةً a wife walā وَلَا and not waladan وَلَدًا a son

3

3.

And [it teaches] that exalted is the nobleness of our Lord; He has not taken a wife or a son

3

wa-annahu وَأَنَّهُ And that he kāna كَانَ used yaqūlu يَقُولُ (to) speak safīhunā سَفِيهُنَا the foolish among us ʿalā عَلَى against l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah shaṭaṭan شَطَطًا an excessive transgression

4

4.

And that our foolish one has been saying about Allah an excessive transgression.

4

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we ẓanannā ظَنَنَّا thought an أَنْ that lan لَنْ never taqūla تَقُولَ will say l-insu الْإِنْسُ the men wal-jinu وَالْجِنُّ and the jinn ʿalā عَلَى against l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah kadhiban كَذِبًا any lie

5

5.

And we had thought that mankind and the jinn would never speak about Allah a lie.

5

wa-annahu وَأَنَّهُ And that kāna كَانَ (there) were rijālun رِجَالٌ men mina مِنَ among l-insi الْإِنْسِ mankind yaʿūdhūna يَعُوذُونَ who sought refuge birijālin بِرِجَالٍ in (the) men mina مِنَ from l-jini الْجِنِّ the jinn fazādūhum فَزَادُوهُمْ so they increased them rahaqan رَهَقًا (in) burden

6

6.

And there were men from mankind who sought refuge in men from the jinn, so they [only] increased them in burden.

6

wa-annahum وَأَنَّهُمْ And that they ẓannū ظَنُّوا thought kamā كَمَا as ẓanantum ظَنَنْتُمْ you thought an أَنْ that lan لَنْ never yabʿatha يَبْعَثَ will raise l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah aḥadan أَحَدًا anyone

7

7.

And they had thought, as you thought, that Allah would never send anyone [as a messenger].

7

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we lamasnā لَمَسْنَا sought to touch l-samāa السَّمَاءَ the heaven fawajadnāhā فَوَجَدْنَاهَا but we found it muli-at مُلِئَتْ filled (with) ḥarasan حَرَسًا guards shadīdan شَدِيدًا severe washuhuban وَشُهُبًا and flaming fires

8

8.

And we have sought [to reach] the heaven but found it filled with powerful guards and burning flames.

8

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we kunnā كُنَّا used (to) naqʿudu نَقْعُدُ sit min'hā مِنْهَا there in maqāʿida مَقَاعِدَ positions lilssamʿi لِلسَّمْعِ for hearing faman فَمَنْ but (he) who yastamiʿi يَسْتَمِعِ listens l-āna الْءَانَ now yajid يَجِدْ will find lahu لَهُ for him shihāban شِهَابًا a flaming fire raṣadan رَصَدًا waiting

9

9.

And we used to sit therein in positions for hearing, but whoever listens now will find a burning flame lying in wait for him.

9

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we lā لَا not nadrī نَدْرِي we know asharrun أَشَرٌّ whether evil urīda أُرِيدَ is intended biman بِمَنْ for (those) who fī فِي (are) in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth am أَمْ or arāda أَرَادَ intends bihim بِهِمْ for them rabbuhum رَبُّهُمْ their Lord rashadan رَشَدًا a right path

10

10.

And we do not know [therefore] whether evil is intended for those on earth or whether their Lord intends for them a right course.

10

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that minnā مِنَّا among us l-ṣāliḥūna الصَّالِحُونَ (are) the righteous waminnā وَمِنَّا and among us dūna دُونَ (are) other than dhālika ذَلِكَ that kunnā كُنَّا We ṭarāiqa طَرَائِقَ (are on) ways qidadan قِدَدًا different

11

11.

And among us are the righteous, and among us are [others] not so; we were [of] divided ways.

11

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we ẓanannā ظَنَنَّا [we] have become certain an أَنْ that lan لَنْ never nuʿ'jiza نُعجِزَ we will cause failure l-laha اللَّهَ (to) Allah fī فِي in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth walan وَلَنْ and never nuʿ'jizahu نُعْجِزَهُ we can escape Him haraban هَرَبًا (by) flight

12

12.

And we have become certain that we will never cause failure to Allah upon earth, nor can we escape Him by flight.

12

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that lammā لَمَّا when samiʿ'nā سَمِعْنَا we heard l-hudā الْهُدَى the Guidance āmannā ءَامَنَّا we believed bihi بِهِ in it faman فَمَنْ And whoever yu'min يُؤْمِنْ believes birabbihi بِرَبِّهِ in his Lord falā فَلَا then not yakhāfu يَخَافُ he will fear bakhsan بَخْسًا any loss walā وَلَا and not rahaqan رَهَقًا any burden

13

13.

And when we heard the guidance, we believed in it. And whoever believes in his Lord will not fear deprivation or burden.

13

wa-annā وَأَنَّا And that we minnā مِنَّا among us l-mus'limūna الْمُسْلِمُونَ (are) Muslims waminnā وَمِنَّا and among us l-qāsiṭūna الْقَاسِطُونَ (are) unjust faman فَمَنْ And whoever aslama أَسْلَمَ submits fa-ulāika فَأُولَئِكَ then those taḥarraw تَحَرَّوْا have sought rashadan رَشَدًا (the) right path

14

14.

And among us are Muslims [in submission to Allah], and among us are the unjust. And whoever has become Muslim - those have sought out the right course.

14

wa-ammā وَأَمَّا And as for l-qāsiṭūna الْقَاسِطُونَ the unjust fakānū فَكَانُوا they will be lijahannama لِجَهَنَّمَ for Hell ḥaṭaban حَطَبًا firewood

15

15.

But as for the unjust, they will be, for Hell, firewood.'

15

wa-allawi وَأَلَّوِ And if is'taqāmū اسْتَقَامُوا they had remained ʿalā عَلَى on l-ṭarīqati الطَّرِيقَةِ the Way la-asqaynāhum لَأَسْقَيْنَاهُمْ surely We (would) have given them to drink māan مَاءً water ghadaqan غَدَقًا (in) abundance

16

16.

And [Allah revealed] that if they had remained straight on the way, We would have given them abundant provision

16

linaftinahum لِنَفْتِنَهُمْ That We might test them fīhi فِيهِ therein waman وَمَنْ And whoever yuʿ'riḍ يُعْرِضْ turns away ʿan عَنْ from dhik'ri ذِكْرِ the Remembrance rabbihi رَبِّهِ (of) his Lord yasluk'hu يَسْلُكْهُ He will make him enter ʿadhāban عَذَابًا a punishment ṣaʿadan صَعَدًا severe

17

17.

So We might test them therein. And whoever turns away from the remembrance of his Lord He will put into arduous punishment.

17

wa-anna وَأَنَّ And that l-masājida الْمَسَاجِدَ the masajid lillahi لِلَّهِ (are) for Allah falā فَلَا so (do) not tadʿū تَدْعُوا call maʿa مَعَ with l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah aḥadan أَحَدًا anyone

18

18.

And [He revealed] that the masjids are for Allah, so do not invoke with Allah anyone.

18

wa-annahu وَأَنَّهُ And that lammā لَمَّا when qāma قَامَ stood up ʿabdu عَبْدُ (the) slave l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah yadʿūhu يَدْعُوهُ calling (upon) Him kādū كَادُوا they almost yakūnūna يَكُونُونَ became ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ around him libadan لِبَدًا a compacted mass

19

19.

And that when the Servant of Allah stood up supplicating Him, they almost became about him a compacted mass."

19

qul قُلْ Say innamā إِنَّمَا Only adʿū أَدْعُوا I call upon rabbī رَبِّي my Lord walā وَلَا and not ush'riku أُشْرِكُ I associate bihi بِهِ with Him aḥadan أَحَدًا anyone

20

20.

Say, [O Muhammad], "I only invoke my Lord and do not associate with Him anyone."

20

qul قُلْ Say innī إِنِّي Indeed I lā لَا (do) not amliku أَمْلِكُ possess lakum لَكُمْ for you ḍarran ضَرًّا any harm walā وَلَا and not rashadan رَشَدًا right path

21

21.

Say, "Indeed, I do not possess for you [the power of] harm or right direction."

21

qul قُلْ Say innī إِنِّي Indeed I lan لَنْ never yujīranī يُجِيرَنِي can protect me mina مِنَ from l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah aḥadun أَحَدٌ anyone walan وَلَنْ and never ajida أَجِدَ can I find min مِنْ from dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him mul'taḥadan مُلْتَحَدًا any refuge

22

22.

Say, "Indeed, there will never protect me from Allah anyone [if I should disobey], nor will I find in other than Him a refuge.

22

illā إِلَّا But balāghan بَلَاغًا (the) notification mina مِنَ from l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah warisālātihi وَرِسَالَاتِهِ and His Messages waman وَمَنْ And whoever yaʿṣi يَعْصِ disobeys l-laha اللَّهَ Allah warasūlahu وَرَسُولَهُ and His Messenger fa-inna فَإِنَّ then indeed lahu لَهُ for him nāra نَارَ (is the) Fire jahannama جَهَنَّمَ (of) Hell khālidīna خَالِدِينَ (they will) abide fīhā فِيهَا therein abadan أَبَدًا forever

23

23.

But [I have for you] only notification from Allah, and His messages." And whoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger - then indeed, for him is the fire of Hell; they will abide therein forever.

23

ḥattā حَتَّى Until idhā إِذَا when ra-aw رَأَوْا they see mā مَا what yūʿadūna يُوعَدُونَ they are promised fasayaʿlamūna فَسَيَعْلَمُونَ then they will know man مَنْ who aḍʿafu أَضْعَفُ (is) weaker nāṣiran نَاصِرًا (in) helpers wa-aqallu وَأَقَلُّ and fewer ʿadadan عَدَدًا (in) number

24

24.

[The disbelievers continue] until, when they see that which they are promised, then they will know who is weaker in helpers and less in number.

24

qul قُلْ Say in إِنْ Not adrī أَدْرِي I know aqarībun أَقَرِيبٌ whether is near mā مَا what tūʿadūna تُوعَدُونَ you are promised am أَمْ or (whether) yajʿalu يَجْعَلُ will appoint lahu لَهُ for it rabbī رَبِّي my Lord amadan أَمَدًا a (distant) term

25

25.

Say, "I do not know if what you are promised is near or if my Lord will grant for it a [long] period."

25

ʿālimu عَالِمُ (The) All-Knower l-ghaybi الْغَيْبِ (of) the unseen falā فَلَا so not yuẓ'hiru يُظْهِرُ He reveals ʿalā عَلَى from ghaybihi غَيْبِهِ His unseen aḥadan أَحَدًا (to) anyone

26

26.

[He is] Knower of the unseen, and He does not disclose His [knowledge of the] unseen to anyone

26

illā إِلَّا Except mani مَنِ whom ir'taḍā ارْتَضَى He has approved min مِنْ of rasūlin رَسُولٍ a Messenger fa-innahu فَإِنَّهُ and indeed, He yasluku يَسْلُكُ makes to march min مِنْ from bayni بَيْنِ before yadayhi يَدَيْهِ him wamin وَمِنْ and from khalfihi خَلْفِهِ behind him raṣadan رَصَدًا a guard

27

27.

Except whom He has approved of messengers, and indeed, He sends before each messenger and behind him observers

27

liyaʿlama لِيَعْلَمَ That He may make evident an أَنْ that qad قَدْ indeed ablaghū أَبْلَغُوا they have conveyed risālāti رِسَالَاتِ (the) Messages rabbihim رَبِّهِمْ (of) their Lord; wa-aḥāṭa وَأَحَاطَ and He has encompassed bimā بِمَا what ladayhim لَدَيْهِمْ (is) with them wa-aḥṣā وَأَحْصَى and He takes account kulla كُلَّ (of) all shayin شَيْءٍ things ʿadadan عَدَدًا (in) number

28

28.

That he may know that they have conveyed the messages of their Lord; and He has encompassed whatever is with them and has enumerated all things in number.
