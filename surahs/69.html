aL-ḥāqatu الْحَاقَّةُ The Inevitable Reality!

1

1.

The Inevitable Reality -

1

mā مَا What l-ḥāqatu الْحَاقَّةُ (is) the Inevitable Reality

2

2.

What is the Inevitable Reality?

2

wamā وَمَا And what adrāka أَدْرَاكَ will make you know mā مَا what l-ḥāqatu الْحَاقَّةُ (is) the Inevitable Reality

3

3.

And what can make you know what is the Inevitable Reality?

3

kadhabat كَذَّبَتْ Denied thamūdu ثَمُودُ Thamud waʿādun وَعَادٌ and Aad bil-qāriʿati بِالْقَارِعَةِ the Striking Calamity

4

4.

Thamud and 'Aad denied the Striking Calamity.

4

fa-ammā فَأَمَّا So as for thamūdu ثَمُودُ Thamud fa-uh'likū فَأُهْلِكُوا they were destroyed bil-ṭāghiyati بِالطَّاغِيَةِ by the overpowering (blast)

5

5.

So as for Thamud, they were destroyed by the overpowering [blast].

5

wa-ammā وَأَمَّا And as for ʿādun عَادٌ Aad fa-uh'likū فَأُهْلِكُوا they were destroyed birīḥin بِرِيحٍ by a wind ṣarṣarin صَرْصَرٍ screaming ʿātiyatin عَاتِيَةٍ violent

6

6.

And as for 'Aad, they were destroyed by a screaming, violent wind

6

sakharahā سَخَّرَهَا Which He imposed ʿalayhim عَلَيْهِمْ upon them sabʿa سَبْعَ (for) seven layālin لَيَالٍ nights wathamāniyata وَثَمَانِيَةَ and eight ayyāmin أَيَّامٍ days ḥusūman حُسُومًا (in) succession fatarā فَتَرَى so you would see l-qawma الْقَوْمَ the people fīhā فِيهَا therein ṣarʿā صَرْعَى fallen ka-annahum كَأَنَّهُمْ as if they were aʿjāzu أَعْجَازُ trunks nakhlin نَخْلٍ (of) date-palms khāwiyatin خَاوِيَةٍ hollow

7

7.

Which Allah imposed upon them for seven nights and eight days in succession, so you would see the people therein fallen as if they were hollow trunks of palm trees.

7

fahal فَهَلْ Then do tarā تَرَى you see lahum لَهُمْ of them min مِنْ any bāqiyatin بَاقِيَةٍ remains

8

8.

Then do you see of them any remains?

8

wajāa وَجَاءَ And came fir'ʿawnu فِرْعَوْنُ Firaun waman وَمَنْ and (those) qablahu قَبْلَهُ before him wal-mu'tafikātu وَالْمُؤْتَفِكَاتُ and the overturned cities bil-khāṭi-ati بِالْخَاطِئَةِ with sin

9

9.

And there came Pharaoh and those before him and the overturned cities with sin.

9

faʿaṣaw فَعَصَوْا And they disobeyed rasūla رَسُولَ (the) Messenger rabbihim رَبِّهِمْ (of) their Lord fa-akhadhahum فَأَخَذَهُمْ so He seized them akhdhatan أَخْذَةً (with) a seizure rābiyatan رَابِيَةً exceeding

10

10.

And they disobeyed the messenger of their Lord, so He seized them with a seizure exceeding [in severity].

10

innā إِنَّا Indeed We lammā لَمَّا when ṭaghā طَغَى overflowed l-māu الْمَاءُ the water ḥamalnākum حَمَلْنَاكُمْ We carried you fī فِي in l-jāriyati الْجَارِيَةِ the sailing (ship)

11

11.

Indeed, when the water overflowed, We carried your ancestors in the sailing ship

11

linajʿalahā لِنَجْعَلَهَا That We might make it lakum لَكُمْ for you tadhkiratan تَذْكِرَةً a reminder wataʿiyahā وَتَعِيَهَا and would be conscious of it udhunun أُذُنٌ an ear wāʿiyatun وَاعِيَةٌ conscious

12

12.

That We might make it for you a reminder and [that] a conscious ear would be conscious of it.

12

fa-idhā فَإِذَا Then when nufikha نُفِخَ is blown fī فِي in l-ṣūri الصُّورِ the trumpet nafkhatun نَفْخَةٌ a blast wāḥidatun وَاحِدَةٌ single

13

13.

Then when the Horn is blown with one blast

13

waḥumilati وَحُمِلَتِ And are lifted l-arḍu الْأَرْضُ the earth wal-jibālu وَالْجِبَالُ and the mountains fadukkatā فَدُكَّتَا and crushed dakkatan دَكَّةً (with) a crushing wāḥidatan وَاحِدَةً single

14

14.

And the earth and the mountains are lifted and leveled with one blow -

14

fayawma-idhin فَيَوْمَئِذٍ Then (on) that Day waqaʿati وَقَعَتِ will occur l-wāqiʿatu الْوَاقِعَةُ the Occurrence

15

15.

Then on that Day, the Resurrection will occur,

15

wa-inshaqqati وَانْشَقَّتِ And will split l-samāu السَّمَاءُ the heaven fahiya فَهِيَ so it yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ (is on) that Day wāhiyatun وَاهِيَةٌ frail

16

16.

And the heaven will split [open], for that Day it is infirm.

16

wal-malaku وَالْمَلَكُ And the Angels ʿalā عَلَى (will be) on arjāihā أَرْجَائِهَا its edges wayaḥmilu وَيَحْمِلُ and will bear ʿarsha عَرْشَ (the) Throne rabbika رَبِّكَ (of) your Lord fawqahum فَوْقَهُمْ above them yawma-idhin يَوْمَئِذٍ that Day thamāniyatun ثَمَانِيَةٌ eight

17

17.

And the angels are at its edges. And there will bear the Throne of your Lord above them, that Day, eight [of them].

17

18

18.

That Day, you will be exhibited [for judgement]; not hidden among you is anything concealed.

18

19

19.

So as for he who is given his record in his right hand, he will say, "Here, read my record!

19

innī إِنِّي Indeed I ẓanantu ظَنَنْتُ was certain annī أَنِّي that I mulāqin مُلَاقٍ (will) meet ḥisābiyah حِسَابِيَهْ my account

20

20.

Indeed, I was certain that I would be meeting my account."

20

21

21.

So he will be in a pleasant life -

21

22

22.

In an elevated garden,

22

23

23.

Its [fruit] to be picked hanging near.

23

kulū كُلُوا Eat wa-ish'rabū وَاشْرَبُوا and drink hanīan هَنِيئًا (in) satisfaction bimā بِمَا for what aslaftum أَسْلَفْتُمْ you sent before you fī فِي in l-ayāmi الْأَيَّامِ the days l-khāliyati الْخَالِيَةِ past

24

24.

[They will be told], "Eat and drink in satisfaction for what you put forth in the days past."

24

25

25.

But as for he who is given his record in his left hand, he will say, "Oh, I wish I had not been given my record

26

26.

And had not known what is my account.

26

27

27.

I wish my death had been the decisive one.

28

28.

29

29.

Gone from me is my authority."

33.

36

36.

Nor any food except from the discharge of wounds;

None will eat it except the sinners.
