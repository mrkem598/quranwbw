aL-ḥamdu الْحَمْدُ All praises lillahi لِلَّهِ (be) to Allah fāṭiri فَاطِرِ Creator l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth jāʿili جَاعِلِ (Who) makes l-malāikati الْمَلَائِكَةِ the Angels rusulan رُسُلًا messengers ulī أُولِي having wings ajniḥatin أَجْنِحَةٍ having wings mathnā مَثْنَى two wathulātha وَثُلَاثَ or three warubāʿa وَرُبَاعَ or four yazīdu يَزِيدُ He increases fī فِي in l-khalqi الْخَلْقِ the creation mā مَا what yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ʿalā عَلَى (is) on kulli كُلِّ every shayin شَيْءٍ thing qadīrun قَدِيرٌ All-Powerful

1

1.

[All] praise is [due] to Allah, Creator of the heavens and the earth, [who] made the angels messengers having wings, two or three or four. He increases in creation what He wills. Indeed, Allah is over all things competent.

1

mā مَا What yaftaḥi يَفْتَحِ Allah grants l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah grants lilnnāsi لِلنَّاسِ to mankind min مِنْ of raḥmatin رَحْمَةٍ Mercy falā فَلَا then none mum'sika مُمْسِكَ (can) withhold lahā لَهَا it wamā وَمَا And what yum'sik يُمْسِكْ He withholds falā فَلَا then none mur'sila مُرْسِلَ (can) release lahu لَهُ it min مِنْ thereafter baʿdihi بَعْدِهِ thereafter wahuwa وَهُوَ And He l-ʿazīzu الْعَزِيزُ (is) the All-Mighty l-ḥakīmu الْحَكِيمُ the All-Wise

2

2.

Whatever Allah grants to people of mercy - none can withhold it; and whatever He withholds - none can release it thereafter. And He is the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

2

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O l-nāsu النَّاسُ mankind udh'kurū اذْكُرُوا Remember niʿ'mata نِعْمَتَ (the) Favor l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah ʿalaykum عَلَيْكُمْ upon you hal هَلْ Is min مِنْ (there) any khāliqin خَالِقٍ creator ghayru غَيْرُ other (than) Allah l-lahi اللَّهِ other (than) Allah yarzuqukum يَرْزُقُكُمْ who provides for you mina مِنَ from l-samāi السَّمَاءِ the sky wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth lā لَا (There is) no ilāha إِلَهَ god illā إِلَّا but huwa هُوَ He fa-annā فَأَنَّى Then, how tu'fakūna تُؤْفَكُونَ (are) you deluded

3

3.

O mankind, remember the favor of Allah upon you. Is there any creator other than Allah who provides for you from the heaven and earth? There is no deity except Him, so how are you deluded?

3

wa-in وَإِنْ And if yukadhibūka يُكَذِّبُوكَ they deny you faqad فَقَدْ then certainly kudhibat كُذِّبَتْ were denied rusulun رُسُلٌ Messengers min مِنْ before you qablika قَبْلِكَ before you wa-ilā وَإِلَى And to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah tur'jaʿu تُرْجَعُ return l-umūru الْأُمُورُ the matters

4

4.

And if they deny you, [O Muhammad] - already were messengers denied before you. And to Allah are returned [all] matters.

4

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O l-nāsu النَّاسُ mankind! inna إِنَّ Indeed waʿda وَعْدَ (the) promise l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah ḥaqqun حَقٌّ (is) true falā فَلَا So (let) not taghurrannakumu تَغُرَّنَّكُمُ deceive you l-ḥayatu الْحَيَوةُ the life l-dun'yā الدُّنْيَا (of) the world walā وَلَا and (let) not yaghurrannakum يَغُرَّنَّكُمْ deceive you bil-lahi بِاللَّهِ about Allah l-gharūru الْغَرُورُ the Deceiver

5

5.

O mankind, indeed the promise of Allah is truth, so let not the worldly life delude you and be not deceived about Allah by the Deceiver.

5

inna إِنَّ Indeed l-shayṭāna الشَّيْطَانَ the Shaitaan lakum لَكُمْ (is) to you ʿaduwwun عَدُوٌّ an enemy fa-ittakhidhūhu فَاتَّخِذُوهُ so take him ʿaduwwan عَدُوًّا (as) an enemy innamā إِنَّمَا Only yadʿū يَدْعُوا he invites ḥiz'bahu حِزْبَهُ his party liyakūnū لِيَكُونُوا that they may be min مِنْ among aṣḥābi أَصْحَابِ (the) companions l-saʿīri السَّعِيرِ (of) the Blaze

6

6.

Indeed, Satan is an enemy to you; so take him as an enemy. He only invites his party to be among the companions of the Blaze.

6

alladhīna الَّذِينَ Those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve lahum لَهُمْ for them ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (will be) a punishment shadīdun شَدِيدٌ severe wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ and those āmanū ءَامَنُوا who believe waʿamilū وَعَمِلُوا and do l-ṣāliḥāti الصَّالِحَاتِ righteous deeds lahum لَهُمْ for them maghfiratun مَغْفِرَةٌ (will be) forgiveness wa-ajrun وَأَجْرٌ and a reward kabīrun كَبِيرٌ great

7

7.

Those who disbelieve will have a severe punishment, and those who believe and do righteous deeds will have forgiveness and great reward.

7

afaman أَفَمَنْ Then is (he) who zuyyina زُيِّنَ is made fair-seeming lahu لَهُ to him sūu سُوءُ (the) evil ʿamalihi عَمَلِهِ (of) his deed faraāhu فَرَءَاهُ so that he sees it ḥasanan حَسَنًا (as) good fa-inna فَإِنَّ For indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah yuḍillu يُضِلُّ lets go astray man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills wayahdī وَيَهْدِي and guides man مَنْ whom yashāu يَشَاءُ He wills falā فَلَا So (let) not tadhhab تَذْهَبْ go out nafsuka نَفْسُكَ your soul ʿalayhim عَلَيْهِمْ for them ḥasarātin حَسَرَاتٍ (in) regrets inna إِنَّ Indeed l-laha اللَّهَ Allah ʿalīmun عَلِيمٌ (is) All-Knower bimā بِمَا of what yaṣnaʿūna يَصْنَعُونَ they do

8

8.

Then is one to whom the evil of his deed has been made attractive so he considers it good [like one rightly guided]? For indeed, Allah sends astray whom He wills and guides whom He wills. So do not let yourself perish over them in regret. Indeed, Allah is Knowing of what they do.

8

wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah alladhī الَّذِي (is) the One Who arsala أَرْسَلَ sends l-riyāḥa الرِّيَاحَ the winds fatuthīru فَتُثِيرُ so that they raise saḥāban سَحَابًا (the) clouds fasuq'nāhu فَسُقْنَاهُ and We drive them ilā إِلَى to baladin بَلَدٍ a land mayyitin مَيِّتٍ dead fa-aḥyaynā فَأَحْيَيْنَا and We revive bihi بِهِ therewith l-arḍa الْأَرْضَ the earth baʿda بَعْدَ after mawtihā مَوْتِهَا its death kadhālika كَذَلِكَ Thus l-nushūru النُّشُورُ (will be) the Resurrection

9

9.

And it is Allah who sends the winds, and they stir the clouds, and We drive them to a dead land and give life thereby to the earth after its lifelessness. Thus is the resurrection.

9

man مَنْ Whoever kāna كَانَ [is] desires yurīdu يُرِيدُ [is] desires l-ʿizata الْعِزَّةَ the honor falillahi فَلِلَّهِ then for Allah l-ʿizatu الْعِزَّةُ (is) the Honor jamīʿan جَمِيعًا all ilayhi إِلَيْهِ To Him yaṣʿadu يَصْعَدُ ascends l-kalimu الْكَلِمُ the words l-ṭayibu الطَّيِّبُ good wal-ʿamalu وَالْعَمَلُ and the deed l-ṣāliḥu الصَّالِحُ righteous yarfaʿuhu يَرْفَعُهُ raises it wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ But those who yamkurūna يَمْكُرُونَ plot l-sayiāti السَّيِّئَاتِ the evil lahum لَهُمْ for them ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment shadīdun شَدِيدٌ severe wamakru وَمَكْرُ and (the) plotting ulāika أُولَئِكَ (of) those huwa هُوَ it yabūru يَبُورُ (will) perish

10

10.

Whoever desires honor [through power] - then to Allah belongs all honor. To Him ascends good speech, and righteous work raises it. But they who plot evil deeds will have a severe punishment, and the plotting of those - it will perish.

10

wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ And Allah khalaqakum خَلَقَكُمْ created you min مِنْ from turābin تُرَابٍ dust thumma ثُمَّ then min مِنْ from nuṭ'fatin نُطْفَةٍ a sperm-drop thumma ثُمَّ then jaʿalakum جَعَلَكُمْ He made you azwājan أَزْوَاجًا mates wamā وَمَا And not taḥmilu تَحْمِلُ conceives min مِنْ any unthā أُنْثَى female walā وَلَا and not taḍaʿu تَضَعُ gives birth illā إِلَّا except biʿil'mihi بِعِلْمِهِ with His knowledge wamā وَمَا And not yuʿammaru يُعَمَّرُ is granted life min مِنْ any muʿammarin مُعَمَّرٍ aged person walā وَلَا and not yunqaṣu يُنْقَصُ is lessened min مِنْ from ʿumurihi عُمُرِهِ his life illā إِلَّا but fī فِي (is) in kitābin كِتَابٍ a Register inna إِنَّ Indeed dhālika ذَلِكَ that ʿalā عَلَى for l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah yasīrun يَسِيرٌ (is) easy

11

11.

And Allah created you from dust, then from a sperm-drop; then He made you mates. And no female conceives nor does she give birth except with His knowledge. And no aged person is granted [additional] life nor is his lifespan lessened but that it is in a register. Indeed, that for Allah is easy.

11

wamā وَمَا And not yastawī يَسْتَوِي are alike l-baḥrāni الْبَحْرَانِ the two seas hādhā هَذَا This ʿadhbun عَذْبٌ (is) fresh furātun فُرَاتٌ sweet sāighun سَائِغٌ pleasant sharābuhu شَرَابُهُ its drink wahādhā وَهَذَا and this mil'ḥun مِلْحٌ salty ujājun أُجَاجٌ (and) bitter wamin وَمِنْ And from kullin كُلٍّ each takulūna تَأْكُلُونَ you eat laḥman لَحْمًا meat ṭariyyan طَرِيًّا fresh watastakhrijūna وَتَسْتَخْرِجُونَ and you extract ḥil'yatan حِلْيَةً ornaments talbasūnahā تَلْبَسُونَهَا you wear them watarā وَتَرَى and you see l-ful'ka الْفُلْكَ the ships fīhi فِيهِ in it mawākhira مَوَاخِرَ cleaving litabtaghū لِتَبْتَغُوا so that you may seek min مِنْ of faḍlihi فَضْلِهِ His Bounty walaʿallakum وَلَعَلَّكُمْ and that you may tashkurūna تَشْكُرُونَ be grateful

12

12.

And not alike are the two bodies of water. One is fresh and sweet, palatable for drinking, and one is salty and bitter. And from each you eat tender meat and extract ornaments which you wear, and you see the ships plowing through [them] that you might seek of His bounty; and perhaps you will be grateful.

12

yūliju يُولِجُ He causes to enter al-layla اللَّيْلَ the night fī فِي in (to) l-nahāri النَّهَارِ the day wayūliju وَيُولِجُ and He causes to enter l-nahāra النَّهَارَ the day fī فِي in (to) al-layli اللَّيْلِ the night wasakhara وَسَخَّرَ and He has subjected l-shamsa الشَّمْسَ the sun wal-qamara وَالْقَمَرَ and the moon kullun كُلٌّ each yajrī يَجْرِي running li-ajalin لِأَجَلٍ for a term musamman مُسَمًّى appointed dhālikumu ذَلِكُمُ That (is) l-lahu اللَّهُ Allah rabbukum رَبُّكُمْ your Lord lahu لَهُ for Him l-mul'ku الْمُلْكُ (is) the Dominion wa-alladhīna وَالَّذِينَ And those whom tadʿūna تَدْعُونَ you invoke min مِنْ besides Him dūnihi دُونِهِ besides Him mā مَا not yamlikūna يَمْلِكُونَ they possess min مِنْ even qiṭ'mīrin قِطْمِيرٍ (as much as) the membrane of a date-seed

13

13.

He causes the night to enter the day, and He causes the day to enter the night and has subjected the sun and the moon - each running [its course] for a specified term. That is Allah, your Lord; to Him belongs sovereignty. And those whom you invoke other than Him do not possess [as much as] the membrane of a date seed.

13

in إِنْ If tadʿūhum تَدْعُوهُمْ you invoke them lā لَا not yasmaʿū يَسْمَعُوا they hear duʿāakum دُعَاءَكُمْ your call walaw وَلَوْ and if samiʿū سَمِعُوا they heard mā مَا not is'tajābū اسْتَجَابُوا they (would) respond lakum لَكُمْ to you wayawma وَيَوْمَ And (on the) Day l-qiyāmati الْقِيَامَةِ (of) the Resurrection yakfurūna يَكْفُرُونَ they will deny bishir'kikum بِشِرْكِكُمْ your association walā وَلَا And none yunabbi-uka يُنَبِّئُكَ can inform you mith'lu مِثْلُ like khabīrin خَبِيرٍ (the) All-Aware

14

14.

If you invoke them, they do not hear your supplication; and if they heard, they would not respond to you. And on the Day of Resurrection they will deny your association. And none can inform you like [one] Acquainted [with all matters].

14

yāayyuhā يَاأَيُّهَا O l-nāsu النَّاسُ mankind! antumu أَنْتُمُ You l-fuqarāu الْفُقَرَاءُ (are) those in need ilā إِلَى of l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah wal-lahu وَاللَّهُ while Allah huwa هُوَ He l-ghaniyu الْغَنِيُّ (is) Free of need l-ḥamīdu الْحَمِيدُ the Praiseworthy

15

15.

O mankind, you are those in need of Allah, while Allah is the Free of need, the Praiseworthy.

15

in إِنْ If yasha يَشَأْ He wills yudh'hib'kum يُذْهِبْكُمْ He (can) do away with you wayati وَيَأْتِ and bring bikhalqin بِخَلْقٍ in a creation jadīdin جَدِيدٍ new

16

16.

If He wills, He can do away with you and bring forth a new creation.

16

wamā وَمَا And not dhālika ذَلِكَ that ʿalā عَلَى (is) on l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah biʿazīzin بِعَزِيزٍ difficult

17

17.

And that is for Allah not difficult.

17

walā وَلَا And not taziru تَزِرُ will bear wāziratun وَازِرَةٌ bearer of burdens wiz'ra وِزْرَ burden ukh'rā أُخْرَى (of) another wa-in وَإِنْ And if tadʿu تَدْعُ calls muth'qalatun مُثْقَلَةٌ a heavily laden ilā إِلَى to ḥim'lihā حِمْلِهَا (carry) its load lā لَا not yuḥ'mal يُحْمَلْ will be carried min'hu مِنْهُ of it shayon شَيْءٌ anything walaw وَلَوْ even if kāna كَانَ he be dhā ذَا near of kin qur'bā قُرْبَى near of kin innamā إِنَّمَا Only tundhiru تُنْذِرُ you can warn alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who yakhshawna يَخْشَوْنَ fear rabbahum رَبَّهُمْ their Lord bil-ghaybi بِالْغَيْبِ unseen wa-aqāmū وَأَقَامُوا and establish l-ṣalata الصَّلَوةَ the prayer waman وَمَنْ And whoever tazakkā تَزَكَّى purifies himself fa-innamā فَإِنَّمَا then only yatazakkā يَتَزَكَّى he purifies linafsihi لِنَفْسِهِ for his own self wa-ilā وَإِلَى And to l-lahi اللَّهِ Allah l-maṣīru الْمَصِيرُ (is) the destination
