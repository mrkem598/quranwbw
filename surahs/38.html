sad صٓ Saad wal-qur'āni وَالْقُرْءَانِ By the Quran dhī ذِي full (of) reminder l-dhik'ri الذِّكْرِ full (of) reminder

1

1.

Sad. By the Qur'an containing reminder...

1

bali بَلِ Nay alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve fī فِي (are) in ʿizzatin عِزَّةٍ self-glory washiqāqin وَشِقَاقٍ and opposition

2

2.

But those who disbelieve are in pride and dissension.

2

kam كَمْ How many ahlaknā أَهْلَكْنَا We destroyed min مِنْ before them qablihim قَبْلِهِمْ before them min مِنْ of qarnin قَرْنٍ a generation fanādaw فَنَادَوْا then they called out walāta وَلَاتَ when there (was) no longer ḥīna حِينَ time manāṣin مَنَاصٍ (for) escape

3

3.

How many a generation have We destroyed before them, and they [then] called out; but it was not a time for escape.

3

waʿajibū وَعَجِبُوا And they wonder an أَنْ that jāahum جَاءَهُمْ has come to them mundhirun مُنْذِرٌ a warner min'hum مِنْهُمْ from among themselves waqāla وَقَالَ And said l-kāfirūna الْكَافِرُونَ the disbelievers hādhā هَذَا This sāḥirun سَاحِرٌ (is) a magician kadhābun كَذَّابٌ a liar

4

4.

And they wonder that there has come to them a warner from among themselves. And the disbelievers say, "This is a magician and a liar.

4

ajaʿala أَجَعَلَ Has he made l-ālihata الْءَالِهَةَ the gods ilāhan إِلَهًا (into) one god wāḥidan وَاحِدًا (into) one god inna إِنَّ Indeed hādhā هَذَا this lashayon لَشَيْءٌ (is) certainly a thing ʿujābun عُجَابٌ curious

5

5.

Has he made the gods [only] one God? Indeed, this is a curious thing."

5

wa-inṭalaqa وَانْطَلَقَ And went forth l-mala-u الْمَلَأُ the chiefs min'hum مِنْهُمْ among them ani أَنِ that im'shū امْشُوا Continue wa-iṣ'birū وَاصْبِرُوا and be patient ʿalā عَلَى over ālihatikum ءَالِهَتِكُمْ your gods inna إِنَّ Indeed hādhā هَذَا this lashayon لَشَيْءٌ (is) certainly a thing yurādu يُرَادُ intended

6

6.

And the eminent among them went forth, [saying], "Continue, and be patient over [the defense of] your gods. Indeed, this is a thing intended.

6

mā مَا Not samiʿ'nā سَمِعْنَا we heard bihādhā بِهَذَا of this fī فِي in l-milati الْمِلَّةِ the religion l-ākhirati الْءَاخِرَةِ the last in إِنْ Not hādhā هَذَا (is) this illā إِلَّا but ikh'tilāqun اخْتِلَاقٌ a fabrication

7

7.

We have not heard of this in the latest religion. This is not but a fabrication.

7

a-unzila أَءُنزِلَ Has been revealed ʿalayhi عَلَيْهِ to him l-dhik'ru الذِّكْرُ the Message min مِنْ from bayninā بَيْنِنَا among us bal بَلْ Nay hum هُمْ They fī فِي (are) in shakkin شَكٍّ doubt min مِنْ about dhik'rī ذِكْرِي My Message bal بَلْ Nay lammā لَمَّا not yadhūqū يَذُوقُوا they have tasted ʿadhābi عَذَابِ My punishment

8

8.

Has the message been revealed to him out of [all of] us?" Rather, they are in doubt about My message. Rather, they have not yet tasted My punishment.

8

am أَمْ Or ʿindahum عِنْدَهُمْ have they khazāinu خَزَائِنُ (the) treasures raḥmati رَحْمَةِ (of the) Mercy rabbika رَبِّكَ (of) your Lord l-ʿazīzi الْعَزِيزِ the All-Mighty l-wahābi الْوَهَّابِ the Bestower

9

9.

Or do they have the depositories of the mercy of your Lord, the Exalted in Might, the Bestower?

9

am أَمْ Or lahum لَهُمْ for them mul'ku مُلْكُ (is the) dominion l-samāwāti السَّمَاوَاتِ (of) the heavens wal-arḍi وَالْأَرْضِ and the earth wamā وَمَا and whatever baynahumā بَيْنَهُمَا (is) between them falyartaqū فَلْيَرْتَقُوا Then let them ascend fī فِي by l-asbābi الْأَسْبَابِ the means

10

10.

Or is theirs the dominion of the heavens and the earth and what is between them? Then let them ascend through [any] ways of access.

10

jundun جُنْدٌ Soldiers mā مَا there hunālika هُنَالِكَ there mahzūmun مَهْزُومٌ (they will be) defeated mina مِنَ among l-aḥzābi الْأَحْزَابِ the companies

11

11.

[They are but] soldiers [who will be] defeated there among the companies [of disbelievers].

11

kadhabat كَذَّبَتْ Denied qablahum قَبْلَهُمْ before them qawmu قَوْمُ (the) people nūḥin نُوحٍ (of) Nuh waʿādun وَعَادٌ and Aad wafir'ʿawnu وَفِرْعَوْنُ and Firaun dhū ذُو (the) owner l-awtādi الْأَوْتَادِ (of) the stakes

12

12.

The people of Noah denied before them, and [the tribe of] 'Aad and Pharaoh, the owner of stakes,

12

wathamūdu وَثَمُودُ And Thamud waqawmu وَقَوْمُ and (the) people lūṭin لُوطٍ (of) Lut wa-aṣḥābu وَأَصْحَابُ and (the) companions al'aykati لْءَيْكَةِ (of) the wood ulāika أُولَئِكَ Those l-aḥzābu الْأَحْزَابُ (were) the companies

13

13.

And [the tribe of] Thamud and the people of Lot and the companions of the thicket. Those are the companies.

13

in إِنْ Not kullun كُلٌّ all (of them) illā إِلَّا but kadhaba كَذَّبَ denied l-rusula الرُّسُلَ the Messengers faḥaqqa فَحَقَّ so was just ʿiqābi عِقَابِ My penalty

14

14.

Each of them denied the messengers, so My penalty was justified.

14

wamā وَمَا And not yanẓuru يَنْظُرُ await hāulāi هَؤُلَاءِ these illā إِلَّا but ṣayḥatan صَيْحَةً a shout wāḥidatan وَاحِدَةً one mā مَا not lahā لَهَا for it min مِنْ any fawāqin فَوَاقٍ delay

15

15.

And these [disbelievers] await not but one blast [of the Horn]; for it there will be no delay.

15

waqālū وَقَالُوا And they say rabbanā رَبَّنَا Our Lord! ʿajjil عَجِّلْ Hasten lanā لَنَا for us qiṭṭanā قِطَّنَا our share qabla قَبْلَ before yawmi يَوْمِ (the) Day l-ḥisābi الْحِسَابِ (of) the Account

16

16.

And they say, "Our Lord, hasten for us our share [of the punishment] before the Day of Account"

16

iṣ'bir اصْبِرْ Be patient ʿalā عَلَى over mā مَا what yaqūlūna يَقُولُونَ they say wa-udh'kur وَاذْكُرْ and remember ʿabdanā عَبْدَنَا Our slave dāwūda دَاوُدَ Dawood dhā ذَا the possessor of strength l-aydi الْأَيْدِ the possessor of strength innahu إِنَّهُ Indeed, he (was) awwābun أَوَّابٌ repeatedly turning

17

17.

Be patient over what they say and remember Our servant, David, the possessor of strength; indeed, he was one who repeatedly turned back [to Allah].

17

innā إِنَّا Indeed We sakharnā سَخَّرْنَا subjected l-jibāla الْجِبَالَ the mountains maʿahu مَعَهُ with him yusabbiḥ'na يُسَبِّحْنَ glorifying bil-ʿashiyi بِالْعَشِيِّ in the evening wal-ish'rāqi وَالْإِشْرَاقِ and [the] sunrise

18

18.

Indeed, We subjected the mountains [to praise] with him, exalting [Allah] in the [late] afternoon and [after] sunrise.

18

wal-ṭayra وَالطَّيْرَ And the birds maḥshūratan مَحْشُورَةً assembled kullun كُلٌّ all lahu لَهُ with him awwābun أَوَّابٌ repeatedly turning

19

19.

And the birds were assembled, all with him repeating [praises].

19

washadadnā وَشَدَدْنَا And We strengthened mul'kahu مُلْكَهُ his kingdom waātaynāhu وَءَاتَيْنَاهُ and We gave him l-ḥik'mata الْحِكْمَةَ [the] wisdom wafaṣla وَفَصْلَ and decisive l-khiṭābi الْخِطَابِ speech

20

20.

And We strengthened his kingdom and gave him wisdom and discernment in speech.

20

wahal وَهَلْ And has (there) atāka أَتَىكَ come to you naba-u نَبَؤُا (the) news l-khaṣmi الْخَصْمِ (of) the litigants idh إِذْ when tasawwarū تَسَوَّرُوا they climbed over the wall l-miḥ'rāba الْمِحْرَابَ (of) the chamber

21

21.

And has there come to you the news of the adversaries, when they climbed over the wall of [his] prayer chamber -

21

idh إِذْ When dakhalū دَخَلُوا they entered ʿalā عَلَى upon dāwūda دَاوُدَ Dawood fafaziʿa فَفَزِعَ and he was afraid min'hum مِنْهُمْ of them qālū قَالُوا they said lā لَا (Do) not takhaf تَخَفْ fear khaṣmāni خَصْمَانِ (We are) two litigants baghā بَغَى has wronged baʿḍunā بَعْضُنَا one of us ʿalā عَلَى to baʿḍin بَعْضٍ another fa-uḥ'kum فَاحْكُمْ so judge baynanā بَيْنَنَا between us bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in truth walā وَلَا and (do) not tush'ṭiṭ تُشْطِطْ be unjust wa-ih'dinā وَاهْدِنَا and guide us ilā إِلَى to sawāi سَوَاءِ an even l-ṣirāṭi الصِّرَاطِ [the] path

22

22.

When they entered upon David and he was alarmed by them? They said, "Fear not. [We are] two adversaries, one of whom has wronged the other, so judge between us with truth and do not exceed [it] and guide us to the sound path.

22

inna إِنَّ Indeed hādhā هَذَا this akhī أَخِي (is) my brother lahu لَهُ he has tis'ʿun تِسْعٌ ninety-nine watis'ʿūna وَتِسْعُونَ ninety-nine naʿjatan نَعْجَةً ewe(s) waliya وَلِيَ while I have naʿjatun نَعْجَةٌ ewe wāḥidatun وَاحِدَةٌ one faqāla فَقَالَ so he said akfil'nīhā أَكْفِلْنِيهَا Entrust her to me waʿazzanī وَعَزَّنِي and he overpowered me fī فِي in l-khiṭābi الْخِطَابِ [the] speech

23

23.

Indeed this, my brother, has ninety-nine ewes, and I have one ewe; so he said, 'Entrust her to me,' and he overpowered me in speech."

23

qāla قَالَ He said laqad لَقَدْ Certainly ẓalamaka ظَلَمَكَ he has wronged you bisuāli بِسُؤَالِ by demanding naʿjatika نَعْجَتِكَ your ewe ilā إِلَى to niʿājihi نِعَاجِهِ his ewes wa-inna وَإِنَّ And indeed kathīran كَثِيرًا many mina مِنَ of l-khulaṭāi الْخُلَطَاءِ the partners layabghī لَيَبْغِي certainly oppress baʿḍuhum بَعْضُهُمْ one ʿalā عَلَى [on] baʿḍin بَعْضٍ another illā إِلَّا except alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe waʿamilū وَعَمِلُوا and do l-ṣāliḥāti الصَّالِحَاتِ righteous deeds waqalīlun وَقَلِيلٌ and few mā مَا (are) they hum هُمْ (are) they waẓanna وَظَنَّ And became certain dāwūdu دَاوُدُ Dawood annamā أَنَّمَا that fatannāhu فَتَنَّاهُ We (had) tried him fa-is'taghfara فَاسْتَغْفَرَ and he asked forgiveness rabbahu رَبَّهُ (of) his Lord wakharra وَخَرَّ and fell down rākiʿan رَاكِعًا bowing wa-anāba وَأَنَابَ and turned in repentance

24

24.

[David] said, "He has certainly wronged you in demanding your ewe [in addition] to his ewes. And indeed, many associates oppress one another, except for those who believe and do righteous deeds - and few are they." And David became certain that We had tried him, and he asked forgiveness of his Lord and fell down bowing [in prostration] and turned in repentance [to Allah].

24

faghafarnā فَغَفَرْنَا So We forgave lahu لَهُ for him dhālika ذَلِكَ that wa-inna وَإِنَّ And indeed lahu لَهُ for him ʿindanā عِنْدَنَا with Us lazul'fā لَزُلْفَى surely is a near access waḥus'na وَحُسْنَ and a good maābin مَءَابٍ place of return

25

25.

So We forgave him that; and indeed, for him is nearness to Us and a good place of return.

25

yādāwūdu يَادَاوُدُ O Dawood! innā إِنَّا Indeed We jaʿalnāka جَعَلْنَاكَ [We] have made you khalīfatan خَلِيفَةً a vicegerent fī فِي in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth fa-uḥ'kum فَاحْكُمْ so judge bayna بَيْنَ between l-nāsi النَّاسِ [the] men bil-ḥaqi بِالْحَقِّ in truth walā وَلَا and (do) not tattabiʿi تَتَّبِعِ follow l-hawā الْهَوَى the desire fayuḍillaka فَيُضِلَّكَ for it will lead you stray ʿan عَنْ from sabīli سَبِيلِ (the) way l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah inna إِنَّ Indeed alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who yaḍillūna يَضِلُّونَ go astray ʿan عَنْ from sabīli سَبِيلِ (the) way l-lahi اللَّهِ (of) Allah lahum لَهُمْ for them ʿadhābun عَذَابٌ (is) a punishment shadīdun شَدِيدٌ severe bimā بِمَا because nasū نَسُوا they forgot yawma يَوْمَ (the) Day l-ḥisābi الْحِسَابِ (of) Account

26

26.

[We said], "O David, indeed We have made you a successor upon the earth, so judge between the people in truth and do not follow [your own] desire, as it will lead you astray from the way of Allah." Indeed, those who go astray from the way of Allah will have a severe punishment for having forgotten the Day of Account.

26

wamā وَمَا And not khalaqnā خَلَقْنَا We created l-samāa السَّمَاءَ the heaven wal-arḍa وَالْأَرْضَ and the earth wamā وَمَا and whatever baynahumā بَيْنَهُمَا (is) between them bāṭilan بَاطِلًا without purpose dhālika ذَلِكَ That ẓannu ظَنُّ (is the) assumption alladhīna الَّذِينَ (of) those who kafarū كَفَرُوا disbelieve fawaylun فَوَيْلٌ So woe lilladhīna لِلَّذِينَ to those kafarū كَفَرُوا who disbelieve mina مِنَ from l-nāri النَّارِ the Fire

27

27.

And We did not create the heaven and the earth and that between them aimlessly. That is the assumption of those who disbelieve, so woe to those who disbelieve from the Fire.

27

am أَمْ Or najʿalu نَجْعَلُ should We treat alladhīna الَّذِينَ those who āmanū ءَامَنُوا believe waʿamilū وَعَمِلُوا and do l-ṣāliḥāti الصَّالِحَاتِ righteous deeds kal-muf'sidīna كَالْمُفْسِدِينَ like those who spread corruption fī فِي in l-arḍi الْأَرْضِ the earth am أَمْ Or najʿalu نَجْعَلُ should We treat l-mutaqīna الْمُتَّقِينَ the pious kal-fujāri كَالْفُجَّارِ like the wicked

28

28.
